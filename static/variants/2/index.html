Strategic Priorities

Three Pillars of Progress

01

Economic Growth

Fostering an environment where American businesses thrive, creating jobs and opportunities for families across the nation. Record business applications signal renewed confidence in our economic future.

02

Protecting America's financial system through robust sanctions enforcement and disrupting networks that threaten our security. Treasury stands as the first line of defense against financial threats.
