## No FEAR Act

The Notification and Federal Employee Antidiscrimination and Retaliation Act of 2002 (No FEAR Act) requires federal agencies to post certain data related to Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) complaints.

### Purpose

The No FEAR Act is designed to:
- Reduce discrimination and retaliation in the federal workplace
- Hold agencies accountable for violations
- Provide greater transparency in EEO complaint data

### Required Data

Treasury posts the following No FEAR Act data:

#### Complaint Activity
- Number of complaints filed
- Number of complainants
- Repeat filers

#### Complaint Processing
- Complaints pending at the beginning/end of the period
- Investigations completed
- Hearings requested/held
- Final agency decisions

#### Findings of Discrimination
- Number of findings by basis and issue
- Pending complaints by basis
- Average processing days

#### Comparative Data
- Annual data comparison
- Trends over time

### EEO Program

Treasury is committed to:
- Providing a workplace free from discrimination
- Ensuring equal opportunity for all employees
- Promptly addressing EEO complaints
- Implementing corrective actions

### Contact

For questions about EEO matters, contact the Treasury Office of Civil Rights and Diversity.
