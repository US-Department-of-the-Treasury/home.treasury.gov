## Frequently Asked Questions

### About Treasury

**What does the Treasury Department do?**
The U.S. Department of the Treasury is the executive agency responsible for promoting economic prosperity and ensuring the financial security of the United States.

**Who is the Secretary of the Treasury?**
Visit our [Officials](/about/general-information/officials/) page for current leadership information.

### Taxes

**How do I get help with my taxes?**
Contact the Internal Revenue Service (IRS):
- Website: [www.irs.gov](https://www.irs.gov/)
- Phone: 1-800-829-1040
- [IRS Local Offices](https://www.irs.gov/help/contact-your-local-irs-office)

**Where is my refund?**
Check your refund status at [www.irs.gov/refunds](https://www.irs.gov/refunds)

### Savings Bonds

**How do I buy savings bonds?**
Purchase savings bonds at [TreasuryDirect.gov](https://www.treasurydirect.gov/)

**How do I cash savings bonds?**
You can cash paper savings bonds at most financial institutions. Electronic bonds are redeemed through TreasuryDirect.

**How do I find lost savings bonds?**
Use [Treasury Hunt](https://treasuryhunt.gov/) to search for matured, uncashed bonds.

### Currency

**How do I exchange damaged currency?**
The Bureau of Engraving and Printing processes claims for damaged currency. Visit [www.bep.gov](https://www.bep.gov/services/mutilated-currency-redemption)

**Where can I buy collectible coins?**
Purchase coins from the [U.S. Mint](https://www.usmint.gov/)

### Payments

**When will I receive my payment?**
For Social Security, VA benefits, or other federal payments, check with the issuing agency or use [GoDirect](https://www.godirect.gov/)

### Fraud

**How do I report fraud?**
Visit our [Report Fraud, Waste, and Abuse](/services/report-fraud-waste-and-abuse/) page.

**How do I report IRS scams?**
Report IRS impersonation scams to [TIGTA](https://www.treasury.gov/tigta)

### Careers

**How do I find jobs at Treasury?**
Search for Treasury jobs at [USAJOBS.gov](https://www.usajobs.gov/)

Learn more at [Careers at Treasury](/about/careers-at-treasury/)
