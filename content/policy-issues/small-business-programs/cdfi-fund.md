## About the CDFI Fund

The Community Development Financial Institutions (CDFI) Fund promotes economic revitalization and community development through investment in and assistance to CDFIs.

## Mission

Expand economic opportunity for underserved people and communities by supporting the growth and capacity of a national network of CDFIs.

## Programs

### CDFI Program
Provides financial and technical assistance to CDFIs serving low-income communities.

### New Markets Tax Credit
Provides tax incentives for investment in low-income communities.

### Bank Enterprise Award Program
Recognizes banks for investments in CDFIs and distressed communities.

### Native American CDFI Assistance Program
Supports CDFIs serving Native American, Alaska Native, and Native Hawaiian communities.

## Impact

CDFIs provide affordable financial products and services to:
- Low-income individuals and families
- Minority-owned businesses
- Rural communities
- Other underserved markets
