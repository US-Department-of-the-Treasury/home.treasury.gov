Treasury supports small businesses and entrepreneurs through various programs designed to improve access to capital and promote economic growth in underserved communities.

## Programs and Initiatives

### Community Development Financial Institutions (CDFI) Fund
The [CDFI Fund](/policy-issues/small-business-programs/cdfi-fund) promotes economic revitalization by investing in and providing assistance to CDFIs that serve low-income communities.

### State Small Business Credit Initiative (SSBCI)
Provides funding to states, territories, and tribal governments to support small business financing programs, including venture capital, loan participation, and collateral support.

### Emergency Capital Investment Program (ECIP)
Supports community development financial institutions and minority depository institutions in providing loans, grants, and forbearance for small businesses.

## Impact

Treasury's small business programs help:

- Expand access to capital for underserved entrepreneurs
- Create and preserve jobs in low-income communities
- Promote economic development in rural and urban areas
- Support minority-owned and women-owned businesses
