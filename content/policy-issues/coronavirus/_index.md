The Treasury Department has played a critical role in implementing economic relief programs to help Americans, businesses, and state and local governments during the COVID-19 pandemic.

## Assistance Programs

### [American Families and Workers](/policy-issues/coronavirus/assistance-for-American-families-and-workers)
Economic Impact Payments, tax credits, and other assistance for individuals and families.

### [Small Businesses](/policy-issues/coronavirus/assistance-for-small-businesses)
Paycheck Protection Program (PPP), Emergency Capital Investment Program, and other small business relief.

### [State, Local, and Tribal Governments](/policy-issues/coronavirus/assistance-for-state-local-and-tribal-governments)
State and Local Fiscal Recovery Funds, Coronavirus Relief Fund, and other government assistance programs.

### [American Industry](/policy-issues/coronavirus/assistance-for-american-industry)
Programs to support critical industries during the pandemic.
