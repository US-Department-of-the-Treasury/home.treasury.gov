The Office of Tribal and Native Affairs coordinates Treasury's engagement with tribal governments and Native communities.

## Mission

Enhance Treasury's government-to-government relationships with federally recognized Tribal governments and ensure that Native communities benefit from Treasury programs.

## Key Initiatives

### Tribal Consultation
Treasury conducts formal tribal consultation on policies affecting Indian Country.

### Program Access
Working to improve tribal access to Treasury programs including:
- CDFI Fund programs
- Economic relief programs
- Tax policy initiatives

### Technical Assistance
Providing support to tribal governments on financial management and economic development.

## Tribal Advisory Committee

The Treasury Tribal Advisory Committee provides recommendations on Treasury policies affecting tribal communities.
