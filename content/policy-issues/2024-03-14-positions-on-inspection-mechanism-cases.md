The inspection mechanisms of the MDIs are vital to ensuring the confidence and integrity of the taxpayer-supported funds that flow through this institutions. The United States treats these proceedings with utmost seriousness. Provided below are the explanations of the United States’ positions and the statements provided to the United States Executive Director for submission into the record regarding matters that have been discussed since January 2004.
