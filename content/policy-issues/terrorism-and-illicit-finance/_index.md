The Office of Terrorism and Financial Intelligence (TFI) leads Treasury's efforts to combat illicit finance, including money laundering, terrorist financing, and sanctions evasion.

## Key Components

### Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC)
Administers and enforces economic [sanctions programs](/policy-issues/terrorism-and-illicit-finance/sanctions) targeting threats to national security and foreign policy.

### Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN)
Safeguards the financial system from illicit use, combats money laundering, and promotes national security through financial intelligence.

### Office of Intelligence and Analysis (OIA)
Treasury's intelligence office, responsible for producing intelligence analysis in support of Treasury leadership and policy.

### Office of Terrorist Financing and Financial Crimes (TFFC)
Develops policies and strategies to combat terrorist financing, money laundering, and other financial crimes.

## Priority Threats

- Terrorism and terrorist financing
- Proliferation of weapons of mass destruction
- Narcotics trafficking
- Transnational organized crime
- Corruption and kleptocracy
- Cyber-enabled financial crime
