OFAC administers and enforces economic sanctions programs based on U.S. foreign policy and national security goals.

## Current Sanctions Programs

### Country-Based Programs
- Russia/Ukraine
- North Korea
- Iran
- Syria
- Cuba
- Venezuela

### List-Based Programs
- Specially Designated Nationals (SDN) List
- Sectoral Sanctions Identifications (SSI) List
- Foreign Sanctions Evaders (FSE) List

## Compliance

### For Businesses
- Screen transactions against OFAC lists
- Maintain compliance programs
- Report blocked transactions

### For Individuals
- U.S. persons must comply with all sanctions
- Prohibited transactions may result in civil or criminal penalties
