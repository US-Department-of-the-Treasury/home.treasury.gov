The Office of Domestic Finance advises and assists in the formulation and execution of policies to address financial markets, financial institutions, and fiscal affairs.

## Key Areas

### [Cash and Debt Forecasting](/policy-issues/financial-markets-financial-institutions-and-fiscal-service/cash-and-debt-forecasting)
Treasury cash forecasting ensures adequate funds are available for the federal government's operations.

### [Debt Limit](/policy-issues/financial-markets-financial-institutions-and-fiscal-service/debt-limit)
Information about the statutory debt limit and Treasury's extraordinary measures.

### [Financial Stability Oversight Council (FSOC)](/policy-issues/financial-markets-financial-institutions-and-fiscal-service/fsoc)
The Council is charged with identifying risks to U.S. financial stability and responding to emerging threats.

### [Federal Insurance Office](/policy-issues/financial-markets-financial-institutions-and-fiscal-service/federal-insurance-office)
Monitors all aspects of the insurance industry and represents the United States on prudential aspects of international insurance matters.

### [RESTORE Act](/policy-issues/financial-markets-financial-institutions-and-fiscal-service/restore-act)
The Resources and Ecosystems Sustainability, Tourist Opportunities, and Revived Economies of the Gulf Coast States Act.

### [1603 Program](/policy-issues/financial-markets-financial-institutions-and-fiscal-service/1603-program-payments-for-specified-energy-property-in-lieu-of-tax-credits)
Payments for Specified Energy Property in Lieu of Tax Credits.

### [Pay for Results (SIPPRA)](/policy-issues/financial-markets-financial-institutions-and-fiscal-service/pay-for-results-sippra)
Social Impact Partnership to Pay for Results Act programs.
