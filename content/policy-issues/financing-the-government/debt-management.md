Treasury's Office of Debt Management is responsible for managing the federal government's borrowing needs at the lowest cost over time.

## Treasury Securities

Treasury issues various securities to finance government operations:

### Marketable Securities
- Treasury Bills (4-52 weeks)
- Treasury Notes (2-10 years)
- Treasury Bonds (20-30 years)
- TIPS (5, 10, 30 years)
- Floating Rate Notes (2 years)

### Non-Marketable Securities
- Savings Bonds
- State and Local Government Series (SLGS)

## Auction Process

Treasury securities are sold through regular auctions:
- Bills: Weekly
- Notes and Bonds: Monthly
- Auction results published on TreasuryDirect

## Borrowing Advisory Committee

The Treasury Borrowing Advisory Committee (TBAC) provides recommendations on debt management.
