Treasury works to protect consumers in the financial marketplace and promote financial literacy and economic inclusion.

## Key Focus Areas

### Financial Literacy
Treasury supports programs that help Americans understand personal finance, including budgeting, saving, investing, and managing debt responsibly.

### Consumer Protection
Working with regulatory partners to ensure fair, transparent, and competitive financial markets that protect consumers from fraud and abusive practices.

### Access to Banking
Promoting initiatives that expand access to mainstream financial services for underserved communities, including the unbanked and underbanked populations.

### Affordable Housing
Developing policies that support sustainable homeownership and access to affordable rental housing.
