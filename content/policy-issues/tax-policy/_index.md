The Office of Tax Policy develops and implements tax policies that promote economic growth, ensure fairness, and simplify tax administration.

## Key Functions

### Legislative Development
Advising the Secretary on domestic and international tax policy matters and developing legislative proposals for Congress.

### Regulatory Guidance
Drafting regulations and guidance to implement tax laws, working closely with the Internal Revenue Service.

### Revenue Analysis
Providing revenue estimates and economic analysis of tax proposals through the Office of Tax Analysis.

### International Coordination
Negotiating tax treaties and working with international partners on global tax policy issues.
