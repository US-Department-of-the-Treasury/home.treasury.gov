The Office of Tax Policy advises the Secretary on domestic and international tax policy, develops tax legislative proposals, and provides economic analysis of tax issues.

## Key Areas

### Individual Taxation
- Income tax policy
- Tax credits and deductions
- Retirement savings incentives

### Business Taxation
- Corporate tax policy
- Pass-through business taxation
- International tax rules

### Tax Administration
- IRS guidance and regulations
- Compliance and enforcement policy
- Taxpayer services

## Tax Analysis

The Office of Tax Analysis provides:
- Revenue estimates
- Economic analysis of tax proposals
- Distribution analysis

## International Tax Policy

Treasury works with international partners on:
- Tax treaty negotiations
- Transfer pricing rules
- Base erosion prevention
