## Climate and Finance

Treasury addresses the intersection of climate change and financial policy, working to understand and mitigate climate-related financial risks.

## Key Areas

### Climate-Related Financial Risk
- Assessing risks to the financial system from climate change
- Developing frameworks for climate risk disclosure
- Supporting international coordination on climate finance

### Clean Energy Investment
- Tax incentives for clean energy
- Green bond development
- Climate finance initiatives

### International Engagement
- Multilateral development bank climate financing
- International climate finance commitments
- G7/G20 climate discussions
