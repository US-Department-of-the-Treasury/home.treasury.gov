Treasury's Office of International Affairs works to protect U.S. economic prosperity and national security by promoting global economic growth, stability, and development.

## Key Policy Areas

### International Financial Stability
Monitoring global economic conditions and coordinating with international partners to promote financial stability and prevent economic crises.

### Multilateral Engagement
Representing the United States at international financial institutions and forums:
- International Monetary Fund (IMF)
- World Bank Group
- G-7 and G-20 Finance Ministers
- Financial Stability Board
- Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development (OECD)

### Trade and Investment
Supporting trade policy development and enforcement, including addressing currency issues and promoting fair trade practices.

### Technical Assistance
Providing expertise to help developing countries build capacity in public financial management, debt management, and financial sector development.

### Development Finance
Overseeing U.S. participation in multilateral development banks and promoting effective development outcomes.
