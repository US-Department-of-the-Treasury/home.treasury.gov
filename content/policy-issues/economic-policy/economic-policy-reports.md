## Economic Policy Reports

The U.S. Department of the Treasury's Office of Economic Policy produces research and analysis on a wide range of economic issues affecting the American economy.

### Types of Reports

#### Economic Analysis
- Macroeconomic trends and forecasting
- Labor market analysis
- Financial sector assessments
- Trade and international economics

#### Policy Research
- Tax policy impact analysis
- Fiscal policy studies
- Regulatory analysis
- Program evaluations

#### Special Reports
- Congressional mandated reports
- Interagency studies
- Response to emerging issues

### Key Areas of Focus

#### Macroeconomic Conditions
- GDP growth and economic outlook
- Employment and wages
- Inflation and price stability
- Consumer spending and business investment

#### Financial Markets
- Market conditions and stability
- Access to credit
- Housing finance
- Financial inclusion

#### International Economics
- Trade policy and competitiveness
- Foreign exchange
- Global economic developments
- International financial institutions

### Annual Reports

Treasury contributes to key annual reports including:
- **Economic Report of the President** - Council of Economic Advisers
- **Social Security and Medicare Trustees Reports** - Annual assessments
- **Financial Stability Reports** - FSOC annual reports

### Data and Statistics

Treasury provides access to economic data through:
- [Treasury Data](/data/)
- [Fiscal Data](https://fiscaldata.treasury.gov/)
- [Bureau of Economic Analysis](https://www.bea.gov/)
