The Office of Economic Policy provides analysis and advice on domestic and international economic issues to support Treasury's policy decisions.

## Key Responsibilities

### Economic Analysis
Monitoring and analyzing current economic conditions, trends, and developments in both domestic and global economies to inform policy decisions.

### Policy Development
Developing and evaluating economic policy proposals across a wide range of issues including fiscal policy, labor markets, and financial markets.

### Forecasting
Producing economic forecasts that support budget planning, policy analysis, and public communications.

### Research
Conducting rigorous economic research on topics critical to Treasury's mission, including productivity, income distribution, and economic mobility.

## Publications and Reports

The Office produces various economic reports and analyses including:

- Economic briefings for senior Treasury officials
- Analysis of proposed legislation and regulations
- Responses to Congressional inquiries
- Research papers on economic policy topics
