## TIC Press Releases by Topic

The Treasury International Capital (TIC) system releases data through regular press releases. This page organizes these releases by topic.

### Major Foreign Holders of Treasury Securities

Monthly releases on foreign holdings of U.S. Treasury securities:
- Country-by-country breakdown
- Changes from previous month
- Historical comparisons

### U.S. International Portfolio Investment

Comprehensive data on cross-border portfolio investment:

#### U.S. Holdings of Foreign Securities
- Annual survey data
- Country and security type breakdown
- Long-term securities holdings

#### Foreign Holdings of U.S. Securities
- Annual survey data
- Holdings by country of holder
- Holdings by type of security

### Monthly TIC Data

Regular monthly releases include:

#### Banking Data
- Net U.S. acquisition of long-term securities
- Cross-border banking flows
- Net foreign purchases

#### Portfolio Flows
- Long-term portfolio capital flows
- Transactions in long-term securities
- Bank-reported data

### Release Schedule

TIC data is released according to a published schedule:
- Monthly banking data
- Monthly portfolio flow data
- Annual surveys
