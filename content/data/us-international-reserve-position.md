## U.S. International Reserve Position

Treasury publishes weekly data on the U.S. international reserve position, including reserve assets and other foreign currency assets.

## Components

### Official Reserve Assets
- Gold
- Special Drawing Rights (SDRs)
- IMF Reserve Position
- Foreign Currency Reserves

### Other Foreign Currency Assets
Assets in currencies of non-residents.

## Publication Schedule

The U.S. International Reserve Position is published weekly, typically on Friday afternoons.

## Data Access

Historical data and current releases are available on the Treasury website and through the Fiscal Data portal.
