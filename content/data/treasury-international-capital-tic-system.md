## TIC System Overview

The Treasury International Capital (TIC) reporting system collects data on cross-border portfolio investment flows and positions between U.S. residents and foreign residents.

## Key Data Series

### Monthly Data
- Net purchases of long-term securities
- Foreign holdings of U.S. securities
- U.S. holdings of foreign securities

### Annual Surveys
- Foreign holdings of U.S. securities (SHL/SHLA)
- U.S. holdings of foreign securities (SHC/SHCA)

## Major Foreign Holders

Monthly estimates of major foreign holders of Treasury securities.

## Uses of TIC Data

- Balance of payments statistics
- International investment position
- Financial stability analysis
- Policy research

## Reporting Requirements

U.S. financial institutions report cross-border transactions and positions to Treasury.
