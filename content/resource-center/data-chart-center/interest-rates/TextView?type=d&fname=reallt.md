## Daily Treasury Real Long-Term Rates

The Treasury real long-term rate is derived from Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS) with remaining maturities of 10 years or more.

### Real Long-Term Average Rate

This rate represents:
- Average yield on TIPS with 10+ years remaining
- Real (inflation-adjusted) return
- Derived from secondary market trading
- Calculated daily

### What It Measures

The real long-term rate shows:
- Expected real return on long-term government bonds
- Compensation for lending after inflation
- Long-term real interest rate expectations

### Relationship to Other Rates

Combined with nominal rates, provides insight into:
- Long-term inflation expectations
- Real vs. nominal economic conditions
- Market views on future inflation

### Data Availability

- **Updates**: Daily (business days)
- **Source**: TIPS secondary market trading
- **History**: Available since TIPS introduction
- **Format**: Text and downloadable

### Uses

Real long-term rates are used for:
- Long-term inflation analysis
- Real return projections
- Liability-driven investment
- Economic modeling
