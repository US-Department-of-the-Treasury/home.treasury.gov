## Daily Treasury Par Real Yield Curve Rates

The Treasury par real yield curve rates are derived from Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS) trading in the secondary market. These rates represent real (inflation-adjusted) yields.

### What Are Real Yield Curve Rates?

Real yield rates represent the return on Treasury securities after adjusting for inflation:
- Based on TIPS secondary market trading
- Interpolated to constant maturity points
- Represent real return to investors

### TIPS Overview

Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS):
- Principal adjusts with inflation (CPI-U)
- Pay fixed coupon on inflation-adjusted principal
- Protect purchasing power against inflation

### Available Maturities

Real yield data is published for:
- 5 year
- 7 year
- 10 year
- 20 year
- 30 year

### Data Availability

- **Updates**: Daily (business days)
- **Time**: Late afternoon Eastern Time
- **History**: Historical data available
- **Format**: Text and downloadable formats

### Uses of Real Yield Rates

These rates are used for:
- Measuring inflation expectations (breakeven analysis)
- Real return benchmarking
- Liability-driven investing
- Inflation hedging analysis
- Economic research
