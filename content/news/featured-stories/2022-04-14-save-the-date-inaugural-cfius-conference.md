The U.S. Department of the Treasury is pleased to announce the first Committee on Foreign Investment in the United States (CFIUS) Conference to take place on Thursday, June 16th at the U.S. Department of the Treasury.  This inaugural event will feature speakers from across the U.S. Government who will provide insights on CFIUS authorities, processes, and practice primarily for the business community.

This is a hybrid virtual and in-person event, with limited in-person seating.  To find more information about the event, including information on how to register, please visit the[Conference website](https://events.treasury.gov/s/event-template/a2mt0000001UNMmAAO/inaugural-cfius-conference).  Please note that online registration does not automatically confirm your attendance.  You will receive a separate email after registering that confirms your attendance status and you may be asked to limit the number of in-person attendees from a single entity due to space constraints.  Please note this event is closed press.
