The U.S. Department of the Treasury is pleased to announce the second Joint Virtual U.S.-EU TTC Stakeholder Outreach event co-hosted with the European Commission’s DG TRADE Unit F4 Technology and Security, FDI Screening and the U.S. Department of State.

The event will take place virtually on December 13, 2022, 10:00 – 11:00 EST.

This event will provide the opportunity for interested stakeholders to learn, discuss, and share their perspectives with representatives from the EU and U.S. government on investment screening related matters.

As identified in the[Pittsburgh Statement](https://www.whitehouse.gov/briefing-room/statements-releases/2021/09/29/u-s-eu-trade-and-technology-council-inaugural-joint-statement/), we acknowledge the importance of and share a commitment to consulting closely with diverse stakeholders on both sides of the Atlantic as we undertake our work in the TTC.

To find more information, including on how to register, please visit the[Stakeholder Outreach Event website](https://events.treasury.gov/s/event-template/a2mt0000001mcsRAAQ).
