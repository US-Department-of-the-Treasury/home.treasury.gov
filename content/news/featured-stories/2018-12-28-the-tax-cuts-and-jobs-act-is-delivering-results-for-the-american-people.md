The Trump Administration achieved one of its top legislative goals by enacting the first comprehensive tax reform legislation in over 30 years.  The Tax Cuts & Jobs Act delivers tax cuts to lower- and middle-income families and makes American businesses more competitive.  Treasury played a critical role in developing this legislation, and is now working to implement it.
