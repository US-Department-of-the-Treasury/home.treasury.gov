Deputy Secretary of the Treasury Sam Bodman will address the Inter-American Development Bank (IDB) on Monday, May 17. Secretary Bodman will make the keynote address at the IDB's Multilateral Investment Fund (MIF) Remittances Seminar.



The seminar will coincide with the MIF's release of the first state-by-state survey of remittances sent to Latin America by millions of migrants in the United States.



**WHO:**Deputy Secretary of the Treasury, Samuel W. Bodman
