U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral will travel to Denver, Colorado Tuesday to discuss American competitiveness and the President's agenda to maintain the economy's momentum and ensure that America remains the leader of the global economy.  While in Denver, Treasurer Cabral will visit the 2 nd National Head Start Hispanic Institute. The following event is open to credentialed media:
