**Treasury Official to Discuss Efforts to Achieve Econo​mic Stability and Growth in Europe**



**WASHINGTON – **The U.S. Department of the Treasury announced today that Assistant Secretary for International Finance Charles Collyns will travel to Athens, Greece and Rome, Italy on July 24-26, 2012, as part of Treasury’s ongoing bilateral engagement. Assistant Secretary Collyns will meet with senior government officials and private sector representatives to discuss economic and policy developments in Greece and Italy.
