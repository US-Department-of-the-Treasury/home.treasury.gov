**WASHINGTON** – On Thursday, June 16th, U.S. Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew will deliver remarks and participate in a discussion on U.S.-China economic relations. Secretary Lew will outline key issues on the U.S.-China bilateral economic agenda, including bilateral trade and investment and China’s economic and financial sector reforms.  Following his remarks, the Secretary will hold a discussion moderated by Greg Ip, Chief Economics Commentator for The Wall Street Journal. **WHO**:   U.S. Treasury Secretary Jacob J.
