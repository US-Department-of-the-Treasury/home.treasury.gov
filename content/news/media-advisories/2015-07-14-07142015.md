**** **​ADDIS ABABA** – U.S. Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew will travel to Frankfurt and Berlin, Germany and Paris, France to continue engagement with European counterparts on the ongoing situation in Greece. Following his visit to Addis Ababa, Ethiopia to lead the U.S. Delegation to the Third International Conference in Financing for Development, Secretary Lew will travel on to Frankfurt on July 15, 2015 to meet with European Central Bank President Mario Draghi.  On July 16, 2015, Secretary Lew will be in Berlin to meet with German Finance Minister Wolfgang Schäuble.  In the afternoon, Secretary Lew will travel on to Paris to meet with French Finance Minister Michel Sapin before returning to Washington, DC. In his meetings, Secretary Lew will engage with his counterparts on the global economy as well as discuss the path forward for Greece within the Eurozone. Additional details regarding the Secretary’s travel will be communicated as available. # # # ​​​
