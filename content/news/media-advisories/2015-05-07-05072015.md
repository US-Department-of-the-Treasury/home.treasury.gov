**WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Acting Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence Adam J. Szubin is traveling to Paris, France and Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates (U.A.E.).  In Paris, he will join Secretary of State John Kerry for the Foreign Ministers meeting of the Gulf Cooperation Council, which will focus on shared regional priorities and security cooperation.  In the U.A.E., Acting Under Secretary Szubin will meet with senior government officials and business leaders for discussions on Iran sanctions and the ongoing negotiations as well as cooperation on combating terrorist financing.
