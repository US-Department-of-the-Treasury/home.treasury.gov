**WASHINGTON** – At 11:30 am EDT on Wednesday, September 21, 2011, a senior Treasury official will hold a background briefing ahead of the G-20 Meeting of Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors and the IMF and World Bank annual meetings in Washington later this week. The briefing will be held in the Media Room at the U.S. Department of the Treasury. **WHO:**   Senior Treasury Official **WHAT:**Background media briefing to preview the G-20 and IMF and World Bank annual meetings in Washington, DC **WHEN:**11:30 am EDT, Wednesday, September 21, 2011 **WHERE:**       U.S. Department of the Treasury   1500 Pennsylvania Ave, NW   Media Room   Washington, DC 20220 A listen-in conference call line will be available for this briefing.  For dial-in information, please email [lacey.rose@treasury.gov](mailto:lacey.rose@treasury.gov). Media without Treasury press credentials planning to attend must contact Ronda Buckmon in Treasury’s Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, Social Security number, date of birth and country of citizenship.  This information may also be emailed to [ronda.buckmon@treasury.gov](mailto:ronda.buckmon@treasury.gov). Please note that this is a background briefing only; no cameras or recording devices for broadcast purposes will be allowed.
