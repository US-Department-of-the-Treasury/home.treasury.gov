**EMBARGOED FOR 10:00 AM EST:  December 16, 2013    ** **CONTACT: Treasury Public Affairs, John Sullivan, john.sullivan@treasury.gov ** **Cohen to Continue Treasury’s Global Consultation on the Implementation of Sanctions**   **WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury announced today that Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence David S. Cohen is traveling to Germany, the United Kingdom, Turkey, and Israel this week to continue Treasury’s global engagement on issues related to illicit finance, including the implementation of existing international economic sanctions against Iran. *###* ​
