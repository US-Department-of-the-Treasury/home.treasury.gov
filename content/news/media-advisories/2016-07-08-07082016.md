**​WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Secretary Jacob J. Lew will travel to Paris, London, Brussels and Berlin from July 11 through July 14. In each city, Secretary Lew will meet with international counterparts to discuss continued economic stability and shared economic growth in the United Kingdom, Europe, and globally following the United Kingdom’s referendum on membership in the European Union.  Secretary Lew will also meet with business leaders and market participants to discuss the state of the global economy. Media planning to cover the Secretary’s travel may contact Whitney Smith at whitney.smith@treasury.gov for information regarding logistics.  Additional details and information about media opportunities will be announced in the coming days. # # #
