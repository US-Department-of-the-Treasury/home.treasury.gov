**WASHINGTON **– On Monday, March 9, 2015, U.S. Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew will travel to Baltimore, Maryland, to highlight the Obama Administration’s commitment to fostering economic growth and investing in American infrastructure.  While in Baltimore, Secretary Lew will tour Ellicott Dredges, a South Baltimore manufacturer of equipment for local and international infrastructure projects.



Founded in 1885, Ellicott Dredges has manufactured dredging equipment in Baltimore for nearly 130 years.  Since its founding, Ellicott Dredges has designed and manufactured over 2,000 dredges, and served customers in over 100 countries.



While in Baltimore, the Secretary will also meet with local business and community leaders.
