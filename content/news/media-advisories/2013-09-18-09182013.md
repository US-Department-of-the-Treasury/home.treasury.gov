**FEDERAL ADVISORY COMMITTEE ON INSURANCE (FACI) TO MEET SEPTEMBER 18**



**WASHINGTON – **On Wednesday, September 18, 2013, the Department of the Treasury’s Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance (FACI) will convene in open session. This meeting will include discussions on perspectives on the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002, regulatory developments regarding reinsurance captives, international insurance activities, and updates from its subcommittees.  Members of the FACI plan to discuss these and other topics in its mission to provide advice and recommendations to the Federal Insurance Office. The meeting will also be streamed live by webcast.



**WHO:**


- Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance
- Federal Insurance Office
