**WASHINGTON **– The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Under Secretary for International Affairs Nathan Sheets is traveling to Tokyo, Japan and Hong Kong and Beijing, China July 29-31, 2015.  On July 29 in Tokyo, Under Secretary Sheets will meet with senior economic and financial policy makers at the Ministry of Finance, Bank of Japan, and the Financial Services Agency to discuss the Japanese economy and global growth.  On July 30 in Hong Kong, Sheets will meet with officials and financial regulators at the Hong Kong Treasury and the Hong Kong Monetary Authority.  On July 31 in Beijing, Sheets will continue discussions on the state of the global and Chinese economy with senior Chinese officials.  # # #
