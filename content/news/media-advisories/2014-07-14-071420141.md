**WASHINGTON** – TOMORROW, Tuesday, July 15, 2014, U.S. Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew will visit Verizon’s facilities in Ashburn, Virginia to discuss cybersecurity and highlight the important role of telecommunications companies in supporting the financial system.  Secretary Lew will hold a brief media availability during the tour, which comes ahead of his remarks on cybersecurity and the U.S. financial sector at CNBC’s Delivering Alpha Conference on Wednesday.   **Press logistics: ** **3:15 PM EDT** SECRETARY LEW TOURS VERIZON’S ASHBURN FACILITIES 22001 Loudon County Parkway Ashburn, VA 20147 There will be a brief press availability and b-roll opportunity during the Secretary’s tour.  Media planning to attend must RSVP to Jeff Nelson at [Jeffrey.nelson@verizon.com](mailto:Jeffrey.nelson@verizon.com) by TOMORROW, July 15, 2014 at 1:00 p.m.  Press should arrive by 2:30 p.m. to allow time for equipment sweeps and set-up.  Press call time is subject to change.  #
