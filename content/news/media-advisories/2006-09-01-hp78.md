Weekly Briefing to the Press
 
**WHEN **   
Tuesday, September 5, 10:30 AM (EDT)
 
**WHERE** 
Treasury Department
Media Room (Room 4121)
1500 Pennsylvania Ave., NW
Washington, DC

Media without Treasury press credentials should contact Frances Anderson at (202) 622-2960 or [Frances.Anderson@treasury.gov](mailto:Frances.Anderson@treasury.gov) with the following information: name, Social Security number, and date of birth.
