U.S. Treasury Assistant Secretary for Public Affairs Tony Fratto will hold the weekly media briefing on Monday, March 6 in Treasury's Media Room. The event is open to all credentialed media.



**Who  **Assistant Secretary for Public Affairs Tony Fratto



**What **Weekly Briefing to the Press



**When**Monday, March 6, 11:15 AM (EST)



**Where**Treasury DepartmentMedia Room (Room 4121)1500 Pennsylvania Ave., NWWashington, DC



**Note     **Media without Treasury press credentials should contact Frances Anderson at (202) 622-2960, or[frances.anderson@treasury.gov](mailto:frances.anderson@treasury.gov)with the following information: name, Social Security number, and date of birth.
