FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

JS-1216

  

Treasury Secretary John Snow and State Secretary for Finance Joop Wijn of The Netherlands will hold a signing ceremony for a protocol amending the US-Netherlands income tax treaty at 11:15 a.m. EST on Monday, March 8, 2004 in the Treasury Department's Media Room (room 4121), 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW.

    

The Room will be available for pre-set at10:00 a.m.

   

Media without Treasury press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office ofPublic Affairsat (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-1999.
