**  ** **WASHINGTON** – On Friday, October 7, 2016, U.S. Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew will join a panel discussion on financial inclusion and de-risking entitled “Financial Inclusion not Exclusion: Managing De-Risking” at the International Monetary Fund (IMF) and World Bank Annual Meetings in Washington, D.C.   **WHO**:   Jacob J. Lew, U.S. Treasury Secretary   Her Majesty Queen Máxima of the Netherlands   Dr. Jim Yong Kim, World Bank Group President   Sri Mulyani Indrawati, Indonesian Minister of Finance and IMF Development Committee Chair   Arun Jaitley, Indian Union Minister of Finance and Corporate Affairs   Zhou Xiaochuan, Governor of the People’s Bank of China   Juan Manuel Vega-Serrano, Financial Action Task Force (FATF)             President **WHAT**:          Panel Discussion “[Financial Inclusion not Exclusion: Managing De-Risking](http://www.worldbank.org/en/events/2016/10/07/financial-inclusion-not-exclusion-managing-derisking#3)” **WHEN**:          Friday, October 7, 2016     11:00 a.m. EDT **WHERE**:        Preston Auditorium World Bank Main Complex 1818 H Street, NW Washington, DC **DETAILS:     **The discussion will be live streamed [*here*](https://live.worldbank.org/financial-inclusion-not-exclusion-managing-derisking) # # #
