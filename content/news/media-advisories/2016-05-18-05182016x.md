**WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Secretary Jacob J. Lew will attend the G-7 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors Meeting in Sendai, Japan.  While in Sendai, Secretary Lew will meet with his international counterparts and attend G-7 working sessions to discuss measures to promote global economic stability and growth. On Saturday, May 21, 2016, Secretary Lew will host a press briefing at the conclusion of the G-7 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors Meeting.  *Note: The press briefing is for journalists and still cameras only.  No video cameras will be permitted.* **WHO**:   Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew **WHAT**:G-7 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors Meeting Press Briefing **WHEN**:Saturday, May 21, 2016  3:15 PM JST/ 2:45 AM EDT **WHERE**:        Hotel Sakan, Tsuji-ga-hana (2nd floor) 28 Yakushi Yumoto Akiumachi Taihaku-ku Sendai 982-0241 Miyagi Prefecture **RSVP**:   Media who wish to attend, please RSVP to [Whitney.Smith@treasury.gov](mailto:Whitney.Smith@treasury.gov).   # # #
