**WASHINGTON –** At 7:00 PM EDT on Thursday, March 15, 2012, Secretary Geithner will address the Economic Club of New York at the Grand Hyatt in New York City. In his remarks, the Secretary will take stock of the U.S. economy since President Obama took office – from the legacy of the crisis and other challenges we have faced to recent signs of a strengthening recovery. The Secretary will also outline the primary economic imperatives facing the country and review the Administration’s strategy to support economic growth, put in place the right investments and reforms for our future, and restore fiscal sustainability. Following his remarks, Secretary Geithner will participate in a discussion with Alan Blinder of Princeton University and John Lipsky of the Johns Hopkins School of Advanced International Studies. **WHO: **  Treasury Secretary Tim Geithner **WHAT:**          Remarks on the state of the economy and the Administration’s strategy to address the economic challenges ahead **WHEN:**          7:00 PM EDT, Thursday, March 15, 2012 **WHERE:**       Grand Hyatt New York   Empire Ballroom 109 East 42nd Street  New York City, NY 10017 This event is open press. Media with camera equipment and satellite trucks should arrive by 5:30p.m. All other media should arrive by 6:00p.m.  Interested media should RSVP by 5:00 p.m., TODAY, with Sabrina Sylvain at [Sabrina@econclubny.org](mailto:Sabrina@econclubny.org) or (212) 947 – 7118.  ###​
