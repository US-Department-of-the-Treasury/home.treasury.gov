​  **WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury announced today that Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence David S. Cohen is traveling to the United Kingdom this week, June 10-11, to continue Treasury’s global engagement on countering terrorist financing and ongoing coordinationrelated to the situation in Ukraine.  On Wednesday, June 11, the Under Secretary will discuss Treasury’s efforts to combat transnational organized crime and illicit finance at the Royal United Services Institute (RUSI). His remarks are open press.      **Press logistics** **2:15 PM BST** Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence David Cohen Remarks at the “Tackling Transnational Organised Crime and Illicit Finance” Conference hosted by the Royal United Services Institute (RUSI) 61 Whitehall, London SQ1A 2ET London, United Kingdom The Under Secretary’s remarks are open press. Registration is mandatory. Please RSVP to Saqeb Mueen at [smueen@rusi.org](mailto:smueen@rusi.org).    ###
