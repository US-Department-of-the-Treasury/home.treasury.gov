**WASHINGTON **– The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Under Secretary for International Affairs Nathan Sheets is traveling to Ottawa, Canada on December 4, 2015. 
 In Ottawa, Under Secretary Sheets will discuss developments in the United States, Canada, and the global economy with his counterparts at the Department of Finance Canada, the Bank of Canada, and the Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions.  Under Secretary Sheets will also meet with members of the private sector.
