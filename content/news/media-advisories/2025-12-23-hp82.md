*(Archived Content)*
 
Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education Dan Iannicola, Jr. will visit teens at the Washington, D.C. Boys and Girls Club on Friday to teach them how to understand their paychecks and the withholding system. Iannicola will teach a class at Richard England Clubhouse #14 as part of "Money Matters" National Education Day, a coordinated volunteer effort by the American Payroll Association.
 
**Who:**     Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education Dan Iannicola, Jr.
 
**What: ** Paycheck Class at the Boys and Girls Club
 
**When: ** Friday, Sept. 8   5:00 p.m.
 
**Where:** Richard England Clubhouse #144103 Benning Road, NEWashington, DC
