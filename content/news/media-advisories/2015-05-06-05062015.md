​**WASHINGTON – **On Thursday, May 7, 2015, the Department of the Treasury’s Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance (FACI) will convene in open session.  During this meeting, the FACI will discuss cybersecurity related to the insurance industry and regulatory developments.  The FACI will also discuss natural catastrophes and the role of mitigation, including President Obama’s Executive Order 13690, which established a Federal Flood Risk Management Standard.  The FACI will also receive updates from its subcommittees.  The meeting will be streamed live by webcast. **WHO:** 
- Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance
- Federal Insurance Office
**WHEN:** Thursday, May 7, 2015 1:00 PM EDT   **WHERE:** U.S. Department of the Treasury Cash Room 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW Washington, DC   This event is open press.  A live webcast of this meeting will be available at [https://home.treasury.gov/news/webcasts](https://home.treasury.gov/news/webcasts). **All interested media must RSVP by 4:00 p.m. today, Wednesday, May 6**, to Ronda Buckmon at [Ronda.Buckmon@treasury.gov](mailto:Ronda.Buckmon@treasury.gov).  Media without press credentials issued by Treasury must provide Ms. Buckmon with the following information for access into the building: 1. full legal name, 2. date of birth, 3. Social Security number and 4. country of citizenship.    ###
