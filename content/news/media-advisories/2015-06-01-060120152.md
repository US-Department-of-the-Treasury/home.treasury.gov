​**WASHINGTON **– The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Acting Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence Adam J. Szubin is traveling to Israel and the Palestinian Territories.  In Israel, Acting Under Secretary Szubin will meet with senior government officials for discussions on Iran sanctions and the ongoing negotiations as well as regional counter-terrorism concerns.  Acting Under Secretary Szubin also will meet with senior Palestinian Authority officials to discuss continued mutual efforts to combat terrorist financing.    ###
