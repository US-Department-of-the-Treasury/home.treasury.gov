*(Archived Content)*

 
In the morning, Secretary Lew will arrive in Moscow, Russia to attend the G-20 Meeting of Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors for meetings with his international counterparts to discuss measures to promote economic growth and job creation.
 
In the afternoon, the Secretary will meet with French Finance Minister Pierre Moscovici. 
 
Also in the afternoon, Secretary Lew will meet with German Finance Minister Wolfgang Schauble.   
 
Later, the Secretary will participate in a meeting of Finance and Labor Ministers.    
 
In the late afternoon, Secretary Lew will meet with Singaporean Deputy Prime Minister Tharman Shanmugaratnam.
 
In the evening, the Secretary will meet with European Central Bank President Mario Draghi.
 
Later in the evening, Secretary Lew will participate in a working dinner of G-20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors.
 
All meetings are closed press.
