**WASHINGTON –** The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Secretary Jacob J. Lew will attend the World Economic Forum Annual Meeting in Davos-Klosters, Switzerland, January 23-24, 2014. While there, the Secretary will meet with his international counterparts to discuss economic developments and policies to boost U.S. and global growth and promote financial stability. Additional details regarding Secretary Lew’s schedule will be available in the days ahead. PLEASE NOTE: Media accreditation for the meeting is closed. The deadline for applications was October 13, 2013. For more information, please visit [http://www.weforum.org/press](http://www.weforum.org/press).  ###​
