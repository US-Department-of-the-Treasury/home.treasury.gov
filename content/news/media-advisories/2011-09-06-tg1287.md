**WASHINGTON –** At 1:00 pm EDT on Wednesday, September 7, 2011, a senior Treasury official will hold a background briefing ahead of Secretary Tim Geithner’s trip this week to Marseille, France to attend the G-7 Meeting of Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors and the Deauville Partnership Finance Ministers Meeting on September 9-10, 2011. The briefing will be held in the Media Room at the U.S. Department of the Treasury. The Secretary will meet with G-7 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors on September 9 to discuss the need for a reinvigorated global effort to support growth, jobs and financial stability. On September 10, Secretary Geithner will participate in a meeting of the Deauville Partnership to discuss continued efforts to support successful transitions toward democratic societies and more inclusive economies in the Middle East and North Africa.

Media without Treasury press credentials planning to attend must contact Frances Anderson in Treasury’s Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, Social Security number, date of birth and country of citizenship.  This information may also be emailed to [frances.anderson@treasury.gov](mailto:frances.anderson@treasury.gov). Please note that this is a background briefing only; no cameras or recording devices for broadcast purposes will be allowed.
