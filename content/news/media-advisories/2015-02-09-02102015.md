**WASHINGTON – **On Tuesday, February 10, 2015, the Department of the Treasury’s Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance (FACI) will convene in open session.  This meeting will include discussions on retirement security, the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program, and the evolving insurance issues related to ride-sharing companies.  The FACI will also receive updates from some of its three subcommittees.  The meeting will also be streamed live by webcast.



**WHO:**


- Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance
- Federal Insurance Office


**WHEN:**



Tuesday, February 10, 2015



1:00 PM EST

  

**WHERE:**



U.S. Department of the Treasury



Cash Room



1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW



Washington, DC

  

This event is open press.  A live webcast of this meeting will be available at[https://home.treasury.gov/news/webcasts](https://home.treasury.gov/news/webcasts).



**All interested media must RSVP by 4:30 p.m. EST TODAY, February 9,**to Stephanie Ma at[*Stephanie.Ma@treasury.gov*](mailto:Stephanie.Ma@treasury.gov).Media without press credentials issued by Treasury must provide the following information for access into the building: 1. full legal name, 2. date of birth, 3. Social Security number and 4. country of citizenship.
