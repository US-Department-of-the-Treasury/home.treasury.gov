**WASHINGTON**– The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Under Secretary for International Affairs Nathan Sheets is traveling to London, England, Paris, France, and Brussels, Belgium May 11-13, 2015.



On May 11 in London, Under Secretary Sheets will meet with senior government officials including Bank of England officials to discuss global economic growth.  On May 12 in Paris, Sheets will meet with his counterparts at the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development (OECD).  On May 13 in Brussels, Sheets will continue discussions on global growth with European officials.
