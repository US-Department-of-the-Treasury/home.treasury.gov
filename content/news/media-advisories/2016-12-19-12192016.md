**FOREST HILLS HS HOSTS CONVERSATION WITH TREASURY SECRETARY JACOB J. LEW AND HISTORIAN RON CHERNOW ** ** ** *Monday, December 19, 2016*   **WHAT:** Forest Hills High School will host a moderated conversation with U.S. Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew and Pulitzer Prize-winning historian and author of *Alexander Hamilton*, Ron Chernow at their alma mater. The Secretary and Mr. Chernow will discuss the history and legacy of Alexander Hamilton, how history can influence the arts and currency, the importance of public education as well as share memories from their time at Forest Hills HS.  Following the discussion, students will have an opportunity to ask questions. **WHO:**Jacob J. Lew, Secretary, U.S. Department of Treasury Ron Chernow, Author, Historian, Biographer, *Alexander Hamilton* **WHEN:**Monday, December 19, 2016 11:00 am - 12:00 pm **WHERE:**       Forest Hills High School Auditorium 67-01 110th Street Forest Hills, NY, 11375   **RSVP: **Please RSVP to Yuridia Peña [ypena14@schools.nyc.gov](mailto:ypena14@schools.nyc.gov) ###      Contact: Chancellor’s Press Office (212) 374-5141
