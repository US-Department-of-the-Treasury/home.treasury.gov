**WASHINGTON **– TODAY, February 19, 2016, a senior Treasury official will conduct a background briefing via conference call to discuss an additional investment in the Hardest Hit Fund (HHF) program. Treasury will exercise its authority granted by Congress to obligate up to $2 billion in additional Troubled Asset Relief Program (TARP) funds to HHF. The additional $2 billion in funding will enable participating state Housing Finance Agencies (HFAs) to continue assisting struggling homeowners and stabilizing neighborhoods in many of the nation’s hardest hit communities.
