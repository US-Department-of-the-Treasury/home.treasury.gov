**WASHINGTON **– Treasury Secretary Tim Geithner will hold a media availability on **Thursday, December 1 at 4:10 PM EDT** to discuss the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau’s (CFPB) efforts to help protect consumers from predatory lending practices and ensure they have the clear, straightforward information they need to make the financial choices that are best for them. The media availability will take place at the CFPB’s headquarters at 1700 G Street, NW in Washington D.C. The media availability will follow a closed press meeting at the CFPB headquarters that Secretary Geithner and Deputy Secretary of the Treasury Neal Wolin will hold with Special Advisor to the Secretary of the Treasury for the CFPB Raj Date and members of the CFPB’s leadership team. **WHO:**   Treasury Secretary Tim Geithner **WHAT:**Remarks on CFPB’s consumer protection efforts   **WHEN:****4:10 PM EDT**, Thursday, December 1, 2011   **WHERE:**       CFPB Headquarters   Amphitheatre     1700 G Street, NW   Washington, D.C. 20006 **Press Logistics:** **Members of the media interested in attending the media availability must RSVP by 12:00 PM, Thursday, December 1, 2011. **Please contact Ashley Gordon in CFPB Public Affairs at (202) 435-7446 or [Ashley.Gordon@CFPB.gov](mailto:Ashley.Gordon@CFPB.gov) with your name and news organization to RSVP. Press with camera equipment should arrive at the event no later than 3:00 PM to allow time for security sweeps and set-up. Final access time for all press for all press will be 3:50 PM.
