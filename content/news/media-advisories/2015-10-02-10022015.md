**** **WASHINGTON **– The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Secretary Jacob J. Lew will travel to Lima, Peru for the Annual Meetings of the International Monetary Fund (IMF) and the World Bank Group October 8-10, 2015.  While in Lima, Secretary Lew will also meet with regional and G-20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors to discuss measures to promote global growth. Additional details and information about media opportunities will be announced in the coming days.  Media planning to travel may contact Whitney Smith at whitney.smith@treasury.gov for information about logistics. ##​# ​​
