**WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Treasury Secretary Tim Geithner will travel to Mexico City, Mexico to attend the G-20 Meeting of Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors on February 25-26, 2012. The Secretary will meet with his international counterparts to discuss measures to promote global economic stability and growth. Additional details regarding Secretary Geithner’s schedule will be made available over the coming days.    Currently, press opportunities are scheduled for Saturday, February 25 and Sunday, February 26, including pooled media opportunities for the meeting and a G-20 press conference at its conclusion. Additional details will be announced by meeting organizers. If you are planning to attend, please email Ronda Buckmon at [ronda.buckmon@treasury.gov](mailto:ronda.buckmon@treasury.gov) with your likely travel dates and contact information, so Treasury can communicate logistical updates as necessary. **MEDIA CREDENTIALS:** Online registration is available at [http://www.g20.org/en/acreditaciones](http://www.g20.org/en/acreditaciones). **Please note: the deadline for registration is Wednesday, February 22, 2012.**

Additional details regarding travel logistics will be available over the coming days.   ### ​
