U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral will speak with the Puerto Rican Chamber of Commerce in Fajardo, Puerto Rico on Friday, June 23.  The event is open to the press and the Treasurer is available for interviews on a range of topics including the U.S. economy, financial education, currency and immigration.



The U.S. Treasurer serves as an adviser to the Secretary of the Treasury on matters relating to currency production and security. The Treasurer also serves as one of the Treasury Department's principal advisors and spokespersons in the area of financial literacy and education. Before taking her office at the Treasury, she served as director of the Smithsonian Institution's Center for Latino Initiatives and as president and CEO of the Hispanic Association on Corporate Responsibility.


- Who: U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral
- What: Remarks before the Puerto Rico Chamber of Commerce
- When: Friday, June 23  3:00 p.m. (AST)
- Where: El Conquistador Resort, Poinsettia Room1000 Conquistador Ave.Fajardo, Puerto Rico
