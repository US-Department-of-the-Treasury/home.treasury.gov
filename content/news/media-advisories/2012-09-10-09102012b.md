Brainard to Meet with European Officials, Discuss Efforts to Achieve Economic Stability and Growth



**WASHINGTON**** –**The U.S. Department of the Treasury announced today that Under Secretary for International Affairs Lael Brainard will travel to Spain, France and Germany on September 10-12, 2012. The Under Secretary will meet with senior government officials to discuss their plans to achieve financial stability and growth in Europe and globally.



On Monday, September 10, Under Secretary Brainard will meet with government officials in Madrid, Spain. On Tuesday, September 11 the Under Secretary will meet with government officials in Paris, France and Berlin, Germany. On Wednesday, September 12, she will meet with officials in Frankfurt, Germany. These meetings are closed press.
