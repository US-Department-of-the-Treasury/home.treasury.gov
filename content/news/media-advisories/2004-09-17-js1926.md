Treasury's Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education, Dan Iannicola will lead a financial education roundtable and discuss Treasury's financial education initiatives at the University of South Florida's Gus A. Stavros Center for Free Enterprise and Economic Education in Tampa.  Iannicola will also discuss the role of the Financial Literacy and Education Commission in improving financial education across the country.



The Gus A. Stavros Center for Free Enterprise and Economic Education, an affiliate of the Florida Council on Economic Education, promotes the integration of free enterprise and economic education into the K-12 curriculum in the ten school districts served by the University of South Florida.



**WHO:**Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education, Dan Iannicola Jr.



**WHAT:**Financial Education Roundtable



**WHEN:**Friday, September 17, 20043 p.m. (EDT)



**WHERE:**The Gus Stavros Center for Free Enterprise and Economic EducationUniversity of South Florida College of Education4202 East Fowler AvenueTampa, FL 33620
