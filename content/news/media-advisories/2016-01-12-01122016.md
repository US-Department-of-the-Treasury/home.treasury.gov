**WASHINGTON **- Secretary Jacob J. Lew will travel to Atlanta, GA on Thursday, January 14, 2016 as part of the “State of the Union: Cabinet in Your Community” road tour to highlight the growing economy and important steps we can take to cement our recovery as well as Treasury’s financial inclusion initiatives, including[myRA](https://myra.gov/).  Secretary Lew will tour the Georgia Institute of Technology’s Advanced Technology Development Center (ATDC) alongside Atlanta Mayor Kasim Reed.  After the tour, Secretary Lew and Mayor Reed will host a roundtable with local entrepreneurs and provide brief remarks.

While in Atlanta, the Secretary will also meet with business leaders to discuss the state of the economy, and deliver a keynote address at the HOPE Global Forums Annual Meeting.  Secretary Lew's remarks will tie back to the event's theme of "reimaging the global economy," with a message focused on greater financial inclusion both here and across the globe.
