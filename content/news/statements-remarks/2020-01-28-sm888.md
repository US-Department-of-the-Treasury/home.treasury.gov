Washington – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin issued the following statement today following the release of President Trump’s Vision for Peace:

“The announcement of President Trump’s Vision for Peace is a historic step towards a more secure and prosperous future for both Israelis and Palestinians.  This plan envisions an unprecedented mutual respect for sovereignty, enhanced economic growth and opportunity in the region, and important measures to ensure safety and security.  We encourage both parties to seize this opportunity to negotiate in good faith, resolve differences, and pursue an agreement based on this realistic proposal that addresses the core requirements of both Israelis and Palestinians.”
