We welcome the commitment of the Argentine authorities to implement macroeconomic policies and a reform agenda to stabilize the economy and promote growth, which are reflected in the recent staff-level agreement that is being considered by the IMF Executive Board.We strongly support the commitment and determination of the Argentine authorities to achieve a strong and stable Argentinian economy that benefits the people of Argentina.
