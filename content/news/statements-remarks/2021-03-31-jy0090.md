**Statement from the U.S. Department of the Treasury**

This #TransDayOfVisibility we stand with our transgender & non-binary community at the Treasury Dept and across the country.

We celebrate your contributions, courage, commitment to authenticity, and the richness you bring to our communities.

[[Tweet](https://twitter.com/USTreasury/status/1377281492691394561?s=20), @USTreasury, 11:27AM 3/31/21]

 

** Statement from Secretary Janet Yellen:**

Treasury’s public servants are the most dedicated, brilliant people I’ve ever worked with.

Treasury’s transgender & non-binary public servants are all that — and incredibly courageous, too. They are an inspiration.

[[Tweet,](https://twitter.com/SecYellen/status/1377282240879718403?s=20)@SecYellen, 11:30AM 3/31/21]
