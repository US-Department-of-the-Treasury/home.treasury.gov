**Washington** – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met today with Japanese Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister Taro Aso. Secretary Mnuchin and Minister Aso reaffirmed the importance of the U.S.-Japan economic relationship and discussed preparations for the G-20 Finance Ministerial in June. They also discussed other key issues including China, North Korea, and Iran sanctions.
