WASHINGTON – Yesterday, Secretary of the Treasury Scott K.H. Bessent met with Mubadala Group Managing Director and CEO Khaldoon Khalifa Al Mubarak of the United Arab Emirates (UAE). Secretary Bessent reaffirmed the strong investment relationship between the United States and UAE and expressed his interest in deepening these strategic economic ties.

Secretary Bessent was glad to hear Managing Director Al Mubarak’s thoughts on his approach to strategic investments, as well as priority investment sectors.
