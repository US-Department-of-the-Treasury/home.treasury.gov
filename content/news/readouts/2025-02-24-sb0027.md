WASHINGTON – Today, U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Scott Bessent held a meeting with French Minister of Economy, Finance, and Industrial and Digital Sovereignty Éric Lombard. During their meeting, Secretary Bessent discussed shared international economic and national security priorities. Secretary Bessent also highlighted the importance of continuing the U.S. and France’s long-standing transatlantic relationship.
