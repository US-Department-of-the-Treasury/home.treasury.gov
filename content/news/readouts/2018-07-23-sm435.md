**BUENOS AIRES **– On Sunday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Governor of the Bank of England Mark Carney. The Secretary and Governor discussed the importance of the Financial Stability Board to the international economy. The Secretary and Governor also discussed issues relating to cryptocurrency and fintech, as well as their shared commitment to achieving strong and broad-based economic growth, trade, and security.
