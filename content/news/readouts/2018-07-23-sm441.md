**BUENOS AIRES**– On Friday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Canadian Minister of Finance Bill Morneau. Secretary Mnuchin and Minister Morneau reaffirmed the importance of U.S.-Canada economic relations, and discussed their shared commitment to achieving strong and broad-based economic growth, trade, and security. The Secretary and Minister discussed the importance of a fair and reciprocal trade relationship between the United States and Canada.
