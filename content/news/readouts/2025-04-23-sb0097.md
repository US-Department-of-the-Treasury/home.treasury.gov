WASHINGTON – Today, Secretary of the Treasury Scott K.H. Bessent met with Prime Minister Denys Shmyhal and Minister of Finance Sergii Marchenko of Ukraine. Secretary Bessent reaffirmed the United States’ support for Ukrainian sovereignty and emphasized the United States’ dedication to secure a lasting, durable peace for the people of both Ukraine and Russia.

Secretary Bessent highlighted the benefits of the economic partnership between the people of the United States and Ukraine and noted that the highly productive technical talks remain ongoing between the two sides. Secretary Bessent emphasized the need to conclude technical talks and sign the economic partnership between the United States and Ukraine as soon as possible.
