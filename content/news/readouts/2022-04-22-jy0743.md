WASHINGTON - U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen met yesterday with UK Chancellor of the Exchequer Rishi Sunak. They discussed continued close coordination on the implementation and enforcement of sanctions in response to Russia’s unprovoked and brutal war on Ukraine. They also discussed investment screening and progress on global minimum tax implementation. They exchanged views on digital assets and looked forward to further collaboration on the topic.
