**Jerusalem**–U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Israeli Finance Minister Moshe Kahlon. Secretary Mnuchin and Minister Kahlon discussed the outlook for the Israeli economy and the government’s fiscal and reform priorities. Secretary Mnuchin underscored the close ties between the United States and the State of Israel and the United States’ continuing commitment to support the safety and security of Israel.
