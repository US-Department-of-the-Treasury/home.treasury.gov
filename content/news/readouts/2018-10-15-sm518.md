**BALI **– Last week, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Finance Minister Sri Mulvani Indrawati. Secretary Mnuchin thanked Indonesia for successfully hosting the IMF and World Bank Annual Meetings, and reaffirmed the importance of continued economic ties between the United States and Indonesia. The Secretary and Minister discussed the importance of free, fair, and reciprocal trade in the Asia-Pacific region, our commitment to maintain pressure on Iran and DPRK through sanctions, as well as ongoing security issues of mutual concern.
