**BALI** – Last week, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Mexican Secretary of Finance and Public Credit Jose Antonio Gonzalez Anaya. Secretary Mnuchin and Secretary Anaya reaffirmed the importance of strong economic ties between the United States and Mexico, and discussed the importance of the USMCA trade agreement. Secretary Mnuchin and Secretary Anaya also discussed the importance of intensifying pressure on the Maduro regime in Venezuela and addressing the continuing humanitarian crisis for which it is responsible.
