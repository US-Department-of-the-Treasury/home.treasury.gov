**WASHINGTON** – U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Steven Mnuchin spoke by phone yesterday with Republic of India Minister of Finance Arun Jaitley.

Secretary Mnuchin underscored India's importance as an economic partner of the United States.  The Secretary  and Finance Minister expressed a desire to continue the robust bilateral dialogue between Treasury and the Ministry of Finance in the year ahead.
