**WASHINGTON** – In his meetings on March 18, 2017, in Baden Baden, Germany, with People's Bank of China Governor Zhou Xiaochuan and Chinese Minister of Finance Xiao Jie, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin underscored the importance of the United States and China working together to foster a more balanced and mutually beneficial economic relationship. The Secretary emphasized he will work with both Minister Xiao and Governor Zhou on economic, financial, and illicit finance issues.
