**WASHINGTON**– U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin met with UK Chancellor of the Exchequer Philip Hammond.  The Secretary and Chancellor discussed bilateral economic issues between the United States and the United Kingdom as well as developments in Brexit negotiations. Secretary Mnuchin thanked the Chancellor for the continued work in countering illicit financial activity as it relates to North Korea, to include the recently released G-7 Finance Ministers joint statement on illicit financial activity by North Korea and the urgency for countries to expel North Korea’s overseas banking and trade representatives
