**WHISTLER**- U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with U.K. Chancellor of the Exchequer Philip Hammond. Secretary Mnuchin and Chancellor Hammond reaffirmed the longstanding special relationship between the United States and United Kingdom, and discussed recent developments in Brexit negotiations. The Secretary and Chancellor also discussed ongoing issues in the Indo-Pacific region, including those pertaining to Iran and North Korea.
