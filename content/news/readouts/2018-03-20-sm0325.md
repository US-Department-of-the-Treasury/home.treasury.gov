**Buenos Aires**– Yesterday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Finance Minister Bill Moreau of Canada. The Secretary and Minister discussed the long standing economic and trade relationship between the United States and Canada, including the successful renegotiation of NAFTA. Secretary Mnuchin and Minister Moreau further discussed working together to help resolve the situation in Venezuela. The Secretary thanked the Minister and emphasized the importance of the two nations collaborating to counter North Korea's illicit financial activityand the financing of terrorism.
