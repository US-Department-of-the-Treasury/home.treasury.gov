Washington - U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Steven T. Mnuchin met with Russian Finance Minister Anton Siluanov at the Minister’s request on the sidelines of the IMF World Bank Annual Meetings. The Secretary and Minister discussed issues involving North Korea, Syria, and Ukraine.
