WASHINGTON – Today, Secretary of the Treasury Scott K.H. Bessent met with Finance H.E. Ali bin Ahmed Al Kuwari of Qatar. Secretary Bessent discussed positive opportunities for expanding economic relations between the United States and Qatar and affirmed the Treasury’s intent to expand American leadership in the Bretton Woods system.

Secretary Bessent updated Minister Al Kuwari on the United States’ maximum pressure campaign against the Iranian regime, as well as other national security issues of mutual interest.
