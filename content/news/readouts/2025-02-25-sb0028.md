WASHINGTON – Today, U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Scott Bessent held a meeting with Australian Treasurer Jim Chalmers. They discussed key areas of cooperation, including economic security issues in the Indo-Pacific region.  The Secretary highlighted the importance of enhancing the resilience of supply chains for critical minerals and underscored our concerns around Chinese economic imbalances and unfair practices.  The Secretary also outlined the America First Trade Policy and welcomed opportunities for joint economic prosperity between our two countries.
