**BIARRITZ** – On Saturday, August 24, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with French Finance Minister Bruno Le Maire. The Secretary reaffirmed the longstanding relationship between the United States and France. The Secretary and Minister further discussed finding a comprehensive solution to modernize international taxation, as well as ongoing security issues of mutual concern.
