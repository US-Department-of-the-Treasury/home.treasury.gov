WASHINGTON – Yesterday Deputy Secretary of the Treasury Wally Adeyemo met with Estonian Foreign Minister Margus Tsahkna. In this conversation, Deputy Secretary Adeyemo reaffirmed the importance of our close coordination on strengthening the international response to Russia’s illegal and unprovoked war on Ukraine through combatting the evasion of sanctions and export control measures. Both confirmed the importance of enforcement of the G7+ price cap mechanism. The Deputy Secretary and Foreign Minister Tsahkna also shared views on ongoing work to ensure that Russian is required to pay for the damage is has caused to Ukraine.
