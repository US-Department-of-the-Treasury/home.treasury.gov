**Washington**- U.S. Treasury Under Secretary for International Affairs David Malpass met with Argentina’s Treasury Minister Nicolas Dujovne. Under Secretary Malpass reiterated strong support for the Macri administration’s market-oriented reform program to promote private sector-led growth. He commended the Minister’s plan to strengthen fiscal policy and Argentina’s decisive actions to help re-anchor inflation. Under Secretary Malpass welcomed Argentina’s discussions with the International Monetary Fund regarding Argentina’s program to boost growth and market reforms and said that Treasury would follow closely the developments.
