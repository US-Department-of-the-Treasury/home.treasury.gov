**Washington**– Today, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Vietnamese Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Pham Binh Minh. The Secretary and Deputy Prime Minister reaffirmed the importance of continued positive economic and trade relations between the United States and Vietnam. Secretary Mnuchin and the Deputy Prime Minister also discussed ongoing security issues of mutual concern.
