**WASHINGTON **– On Saturday, April 22, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met his counterparts Minister of Finance Amr El-Garhy and Central Bank Governor Tarek Amer of Egypt.  The Secretary highlighted U.S. support for Egypt’s economic reform programs.  Secretary Mnuchin and the Minister also discussed the importance of bilateral cooperation on countering the financing of terrorism issues.
