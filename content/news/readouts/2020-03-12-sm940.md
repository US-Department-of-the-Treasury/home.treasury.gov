**WASHINGTON **– Today, U.S. Treasury Under Secretary for International Affairs Brent McIntosh held a call with his counterparts from Canada, the European Commission, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, and the United Kingdom to discuss COVID-19. The G7 is in regular contact and committed to continued international cooperation to address the global health and economic impacts of COVID-19.
