**Washington** – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Prime Minister Malcolm Turnbull of Australia. Secretary Mnuchin and Prime Minister Turnbull discussed the global economic outlook, as well as latest developments in the U.S. and Australian economies. The Secretary highlighted the importance of cooperation within the region as it relates to the North Korea nuclear threat.
