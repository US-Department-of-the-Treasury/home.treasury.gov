**WASHINGTON**– U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin spoke by phone last night with Japanese Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister Taro Aso.Secretary Mnuchin underscored that he looked forward to working with Deputy Prime Minister Aso to further enhance the close economic relationship between the United States and Japan.  The Secretary stressed the importance of cooperation between the United States and Japan on bilateral, regional, and global macroeconomic and financial issues.
