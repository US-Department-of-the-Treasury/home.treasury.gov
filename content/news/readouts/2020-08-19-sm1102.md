**WASHINGTON** – Today U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Iraqi Prime Minister Mustafa al-Kadhimi.  The Secretary and Prime Minister reaffirmed the partnership between the U.S. and Iraq and discussed collaborative efforts to promote economic stability, security and prosperity in Iraq, and to combat illicit finance and terrorism.
