**BUENOS AIRES** – On Sunday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Vice Chancellor and Finance Minister Olaf Scholz of Germany. Secretary Mnuchin and Vice Chancellor Scholz emphasized the importance of continued bilateral economic relations between the United States and Germany, as well as security issues of mutual concern. The Secretary and Vice Chancellor also discussed their shared commitment to achieving strong and broad-based economic growth, trade, and security.
