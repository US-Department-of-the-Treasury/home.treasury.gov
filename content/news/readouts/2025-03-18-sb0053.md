**WASHINGTON** – Today, Secretary of the Treasury Scott K.H. Bessent met with United Arab Emirates (UAE) National Security Advisor His Highness Sheikh Tahnoon bin Zayed Al Nahyan. Secretary Bessent underscored the strong economic, investment, and national security ties between the UAE and the United States, which he hopes to build on during his tenure. He also emphasized the importance of cooperation to curtail Iran’s nuclear and ballistic missile efforts as well as its support for terrorists that destabilize the region.
