**WASHINGTON** – Earlier today, Secretary of the Treasury Scott Bessent held an introductory call with European Commissioner Valdis Dombrovskis.  Secretary Bessent discussed shared priorities in the context of the President’s America First economic agenda and underlined his commitment to working with the EU on key policy issues of mutual interest.
