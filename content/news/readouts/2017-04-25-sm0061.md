**WASHINGTON** – On Saturday, April 22, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Finance Minister of Arun Jaitley of India. Secretary Mnuchin underscored the strong U.S. – India bilateral economic relationship.  The Secretary expressed confidence in increased economic collaboration between the two nations.  Secretary Mnuchin relayed that he looks forward to working with Minister Jaitley on India’s successful economic policy objectives and reforms.
