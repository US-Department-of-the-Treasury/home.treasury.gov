**WHISTLER** - U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Japanese Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister Taro Aso. Secretary Mnuchin and Deputy Prime Minister Aso discussed the ongoing close cooperation between the United States and Japan on Indo-Pacific and bilateral economic issues. The Secretary and Deputy Prime Minister also discussed developments related to North Korea and Iran.
