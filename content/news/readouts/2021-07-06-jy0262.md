WASHINGTON – Earlier today, Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen spoke with European Commission Executive Vice President and Commissioner for a Europe Fit for the Digital Age Margrethe Vestager. The Secretary discussed international taxation issues and welcomed the shared goals of the United States and European Union to make the international tax system more equitable and to end the global race to the bottom on corporate taxes.
