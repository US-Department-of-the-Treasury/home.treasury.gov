**WASHINGTON** – Last week, Secretary of the Treasury Scott Bessent met with Vice Chancellor and Finance Minister Lars Klingbeil of Germany to discuss the EU’s planned support for Ukraine. Secretary Bessent underscored the Administration’s expectation that Europe proactively increase G7 pressure on Russia.

Secretary Bessent went on to emphasize the United States’ desire to cooperate with Germany and European allies on additional sanctions on Iran following the UN snapback.
