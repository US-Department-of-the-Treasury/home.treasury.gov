**BUENOS AIRES** – On Sunday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Italian Minister of Economy and Finances Giovanni Tria. Secretary Mnuchin and Minister Tria reaffirmed the importance of the bilateral economic relationship between the United States and Italy, and discussed their shared commitment to strong and broad-based economic growth, trade, and security. The Secretary and Minister also discussed ongoing security issues of mutual concern.
