**New York** – Today, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with His Excellency Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad Al Thani, Amir of Qatar, to discuss the strong bilateral economic relations between the United States and Qatar. They also discussed the importance of the continued partnership to counter terrorist- and proliferation-financing networks.
