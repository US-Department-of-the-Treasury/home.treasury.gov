**BUENOS AIRES** – On Saturday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with U.K. Chancellor of the Exchequer Philip Hammond. Secretary Mnuchin and Chancellor Hammond reaffirmed the longstanding special relationship between the United States and United Kingdom, and discussed recent developments as pertaining to Brexit. The Secretary and Chancellor also discussed their shared commitment to achieving strong and broad-based economic growth, trade, and security.
