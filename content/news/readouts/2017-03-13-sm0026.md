**WASHINGTON**– U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin spoke by phone today with Pierre Moscovici, European Commissioner for Economic and Financial Affairs.Secretary Mnuchin conveyed that he looks forward to working closely with Commissioner Moscovici and with the European Commission on a range of strategic issues. He welcomed collaboration to bolster stronger and more inclusive growth and strengthen job creation.
