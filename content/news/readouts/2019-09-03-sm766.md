**WASHINGTON**– Today, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with French Finance Minister Bruno Le Maire. The Secretary reaffirmed the longstanding relationship between the United States and France. The Secretary and the Minister reiterated the commitment of both parties to work together constructively to reach an OECD solution on international taxation by early 2020.
