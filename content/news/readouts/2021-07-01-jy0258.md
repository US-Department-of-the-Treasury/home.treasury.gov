WASHINGTON – Earlier today, Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen spoke with French Minister of Finance, the Economy, and the Recovery Bruno Le Maire. They discussed the importance of advancing global tax reform efforts in the OECD/G20 discussions on international taxation. Secretary Yellen thanked Minister Le Maire for the close cooperation between the U.S. and French teams and his dedication to this effort.
