**Washington**—U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met today with Giancarlo Giorgetti, Italian Under Secretary at the Presidency of the Council of Ministers. Secretary Mnuchin and Under Secretary Giorgetti discussed the importance of continued economic and trade relations between the United States and Italy, as well as the Italian government’s focus on the necessary measures that will lead to increased stability and growth in Italy and throughout Europe.
