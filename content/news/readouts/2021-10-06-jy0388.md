Earlier today, Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen spoke with Jordanian Finance Minister Mohamad Al-Ississ. During the call, Secretary Yellen discussed the importance of joint collaboration and further engagement on shared interests. This includes supporting Jordan’s economic development and reform efforts, and cooperating to counter illicit finance to combat terrorism. Secretary Yellen also emphasized a shared commitment to forcefully address the threat of climate change.
