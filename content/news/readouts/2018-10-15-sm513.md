**BALI** – Last week, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with European Commission Vice President Valdis Dombrovskis. Secretary Mnuchin and Vice President Dombrovskis reaffirmed the importance of continued economic ties between the United States and the European Union. The Secretary and Vice President also discussed ongoing trade and security issues.
