**WASHINGTON**– Earlier today, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with His Excellency** ** Giorgi Kvirikashvili, Prime Minister of Georgia. Secretary Mnuchin and Prime Minister Kvirikashvili discussed Georgia's economic priorities. The Secretary congratulated Prime Minister Kvirikashvili on reaching an agreement with the International Monetary Fund on a program that support Georgia's economic reforms. Secretary Mnuchin also underscored the importance of working together on mutual national security issues.
