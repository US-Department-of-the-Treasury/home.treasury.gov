**Davos** –  U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with His Majesty King Abdullah II Ibn Al Hussein of Jordan. Secretary Mnuchin and King Abdullah II discussed the bilateral economic relationship between the United States and Jordan. Additionally, the Secretary and His Majesty discussed the progress of fiscal and economic reforms taking place in Jordan. The Secretary thanked King Abdullah II for his nation’s partnership in the disruption of ISIS financial networks in the region.
