**LONDON**– Today, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with U.K. Chancellor of the Exchequer Sajid Javid. The Secretary received an update on the Brexit process and reaffirmed the longstanding special relationship between the United States and United Kingdom. Secretary Mnuchin and Chancellor Javid discussed economic issues as well as shared security priorities.
