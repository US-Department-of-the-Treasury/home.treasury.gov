**BAHRAIN**- U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with His Majesty the King Shaikh Hamad bin Isa Al Khalifa of Bahrain.  The Secretary thanked the King for his generous hospitality, and for his support of the Peace to Prosperity initiative.  Secretary Mnuchin and the King also reaffirmed the importance of the ongoing partnership between the United States, Bahrain, and other allies on the Terrorist Financing Targeting Center (TFTC), and discussed other ongoing security issues of mutual concern.
