**OSAKA** – On June 28, 2019, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with French Minister of Finance and Economy Bruno Le Maire. The Secretary and Minister underscored the strong economic partnership between the United States and France. They also discussed issues related to digital taxation and ongoing cooperation between the U.S. and France to combat illicit financial activity. The Secretary highlighted the need to counter terror financing by Iran. The Secretary and Minister further discussed ongoing economic reforms in the EU.
