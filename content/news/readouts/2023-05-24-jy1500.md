WASHINGTON - Today, U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen met today with United Kingdom Shadow Chancellor of the Exchequer Rachel Reeves. The Secretary discussed Russia sanctions and support for Ukraine, as well as policies to generate growth and jobs.
