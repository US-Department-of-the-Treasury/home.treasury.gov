NIIGATA – Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen met with Finance Minister of Indonesia, Sri Mulyani on Thursday.  Secretary Yellen discussed key G20 priorities including debt and MDB evolution.  She also discussed Indonesia’s vital role co-chairing the G20 Joint Finance and Health Task Force and goals for Indonesia’s Just Energy Transition Partnership.  Secretary Yellen affirmed the strong U.S.-Indonesia partnership and agreed to continue to work together addressing global economic challenges.
