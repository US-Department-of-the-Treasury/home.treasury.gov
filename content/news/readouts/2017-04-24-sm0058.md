## ​Secretary Mnuchin’s Meeting with European Commission Vice President for the Euro and Social Dialogue, Financial Stability, Financial Services and Capital Markets Union Valdis Dombrovskis and European Commissioner for Economic and Financial, Taxation and Customs Affairs Pierre Moscovici

**WASHINGTON** – On Thursday, April 20, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with European Commission Vice President for the Euro and Social Dialogue, Financial Stability, Financial Services and Capital Markets Union Valdis Dombrovskis and European Commissioner for Economic and Financial, Taxation and Customs Affairs Pierre Moscovici.  Secretary Mnuchin underscored the U.S. commitment to engage in international financial regulatory forums that promote financial stability and a level economic playing field globally.
