**Buenos Aires** – Yesterday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with French Minister of Finance and Economy Bruno Le Maire. The Secretary and Minister discussed the long standing economic and trade relationship between the United States and France. Secretary Mnuchin and Minister Le Maire further discussed the continuing economic reforms taking place in the EU. The Secretary also underscored the importance of U.S. and French cooperation to counter the financing of terrorism and address North Korea and Iran's illicit financial activity.
