**Buenos Aires**- U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with U.K. Chancellor of the Exchequer Philip Hammond. The Secretary and Chancellor discussed the longstanding special relationship between the United Stated and United Kingdom including the strong bilateral economic relationship between the two nations.  Secretary Mnuchin underscored the importance of the U.S. and UK working together to address North Korea and Iran's illicit financial activity and counter the financing of terrorism.
