**WASHINGTON** – Today, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Latvian Prime Minister Krišjānis Kariņš. Secretary Mnuchin offered his congratulations on the inauguration of Latvia’s new President Egils Levits. The Secretary and Prime Minister discussed Latvia’s recent passage of anti-money laundering reform legislation, and the Secretary encouraged meaningful implementation of the new law. The Secretary and Prime Minister further discussed regional security issues of mutual concern.
