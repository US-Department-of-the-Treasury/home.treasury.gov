**WASHINGTON**– U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin met with with Minister of State for Financial Affairs Obaid Humaid Al Tayer and Central Bank Governor Mubarak al-Mansoori of the United Arab Emirates.  Secretary Mnuchin thanked Minister Al Tayer and Governor al-Mansoori for their leadership on combating illicit finance and their continued support for the Terrorist Financing Targeting Center (TFTC).  The Secretary, Minister and Governor also discussed the bilateral economic and trade issues between the two nations.
