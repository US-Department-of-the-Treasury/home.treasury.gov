**BALI** – Last week, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Vice Chancellor and Finance Minister Olaf Scholz of Germany. Secretary Mnuchin and Vice Chancellor Scholz discussed the importance of continued economic relations between the United States and Germany, as well as with the European Union as a whole. The Secretary and Vice Chancellor also discussed the importance of free fair, and reciprocal trade, as well as ongoing security issues of mutual concern.
