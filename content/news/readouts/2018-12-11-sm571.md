**WASHINGTON** – Today, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Finance Minister Paschal Donohoe of the Republic of Ireland. Secretary Mnuchin and Finance Minister Donohoe discussed the importance of continued good economic relations between the United States and Ireland, as well as with the European Union as a whole. The Secretary and Finance Minister also discussed the need for comprehensive, consensus-based approaches to international tax, and ongoing security issues of mutual concern.
