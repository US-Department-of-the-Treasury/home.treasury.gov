London – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Chancellor of the Exchequer Sajid Javid, of the United Kingdom (U.K.). The Secretary and Chancellor underscored the importance of the special relationship between the U.S. and the U.K., and discussed a range of topics including trade, Brexit, international taxes, and sanctions.
