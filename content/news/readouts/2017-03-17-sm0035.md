**BADEN BADEN**– U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin today met with Michel Sapin, France’s Minister of Finance.

 
During their meeting, Secretary Mnuchin underscored the importance of the U.S. – France relationship. The Secretary emphasized the continued importance of the U.S.-France partnership and our desire to work closely together, including within international institutions like the G-20 and G-7, to promote growth and jobs and counter illicit finance threats.
