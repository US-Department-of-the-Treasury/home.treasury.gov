**BUENOS AIRES**– On Sunday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met at lunch with Saudi Arabian Finance Minister Mohammed Al-Jadaan. Secretary Mnuchin and Minister Al-Jadaan reaffirmed the importance of the relationship between the United States and Saudi Arabia, and discussed ongoing efforts towards combatting illicit finance. The Secretary and Minister also discussed their shared commitment to achieving strong and broad-based economic growth, trade, and security.
