**WASHINGTON**– U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Steven Mnuchin spoke by phone yesterday with South Africa's Minister of Finance Pravin Gordhan.

Secretary Mnuchin underscored the mutual importance of the strong U.S.-South Africa economic relationship, which supports jobs and investment in both countries.  Secretary Mnuchin looked forward to working with Minister Gordhan to promote shared economic interests, bilaterally, within the G20, and in other multilateral fora.
