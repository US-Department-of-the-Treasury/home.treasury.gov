WASHINGTON – Earlier today, U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Scott Bessent held an introductory call with German Finance Minister Jörg Kukies.  Secretary Bessent discussed shared priorities in the context of the President’s America First economic agenda and reaffirmed the importance of the U.S.-Germany economic relationship.
