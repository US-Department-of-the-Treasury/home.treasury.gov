**WASHINGTON **– On Saturday, April 22, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Minister of Finance Ishaq Dar of Pakistan.  The Secretary welcomed Minister Dar and looked forward to increasing the bilateral relationship between the U.S. and Pakistan, including countering the financing of terrorism.
