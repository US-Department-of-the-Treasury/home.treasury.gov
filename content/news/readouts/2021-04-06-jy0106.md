Earlier today, Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen addressed the virtual meeting of the African Leaders Dialogue on the COVID-Climate Emergency, co-hosted by African Development Bank President Akinwumi Adesina and Global Center on Adaptation Chair Ban Ki-Moon.  The Secretary stressed her commitment to cooperate with African partners on innovative solutions, including through multilateral financial institutions, to help people cope with the impacts of a climate that is already changing.
