**Davos** – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Secretary of Finance José Antonio González Anaya of Mexico. Secretary Mnuchin congratulated Secretary González on his recent appointment. Secretary Mnuchin noted the importance of successfully renegotiating NAFTA to improve the longstanding bilateral economic ties between the United States and Mexico. Secretary Mnuchin underscored the importance of the United States and Mexico working together to combat illicit finance in the Western Hemisphere.
