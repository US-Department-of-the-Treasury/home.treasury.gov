**Washington** – Secretary of the Treasury Scott Bessent spoke last night with Acting President Choi Sang-mok of Korea.  They discussed opportunities for joint prosperity in the context of the Trump Administration’s America First Trade Policy.  Secretary Bessent looks forward to working closely with Acting President Choi to address shared economic and security challenges in the Indo-Pacific region and beyond.
