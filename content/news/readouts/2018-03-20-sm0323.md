**Buenos Aires**– Yesterday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Mark Carney, Chair of the Financial Stability Board and Governor of the Bank of England. The Secretary and Governor discussed international financial regulatory issues that address financial stability risks and promote efficient global financial markets and foster a level playing field globally.
