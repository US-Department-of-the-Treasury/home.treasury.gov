**WASHINGTON** -- Today, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin met with His Majesty King Abdullah II and reaffirmed the United States’ strong support for Jordan.  They discussed economic developments in Jordan and the reforms needed to create stronger growth and opportunities for all Jordanians.  The Secretary and His Majesty also discussed U.S. and Jordanian efforts to disrupt terrorist financing networks.
