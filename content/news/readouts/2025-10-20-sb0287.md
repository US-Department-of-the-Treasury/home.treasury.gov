**WASHINGTON **– Last week, Secretary of the Treasury Scott Bessent met with Minister of National Economy and Finance Kyriakos Pierrakakis of Greece to discuss bilateral trade and investment ties between the United States and Greece. Secretary Bessent and Finance Minister Kyriakos also stressed the need for cooperation on national security issues.
