**Davos** – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Finance Minister Mohammed al-Jadaan of Saudi Arabia. The Secretary and Minister further discussed the important bilateral economic and security relationship between the United States and Saudi Arabia.  Secretary Mnuchin underscored support for Saudi Arabia continuing their Vision 2030 reforms and advancing efforts to diversify the Saudi Arabian economy. The Secretary thanked the Minister for Saudi Arabia’s continued leadership with respect to the Terrorist Financing Targeting Center (TFTC) and ongoing efforts to counter illicit finance and combat terrorism.
