**BUENOS AIRES** – On Sunday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Japanese Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister Taro Aso.  Secretary Mnuchin and Deputy Prime Minister Aso reaffirmed the strong economic relationship between the United States and Japan, and discussed the ongoing close cooperation and shared commitment between the United States and Japan towards achieving strong and broad-based economic growth, trade, and security. The Secretary and Deputy Prime Minister also discussed ongoing security issues of mutual concern.
