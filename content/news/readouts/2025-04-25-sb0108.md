WASHINGTON – Today, Secretary of the Treasury Scott K.H. Bessent met with President Karin Keller-Sutter and Vice President Guy Parmelin of the Swiss Confederation. The Secretary was glad to hear the President and Vice President's thoughts on how to best advance the economic relationship between the United States and Switzerland, including on fair and reciprocal trade.
