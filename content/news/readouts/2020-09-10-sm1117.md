**WASHINGTON**– On September 9, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin participated in a call with his counterparts from Australia, Canada, New Zealand, and the United Kingdom.  The call, hosted by the Finance Minister of New Zealand, was the fourth in a series of regular calls among the Finance Ministers of the Five Eyes nations.  The Secretary and Ministers discussed the economic impact of the coronavirus pandemic and the policy responses underway.
