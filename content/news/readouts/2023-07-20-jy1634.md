HANOI – Today, U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen met with Party Central Committee Economic Commission Chairman Tran Tuan Ahn while visiting Vietnam’s capital. Secretary Yellen highlighted the importance of strengthening the U.S.-Vietnamese economic partnership.  The two also discussed Vietnam’s progress in the Just Energy Transition Partnership and paths toward increasing supply chain resiliency.
