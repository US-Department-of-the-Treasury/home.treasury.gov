WASHINGTON - Earlier this week, Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen spoke with Indonesian Finance Minister Sri Mulyani Indrawati. Secretary Yellen emphasized her support for strong U.S.-Indonesia relations and conveyed her intention to deepen bilateral and multilateral cooperation with Indonesia to address key regional and global challenges, including the need for a strong recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic and global efforts to address the threat of climate change.
