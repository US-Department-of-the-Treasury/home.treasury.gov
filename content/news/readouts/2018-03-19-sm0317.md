**Buenos Aires** – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Strategy and Finance Kim, Dong-Yeon of the Republic of Korea. The Secretary and Minister discussed the importance of the bilateral economic and trade relationship between the United States and the Republic of Korea. The Secretary emphasized the importance of cooperation and collaboration between the United States and the Republic of Korea as it relates to North Korea's illicit financial activity in support of its prohibited weapons programs.
