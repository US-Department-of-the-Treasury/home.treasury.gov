**WASHINGTON **–U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin spoke by phone today with Italian Finance Minister Pier Carlo Padoan.Secretary Mnuchin looks forward to working closely with Italy throughthe G-20,and over the year of Italy’s G-7 presidency.  The Secretary welcomed the opportunity to collaborate with Italy on a range of shared interests, including bolstering stronger and more inclusive growth and strengthening job creation.  Secretary Mnuchin also highlighted the strong cooperation on illicit finance threats and looked forward to continuing the close collaboration on these critical issues.
