**BALI** – Last week, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with U.K. Chancellor of the Exchequer Philip Hammond. Secretary Mnuchin and Chancellor Hammond reaffirmed the longstanding special relationship between the United States and United Kingdom, as well as the importance of free, fair, and reciprocal trade. The Secretary and Chancellor also recent developments as pertaining to Brexit, as well as recent security issues of mutual concern, including Iran, Russia, and North Korea.
