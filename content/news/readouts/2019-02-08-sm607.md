**Washington**- Yesterday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with His Excellency Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al Thani of Qatar, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, to discuss the strong bilateral economic relations between the United States and Qatar.   They also discussed the importance of our continued partnership to defeat ISIS, and ongoing efforts to counter terrorist financing in the region.
