**WASHINGTON**– On Thursday, April 20, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister Taro Aso of Japan. The Secretary and Minister discussed the future of the long standing bilateral relationship.  Secretary Mnuchin congratulated Minister Aso on the launch of U.S. – Japan Economic Dialogue and looks forward to working together on the economic issues of mutual benefit.
