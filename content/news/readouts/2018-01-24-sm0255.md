**Davos **– U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Deputy Prime Minister Tharman Shanmugaratnam of Singapore. Secretary Mnuchin congratulated the Deputy Prime Minister on Singapore chairing the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) and discussed the dynamics of Asia’s regional economy. The Secretary thanked the Minister and underscored the importance of working together to combat North Korea’s illicit activity.
