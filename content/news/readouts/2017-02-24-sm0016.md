**WASHINGTON **– U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin spoke by phone today with French Finance Minister Michel Sapin.Secretary Mnuchin welcomed the opportunity to work with France, through the G-20 and bilaterally, on a range of shared interests, including bolstering stronger and more inclusive growth, where the United States and France can cooperate fruitfully.  Secretary Mnuchin also highlighted the strong cooperation on illicit finance threats and looked forward to continuing the close collaboration on these critical issues.
