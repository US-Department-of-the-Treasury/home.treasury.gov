**WASHINGTON** – Today, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with German Federal Minister of Economic Affairs and Energy Peter Altmaier. The Secretary and the Minister discussed sanctions issues related to trade with Iran. The Secretary and Minister further discussed European energy security and macroeconomic policies to foster greater global growth prospects.
