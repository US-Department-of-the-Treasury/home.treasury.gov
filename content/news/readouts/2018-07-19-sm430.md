**San Juan, Puerto Rico**– Today, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Puerto Rico Governor Ricardo Rosselló to discuss the island’s return to fiscal stability and economic growth.  The Secretary received a briefing from members of the Governor’s cabinet on fiscal and economic reforms.  He offered to provide technical assistance from the U.S. Treasury to support efforts to reform Puerto Rico government operations.
