**WASHINGTON**– Today U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Egyptian Ambassador to the United States Yasser Reda.  Secretary Mnuchin expressed his appreciation to the Ambassador for his service in Washington and engagement on a range of important issues, including the negotiations on the Grand Ethiopian Renaissance Dam, and wished him well.
