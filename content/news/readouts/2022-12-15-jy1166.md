WASHINGTON- Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen today met with the Chinese Ambassador to the United States, Qin Gang.  They exchanged views on global macroeconomic and financial developments as part of efforts to maintain communication and work together on transnational challenges.  Secretary Yellen noted that she looks forward to future engagements with the Chinese side.
