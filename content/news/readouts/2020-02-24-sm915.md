**Readout from a Treasury Spokesperson on Secretary Mnuchin’s meeting with Japanese Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister Taro Aso**Riyadh – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Japanese Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister Taro Aso. Secretary Mnuchin and Deputy Prime Minister Aso reaffirmed the importance of the economic relationship between the U.S. and Japan, and discussed global economic issues, including the coronavirus outbreak, international taxation and digital assets.**Readout from a Treasury Spokesperson on Secretary Mnuchin’s meeting with Mexican Secretary for Finance and Public Credit Arturo Herrera**Riyadh – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Mexican Secretary for Finance and Public Credit Arturo Herrera. Secretary Mnuchin underscored the importance of the bilateral relationship between the U.S. and Mexico and working together on economic and security issues of mutual concern.**Readout from a Treasury Spokesperson on Secretary Mnuchin’s meeting with Argentine Economy Minister Martín Guzmán**Riyadh – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Argentine Economy Minister Martín Guzmán. Secretary Mnuchin engaged on Argentina’s plans to implement economic policies.**Readout from a Treasury Spokesperson on Secretary Mnuchin’s meeting with Public Investment Fund Governor and Chairman of Aramco, His Excellency Yasir al-Rumayyan**Riyadh – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with His Excellency Yasir al-Rumayyan, Public Investment Fund Governor and Chairman of Aramco. They discussed Aramco’s initial public offering, the Public Investment Fund’s investment outlook, and U.S. investments.**Readout from a Treasury Spokesperson on Secretary Mnuchin’s meeting with Saudi Arabian Crown Prince, His Royal Highness Mohammed bin Salman Al Saud**Riyadh – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with His Royal Highness Mohammed bin Salman Al Saud, Crown Prince, Deputy Premier, and Chairman of the Council of Economic and Development Affairs of Saudi Arabia.  Secretary Mnuchin thanked the Crown Prince for his hospitality and expressed support for Saudi Arabia’s G20 financial sector agenda. They also discussed Vision 2030, economic opportunities, counterterrorism, illicit finance priorities, and continued partnership on the Terrorist Finance Targeting Center.
