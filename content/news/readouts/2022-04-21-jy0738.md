WASHINGTON -- Deputy Secretary of the Treasury Wally Adeyemo met with Chilean Finance Minister Mario Marcel today on the margins of the IMF/World Bank Spring meetings. They discussed the economic policy priorities of President Boric’s administration, including on combatting climate change, and the impact of Russia’s war against Ukraine and spillover effects on the global economy.
