KYIV – Today, Deputy Secretary Adeyemo met with young professionals, entrepreneurs, and activists that are working to help resist Russia’s invasion and secure a better economic future for Ukraine. The Deputy Secretary heard directly from Ukrainian citizens what their perspectives are on Ukraine’s future and the efforts that they are undertaking to support their country. Deputy Secretary Adeyemo also saw how small businesses are adapting to contribute to Ukraine’s war effort.
