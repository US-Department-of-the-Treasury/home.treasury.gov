**BALI**– Last week, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with South Korean Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Economy and Finance Dong Yeon Kim. Secretary Mnuchin and Deputy Prime Minister Kim reaffirmed the strong economic ties between the United States and South Korea, and discussed the importance of the recently revised United States-Korea Trade Agreement. The Secretary and Deputy Prime Minister also discussed ongoing security issues of mutual concern, including Iran and North Korea.
