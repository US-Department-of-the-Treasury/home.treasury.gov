KYIV – Today, Deputy Secretary Adeyemo met with a group of Kyiv School of Economics faculty and students, as well select non-governmental organizations while in Kyiv. The group engaged in a robust discussion on supporting Ukraine’s economic recovery, including through unlocking the value of immobilized Russia sovereign assets, Russia’s transition to a wartime economy, and restricting Russia’s revenue and disrupting Russian procurement of priority goods.
