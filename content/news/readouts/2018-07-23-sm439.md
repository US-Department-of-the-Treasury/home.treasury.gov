**BUENOS AIRES** – On Saturday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with French Minister for the Economy and Finance Bruno Le Maire. The Secretary and Minister reaffirmed the continued importance of the longstanding economic and trade relationship between the United States and France, and discussed the need for strong and broad-based economic growth, trade, and security. Secretary Mnuchin and Minister Le Maire also discussed ongoing security issues of mutual concern, as well as continued economic pressure against sanctioned entities.
