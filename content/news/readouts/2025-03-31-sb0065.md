WASHINGTON — Last Friday, U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Scott K.H. Bessent met with German Finance Minister Jörg Kukies in Washington, DC. The Secretary and Minister discussed shared priorities in the context of the President’s America First economic agenda, and reaffirmed the importance of the U.S.-Germany economic relationship.
