**WASHINGTON** – Today, John K. Hurley, the U.S. Department of the Treasury's Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence, will visit the U.S.-Mexico Border in San Diego, California, where he will meet with law enforcement and the U.S. Department of Justice (U.S. Attorney’s Office for the Southern District of California) to discuss ways that Treasury can continue to collaborate with our partners to help dismantle terrorist drug cartels. Under President Trump and Secretary Bessent’s leadership, Treasury will continue to aggressively target Mexican drug cartels financial networks to keep Americans safe.
