**WASHINGTON** – Today U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Saudi Minister and Advisor to the Royal Court, Mohammed Al-Tuwaijri.  The Secretary and Minister discussed the World Trade Organization, the economic impact of the coronavirus pandemic and associated response measures, and other topics of mutual interest.
