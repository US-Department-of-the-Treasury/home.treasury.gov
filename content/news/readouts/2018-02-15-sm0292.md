**Washington**– U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Finance Minister Johan Van Overtveldt of Belgium. The Secretary and Minister discussed global economic developments and bilateral economic issues between the United States and Belgium. The Secretary underscored the importance of cooperating to counter the financing of terrorism, including Iran’s support to terrorist groups.
