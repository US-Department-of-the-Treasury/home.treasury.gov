**BALI** – Last week, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Canadian Minister of Finance Bill Morneau. Secretary Mnuchin and Minister Morneau reaffirmed the importance of the U.S.-Canada economic relationship, and the importance of cooperation on a range of issues under Canada’s G-7 chairmanship. They also discussed the importance of intensifying pressure on the Maduro regime in Venezuela.
