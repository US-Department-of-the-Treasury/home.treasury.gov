Washington – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Canadian Finance Minister Bill Morneau. Secretary Mnuchin and Minister Morneau reaffirmed the strong economic and trade relationship between the United States and Canada and discussed continued efforts to reach a multilateral agreement on issues associated with international taxation. Other topics discussed include the 2020 United States presidency of the G7, and the United States-Mexico-Canada Trade Agreement.
