**WASHINGTON** – Yesterday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin met with European Commission Vice President Valdis Dombrovskis.  Secretary Mnuchin and Vice President Dombrovskis discussed financial regulatory issues and cooperation between the United States and the European Commission.
