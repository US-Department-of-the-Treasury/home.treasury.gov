**BUENOS AIRES** – On Saturday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with South Korean Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Strategy and Finance Dong Yeon Kim. Secretary Mnuchin and Deputy Prime Minister Kim reaffirmed the strong economic relationship between the United States and South Korea, and emphasized ongoing efforts between the United States and South Korea on security issues of mutual concern.  The Secretary and Deputy Prime Minister also discussed their shared commitment to achieving strong and broad-based economic growth, trade, and security.
