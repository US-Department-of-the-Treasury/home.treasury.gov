**Washington **– U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Finance Minister Xiao Jie of China. The Secretary and Minister discussed bilateral economic issues, including the need for a more balanced and reciprocal trade and investment relationship between the United States and China. The Secretary underscored the importance of cooperation between the two nations as it relates to North Korea nuclear threat.
