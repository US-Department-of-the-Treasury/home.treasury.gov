WASHINGTON – Today, Secretary of the Treasury Scott K.H. Bessent met with Secretary of Finance and Public Credit Edgar Amador Zamora of Mexico. The Secretary urged the Minister to maintain positive momentum on implementation of important reforms to disrupt financing to cartels and reiterated the United States’ willingness to support Mexico’s development of a national-security focused investment screening mechanism.
