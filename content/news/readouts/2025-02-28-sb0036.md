WASHINGTON – Secretary of the Treasury Scott Bessent spoke with Qatari Finance Minister Ali bin Ahmed al Kuwari earlier today.  Secretary Bessent underscored the strong economic, investment, and national security ties between Qatar and the United States, which he hopes to build on during his tenure.  He also discussed regional developments and raised the importance of cooperation to curtail Iran’s nuclear and ballistic missile efforts, as well as its support for terrorists that destabilize the region.
