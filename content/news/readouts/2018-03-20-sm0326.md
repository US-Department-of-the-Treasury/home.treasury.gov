**Buenos Aires**– Yesterday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Finance Minister Olaf Scholz of Germany. Secretary Mnuchin congratulated Minister Scholz on his recent appointment as Finance Minister. Secretary Mnuchin and Minister Scholz discussed opportunities to work together on shared economic interests between the United States and Germany. Secretary Mnuchin underscored the importance of the United States and Germany working together to address North Korea and Iran's illicit financial activity and counter the financing of terrorism.
