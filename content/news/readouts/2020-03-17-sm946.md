WASHINGTON – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin held a call with Australian Treasurer Josh Frydenberg on Monday, March 16, to discuss the economic impact of the global coronavirus outbreak. They shared updates and discussed policy measures to support the economy, and agreed to stay in regular contact, both on a bilateral basis and through regular discussions among the G20, as the situation evolves.
