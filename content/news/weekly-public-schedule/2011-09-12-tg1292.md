**WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that, at the invitation of the Polish government, Treasury Secretary Tim Geithner will travel to Wroclaw, Poland to attend a meeting of Europe’s Economic and Financial Affairs Council (ECOFIN) and the Eurofi Financial Forum on Friday, September 16, 2011. As the United States works to boost growth and employment through the President’s American Jobs Act, the Secretary will discuss with his European counterparts their efforts to contribute to global economic recovery and our continuing cooperation on financial regulatory reform. Currently, all of the Secretary’s meetings in Poland are closed press. Additional details will be announced in the coming days.
