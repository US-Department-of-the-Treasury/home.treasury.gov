**WASHINGTON –**The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Under Secretary for International Affairs Lael Brainard will travel to Moscow, Russia to attend the G-20 Meeting of Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors on February 15-16, 2013. The Under Secretary will meet with international counterparts to discuss measures to promote global economic stability and growth. Additional details regarding Under Secretary Brainard’s schedule will be made available over the coming days.



If you are planning to attend the G-20, please email Holly Shulman at[holly.shulman@treasury.gov](mailto:holly.shulman@treasury.gov)with your contact information so that Treasury can provide updates on the Under Secretary’s schedule as needed.



MEDIA CREDENTIALS: Online registration is available at[http://form.g20org.ru/](http://form.g20org.ru/).Please note: the deadline for registration was February 7, 2013.
