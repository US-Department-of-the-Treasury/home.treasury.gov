**Washington, DC--**Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson will swear in the Department's General Counsel Bob Hoyt at a ceremony in Treasury's Cash Room on Friday, January 5 at 4:00p.m.Treasury's General Counsel serves as the chief law officer of the Department of the Treasury and a senior policy advisor to the Secretary. In addition, he is responsible for the supervision of approximately 2,000 lawyers within the Department's legal division.Media without Treasury press credentials should contact Brittni Aldridge at (202) 622-2960, or [Brittni.Aldridge@treasury.gov](mailto:Brittni.Aldridge@treasury.gov) with the following information: full name, Social Security number, and date of birth.
