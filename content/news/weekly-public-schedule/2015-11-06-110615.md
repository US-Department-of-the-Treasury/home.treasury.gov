**WASHINGTON **– The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Acting Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence Adam J. Szubin will visit China, South Korea, and Japan from November 10-13, 2015.  Acting Under Secretary Szubin will meet with senior government officials and business leaders to discuss key national security issues.  During the trip, he will emphasize the United States’ commitment to the successful implementation of the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action as well as our continued efforts to combat Iran’s support for terrorism and other destabilizing activity.  Acting Under Secretary Szubin will also raise other areas of mutual concern, including efforts to counter the financing of terrorism and North Korea’s illicit and provocative activity. ###
