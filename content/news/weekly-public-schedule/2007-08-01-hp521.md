**UPDATE **



Washington --Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will travel to Billings, Montana on Tuesday, August 7 to discuss the importance of trade and the economy in rural America.While in Billings, the Secretary will participate in a public forum on keeping rural areas competitive in the global economy.  He will also tour MRL Equipment, a local manufacturer focused on domestic and international production of pavement marking application and removal equipment, and give remarks to MRL employees.
