   **WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury announced today that Under Secretary for International Affairs Lael Brainard will travel to Beijing, China on June 26-27, 2013 for meetings with China’s senior economic officials as part of the Administration’s continuing bilateral engagement on economic issues of importance. While there, Under Secretary Brainard will discuss with her counterparts preparations for the upcoming meeting of the U.S.-China Strategic & Economic Dialogue, which will be held in Washington, D.C. during the week of July 8.
