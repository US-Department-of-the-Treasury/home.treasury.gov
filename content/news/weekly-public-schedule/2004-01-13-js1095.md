*(Archived Content)*

  
  

Treasury Assistant Secretary for Tax Policy Pam Olson will hold a briefing onTuesday, January 13, 2004at10:00 amin room 3327 (Large Conference Room). This briefing will provide information on new proposals that will be included in the President’s FY ’05 Budget to close loopholes, improve compliance, end several abusive tax avoidance transactions, and simplify the tax code.

  

Media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-1999.
