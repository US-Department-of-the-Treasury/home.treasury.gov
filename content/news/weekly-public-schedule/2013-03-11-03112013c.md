**** MEDIA ADVISORY**  **
 
**** 
 
**WASHINGTON –**On Wednesday, March 13, 2013, the Department of the Treasury’s Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance (FACI) will convene in open session.  The meeting will include a discussion on Superstorm Sandy insurance matters, affordability and accessibility of non-health insurance products to traditionally underserved communities and an update on the international work streams, including work with the International Association of Insurance Supervisors (IAIS) and on the EU-U.S. Insurance Dialogue.  Members of the FACI plan to discuss these and other topics in its mission to provide advice and recommendations to the Federal Insurance Office.  The meeting will also be streamed live by webcast.
 
**WHO:**
 
- Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance
- Federal Insurance Office
 
**WHEN:**
 
Wednesday, March 13, 2013
 
9:00 AM EDT 
 
 
 
**WHERE:**
 
U.S. Department of the Treasury
 
Media Room 4121
 
1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW
 
Washington, D.C.
 
 
 
This event is open to the press.  A live webcast of the open session will be available at [https://home.treasury.gov/news/webcasts](https://home.treasury.gov/news/webcasts). 
 
Media without Treasury press credentials planning to attend should email Ronda Buckmon at [Ronda.Buckmon@treasury.gov](mailto:Ronda.Buckmon@treasury.gov) with the following information: full legal name, Social Security number, date of birth, and country of citizenship.  **The deadline to RSVP is TUESDAY, MARCH 12 at 4:00 PM. **
