​**WASHINGTON –** The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Secretary Jacob J. Lew will travel to Seattle before attending the G-20 Leaders’ Meeting in Brisbane, Australia, departing Washington on November 11, 2014.On November 12, 2014, while in Seattle, Secretary Lew will deliver remarks at the World Affairs Council on the state of the global economy and what can be done to strengthen global economic growth.  On November 14-16, 2014, Secretary Lew will attend meetings of the G-20 Leaders’ Summit in Brisbane, Australia. The Secretary will depart Australia for the United States on the evening of November 16.Further information regarding the Secretary Lew’s schedule will be made available in the days ahead.If you are planning to attend the G-20 Summit in Brisbane, please email Holly Shulman at [Holly.Shulman@treasury.gov](mailto:Holly.Shulman@treasury.gov) with your travel dates and contact information, so that Treasury can communicate logistical updates regarding the Secretary’s schedule as necessary.The deadline to apply for media accreditation for the G-20 has passed. Additional information is available at  [https://www.g20.org/accreditation/media_registration](https://www.g20.org/accreditation/media_registration).
