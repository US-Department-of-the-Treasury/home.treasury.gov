**WASHINGTON **– The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Under Secretary for International Affairs Nathan Sheets will travel to Paris, France; Athens, Greece; and Rome, Italy from May 10-12, 2016. On May 10 in Paris, Under Secretary Sheets will meet with government officials to discuss recent developments in the global economy and financial system. On May 11 in Athens, Under Secretary Sheets will meet with government officials and private sector participants to discuss the latest developments in concluding the first review of Greece’s reform program and the state of play in discussions with Europe for meaningful debt relief. On May 12 in Rome, Under Secretary Sheets will meet with government officials and private sector leaders to discuss economic and financial sector developments. # # #
