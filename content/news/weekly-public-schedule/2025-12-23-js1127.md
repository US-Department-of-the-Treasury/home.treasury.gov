Treasury Assistant Secretary for Tax Policy Pam Olson will hold the "Blue Book" technical background briefing on the tax proposals in the President's FY 2005 budget on Monday, February 2, 2004 at 12:00 p.m. in room 4121 (Treasury's media room). This session will provide a synopsis of the tax proposals and will also allow for a Question and Answer session with Tax Policy staff. No cameras will be admitted-- this is a "pen and pad" only briefing.
 
Media without Treasury press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-1999.
