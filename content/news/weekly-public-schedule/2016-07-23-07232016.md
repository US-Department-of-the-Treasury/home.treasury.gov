**CHENGDU, CHINA** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Secretary Jacob J. Lew will hold a press conference at the conclusion of the G-20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors Meeting in Chengdu, China. **WHO: **           U.S. Treasury Secretary Lew **WHAT:**G-20 Press Conference **WHEN:**Sunday, July 24, 2016   3:15pm CST** **WHERE:       **ShuShan Room   Third Floor InterContinental Century, City Convention Centre Chengdu, China **NOTE: The U.S. press conference will begin following the conclusion of the G-20 Host’s press conference.   For additional media inquiries, please contact Whitney Smith at [whitney.smith@treasury.gov](mailto:whitney.smith@treasury.gov).
