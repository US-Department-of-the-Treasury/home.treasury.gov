**** **WASHINGTON **– The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Under Secretary for International Affairs Nathan Sheets is traveling to London, England and Frankfurt, Germany October 21-23, 2015.  On October 21 in London, Under Secretary Sheets will discuss developments in the United States, United Kingdom, euro area, and global economy with his counterparts at HM Treasury and meet with members of the business community.  On October 22 in London, Under Secretary Sheets will deliver remarks on China at a conference hosted by the Royal Institute of International Affairs.  This event will be held under the Chatham House Rule.  On October 23 in Frankfurt, Under Secretary Sheets will meet with European Central Bank officials and business leaders to discuss developments and risks to the outlook in the euro area and Greece, as well as the United States and global economy. ###
