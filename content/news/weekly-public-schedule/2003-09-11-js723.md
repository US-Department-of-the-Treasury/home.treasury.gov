FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

JS-723

**MEDIA ADVISORY** Treasury Secretary John Snow will hold a press conference today in advance of his trip to Israel, Saudi Arabia, Afghanistan, Pakistan and UAE.  The press conference will be held at 2:30 PM at the Treasury Building.  Secretary Snow will make an opening statement and then be available for questions. 

Please click on the attached link to read the announcement outlining the Secretary's trip[treas.gov/press/releases/js720.htm](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/js720)



Members of the media who do not hold either Treasury or White House credentials should contact Frances Anderson at 202-622-2960 for clearance into the Treasury Building as soon as possible.  Late requests will not be accepted.
