U.S. Treasury Under Secretary for Domestic Finance will give luncheon remarks before the Exchequer Club in Washington, D.C. Wednesday.  The Under Secretary will discuss Treasury's domestic finance agenda, including capital markets competitiveness, government sponsored enterprise reform and other issues critical to U.S. capital markets, financial institutions and federal finance.
