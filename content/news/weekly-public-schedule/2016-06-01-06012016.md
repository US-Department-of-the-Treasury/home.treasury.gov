**WASHINGTON** – On Sunday, June 5th, U.S. Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew will participate in a discussion on U.S.-China economic relations and the 2016 U.S.-China Strategic and Economic Dialogue at Tsinghua University School of Economics and Management.  Secretary Lew will discuss key issues on the U.S.-China bilateral economic agenda, including bilateral trade and investment and China’s economic and financial sector reforms, as well as highlight the Strategic and Economic Dialogue as an important mechanism for managing and building a durable and constructive relationship between the United States and China.  The discussion will be moderated by China Business News anchor Yanqing Yang. **WHO**:   U.S. Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew     China Business News Anchor Yanqing Yang **WHAT**:  Discussion on the 2016 U.S.-China S&ED and U.S.-China Bilateral Economic Relations **WHEN**:Sunday, June 5, 2016 4:00 p.m. CST / 4:00 a.m. EDT **WHERE**:        Tsinghua University School of Economics and Management Auditorium   Tsinghua Xi Lu, Haidian District    Beijing, China **DETAILS**:     Media interested in covering this event must register with the United States Embassy, Beijing Public Affairs Office by Friday, June 3, at 12:00 p.m. CST / 12:00 a.m. EDT.  Please email the registration form below to [SunRX@state.gov](mailto:SunRX@state.gov) or fax to 8531-4040.
