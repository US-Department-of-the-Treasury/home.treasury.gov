**** MEDIA ADVISORY****



**WASHINGTON – **On Wednesday, November 14, 2012, the U.S. Department of the Treasury’s Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance (FACI) will convene in open session. The meeting will include a discussion about flood insurance in the aftermath of Hurricane Sandy, an update on the Federal Insurance Office’s (FIO) engagement with the International Association of Insurance Supervisors (IAIS), and subcommittee updates, among other topics. Michael T. McRaith, Director of Treasury’s FIO, will lead the meeting, which will also be streamed live by webcast.



**WHO:**


- Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance
- Federal Insurance Office


**WHEN:**



Wednesday, November 14, 2012



1:30 PM



**WHERE:**



U.S. Department of the TreasuryCash Room1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NWWashington, D.C.



This event is open to the press.  A live webcast of the open session will be available at[http://www.home.treasury.gov/news/webcasts-center/Video-Audio-Webcasts/Pages/Webcasts.aspx](https://home.treasury.gov/news/webcasts).



Media without Treasury press credentials planning to attend should email Stephanie Ma at[Stephanie.Ma@treasury.gov](mailto:Stephanie.Ma@treasury.gov)with the following information: full legal name, Social Security number, date of birth, and country of citizenship.  The deadline to RSVP is TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 13 at 12:00 PM.
