  **** MEDIA ADVISORY** **   **WASHINGTON – **On Wednesday, June 12, 2013, the Department of the Treasury’s Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance (FACI) will convene in open session. The Committee Members will hear a presentation from the Hurricane Sandy Rebuilding Task Force, and discuss regulatory developments relating to reinsurance captives and the impact of global demographics on the insurance industry. Members of the FACI plan to discuss these and other topics in its mission to provide advice and recommendations to the Federal Insurance Office. The meeting will also be streamed live by webcast. **WHO:** 
- Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance
- Federal Insurance Office
 **WHEN:** Wednesday, June 12, 2013 1:30 PM EDT   **WHERE:** U.S. Department of the Treasury Cash Room 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW Washington, DC   This event is open to the press.  A live webcast of the open session will be available at [https://home.treasury.gov/news/webcasts](https://home.treasury.gov/news/webcasts). All interested media must RSVP by 12:00 p.m. on Tuesday, June 11 to Ronda Buckmon at [Ronda.Buckmon@treasury.gov](mailto:Ronda.Buckmon@treasury.gov).  Media without press credentials issued by Treasury must provide Ms. Buckmon with the following information for access into the building: full legal name, date of birth, Social Security number and country of citizenship.    ###​
