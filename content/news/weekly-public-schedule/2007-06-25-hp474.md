U.S. Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will swear in Assistant Secretary for Financial Institutions David G. Nason Thursday, June 28 at the Treasury Department.  Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Robert K. Steel will also deliver remarks at the ceremony. The following event is open to credentialed media:
