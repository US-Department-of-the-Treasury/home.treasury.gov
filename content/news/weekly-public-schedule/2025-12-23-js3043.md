Treasury's Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education Dan Iannicola, Jr. will teach a lesson on savings to second graders at Greenbriar East Elementary School in Farifax, Virginia next week.  The students at Greenbriar East Elementary School have been participating in an economics class since September, where they have learned about money, spending and saving.
 
This event is open to the press:
 
**WHEN**: December 14, 2005 
 
**WHERE: ** Greenbriar East Elementary School        13006 Point Pleasant Drive                  Fairfax, VA  22033
 
**TIME: **     10 a.m. (EST)
