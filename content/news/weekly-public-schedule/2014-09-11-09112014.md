​**WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew will travel to Cairns, Australia to attend the G-20 Meeting of Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors on September 19-21, 2014.  Secretary Lew will meet with his international counterparts to discuss measures to promote global demand and growth. The Secretary will depart Washington for his trip on September 16. 

Additional details regarding Secretary Lew’s schedule will be made available over the coming days.     If you are planning to attend, please email Holly Shulman at [Holly.Shulman@treasury.gov](mailto:Holly.Shulman@treasury.gov) with your likely travel dates and contact information, so that Treasury can communicate logistical updates as necessary. **MEDIA CREDENTIALS:** The deadline for online registration for the G-20 was September 8, 2014. For additional information, please visit [https://www.g20.org/accreditation/media_registration](https://www.g20.org/accreditation/media_registration). ###
