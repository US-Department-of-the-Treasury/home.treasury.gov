**FINANCIAL STABILITY OVERSIGHT COUNCIL TO MEET MAY 7**



**WASHINGTON**– On Wednesday, May 7, Secretary Jacob J. Lew will preside over an open session of the Financial Stability Oversight Council. The Council will vote on its annual report, which will describe the activities of the Council, outline significant financial market and regulatory developments, analyze potential emerging threats to financial stability, and make relevant recommendations.  In addition, the Council will consider adopting enhancements to its existing transparency policy and bylaws for its Deputies Committee.
