**WASHINGTON –** The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Treasury Secretary Tim Geithner will attend the World Economic Forum Annual Meeting in Davos-Klosters, Switzerland, January 26-27, 2012. While there, the Secretary will meet with his international counterparts to discuss the global economic outlook and Europe’s strategy to reinforce the institutions of the euro area. Additional details regarding Secretary Geithner’s schedule will be available in the days ahead. PLEASE NOTE: Media accreditation for the meeting is closed. The deadline for applications was November 4, 2011. For more information, please visit [http://www.weforum.org/press](http://www.weforum.org/press).  ###​
