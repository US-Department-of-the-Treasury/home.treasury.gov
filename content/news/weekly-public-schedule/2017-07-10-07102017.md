#### What

Opening Remarks and Closing Press Conference as part of U.S. – China Comprehensive Economic Dialogue    

#### When

Wednesday, July 19, 2017; Times: 9:00 a.m. Opening Remarks and 5:00 p.m. Press Conference (subject to change    

#### Where

Opening Remarks at the Department of the Treasury Cash Room Press Conference at White House James A. Brady Press Briefing Room   [To request media credentials, please register here](http://www.cvent.com/d/95qf7s).  The registration site will close on Thursday, July 13 at 5:00pm. Approved media will be notified via email and be able to pick up credentials at the National Press Club on Tuesday, July 18, 2017 at 10am - 12pm and 2pm -4pm.    

#### Why

The U.S.-China Comprehensive Economic Dialogue (CED) was established by President Trump and President Xi in April 2017 to enable the two countries to address and resolve the comprehensive set of economic issues in our relationship, representing the highest-level bilateral economic forum. The dialogue is co-chaired by U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin and U.S. Commerce Secretary Wilbur Ross and Vice Premier of China's State Council Wang Yang.    

#### Details

Media without press credentials issued by Treasury must include the following information for access into the building: (1) full legal name, (2) gender (3) date of birth, (4) Social Security or passport number and (5) country of citizenship. If any information is missing, you cannot enter the building. You must present a U.S. government-issued I.D. or passport with photo at the gate. Media attending with television camera equipment must arrive at the South Entrance (15th Street and Alexander Hamilton Place) no later than 7 a.m.
