​**WASHINGTON **- The U.S. Department of the Treasury announced today that Deputy Secretary Sarah Bloom Raskin will travel to Havana, Cuba on August 14, 2015. Deputy Secretary Raskin will accompany Secretary of State John Kerry for the opening of U.S. Embassy Havana. While in Havana, she will also meet with senior Cuban government officials. ###
