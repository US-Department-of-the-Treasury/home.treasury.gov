**WASHINGTON –** The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Secretary Jacob J. Lew will travel to Ankara, Turkey for the G-20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors Meeting September 4-5. 

On September 4, Secretary Lew will attend a joint meeting of the G20 Finance and Labor Ministers.  On September 4 and 5, Secretary Lew will attend meetings of the G-20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors.  While in Ankara, Secretary Lew will meet with his international counterparts to discuss measures to promote global growth and financial stability.



Media planning to travel may contact Whitney Smith at[whitney.smith@treasury.gov](mailto:whitney.smith@treasury.gov)for information about logistics.
