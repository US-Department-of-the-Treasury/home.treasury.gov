FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS



JS-143




Treasury Secretary John Snow and Acting Ambassador for the United Kingdom, Tony Brenton will hold a ceremony to exchange of instruments of ratification for the new US-UK tax treaty at 11:00 a.m. EST on Monday, March 31, 2003 in the Treasury Department's Diplomatic Reception Room (Room 3311), 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW.

      

The Room will be available for pre-set at10:00 a.m.

      

Media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-1999.
