- **What: **Pen-and-Pad Background Briefing

- **Where **: Department of the TreasuryMedia Room (4121)1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NWWashington, D.C.
- **Note: **Media without Treasury press credentials should contact Frances Anderson at (202) 622-2960 or[frances.anderson@treasury.gov](mailto:frances.anderson@treasury.gov)with the following information: full name, Social Security number, and date of birth.
