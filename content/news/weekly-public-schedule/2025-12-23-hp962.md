May 2, 2008HP-962
 
 **Treasurer Cabral To Visit Treasury Printing Facility in San Francisco Next Week to Observe Stimulus Checks Rolling off the Presses** 
 
 **Washington, DC**--U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral will tour a Treasury Department printing facility in San Francisco next Thursday to observe the first mass production printing and packaging of the 2008 stimulus checks.  
 
The following event is open to the press: 
 
 **Who**     Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral 
 
 **What **  Facility Tour and Press Availability
 
 **When    **         Thursday, May 8, 11:00 a.m. PDT
 
 **Where **San Francisco Regional Financial Center1650 65th St, Suite A Emeryville, CA 94608
 
 **Note   ** All media should RSVP to Abbie Loftus at (510) 594-7100, or Fay Rurup (510) 594-7330 with the following information: full name, Social Security number, date of birth, position, and organization.
