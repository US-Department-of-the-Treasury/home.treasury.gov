**WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew will travel to the United Kingdom May 10-11, 2013 for the G-7 Meeting of Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors to discuss measures to promote global economic stability and growth.

The Secretary will depart for the United Kingdom on May 9, 2013. 

On May 10, the Secretary will hold meetings in London before traveling to the G-7 Meeting in Buckinghamshire. 
The Secretary will depart the United Kingdom for Washington, DC on May 11, 2013.

The deadline for media credentials for the G-7 Meeting of Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors has passed. For more information regarding the G-7 or this visit to the U.K., please contact [holly.shulman@treasury.gov](mailto:holly.shulman@treasury.gov).   ### ​
