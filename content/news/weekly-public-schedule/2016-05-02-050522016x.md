​ **WASHINGTON** – Tomorrow, May 3, 2016, Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew will deliver remarks at the Access to Capital Conference in Los Angeles hosted by Mayor Eric Garcetti and Councilmember Curren D. Prince, Jr. In recognition of National Small Business Week, Secretary Lew will highlight the contributions small businesses make to our growing economy and share Treasury’s commitment to connecting more small businesses and entrepreneurs to the financial products and services they need to succeed.

The conference will bring together a variety of small business owners for discussions and workshops on how they can access capital, contracting opportunities, and their rights as borrowers. **WHO**:             Jacob J. Lew, Treasury Secretary   Eric Garcetti, Mayor, City of Los Angeles   Curren D.
