**OFFICE OF FINANCIAL RESEARCH HOSTS MEETING OF THE FINANCIAL RESEARCH ADVISORY COMMITTEE**
 
**WASHINGTON** – Tomorrow, August 1, 2013, the Office of Financial Research (OFR) will host a meeting of the Financial Research Advisory Committee, which provides advice to the OFR, informs the OFR’s agendas for research and data, and assists the OFR in fulfilling its mission. During the meeting, OFR Director Richard Berner will give remarks and the committee will present its initial recommendations to the OFR.
