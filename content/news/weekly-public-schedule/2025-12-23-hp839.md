Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will travel to Chicago, Ill. this week to deliver remarks to the Economic Club of Chicago. His remarks will focus on the U.S. economy and capital markets. The following event is open to the media:
 
**What ** Remarks to the Economic Club of Chicago
 
**When ** Thursday, February 28, 8:00 p.m. CST
 
**Where**  Sheraton Hotel301 East North Water StreetChicago, Ill.
