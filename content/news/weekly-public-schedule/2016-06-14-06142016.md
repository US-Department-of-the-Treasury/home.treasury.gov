**WASHINGTON** – On Thursday, June 16th, U.S. Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew will deliver remarks and participate in a discussion on U.S.-China economic relations. Secretary Lew will outline key issues on the U.S.-China bilateral economic agenda, including bilateral trade and investment and China’s economic and financial sector reforms.  Following his remarks, the Secretary will hold a discussion moderated by Greg Ip, Chief Economics Commentator for The Wall Street Journal. **WHO**:   U.S. Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew Greg Ip, Chief Economics Commentator, The Wall Street Journal. **WHAT**:          Remarks and Discussion on U.S.-China Economic Relations **WHEN**:Thursday, June 16, 2016 8:30 AM EDT **WHERE**:        American Enterprise Institute for Public Policy Research 1150 Seventeenth Street, NW Twelfth Floor Washington, DC **DETAILS**:     Media interested in covering this event must register by clicking [here](http://www.aei.org/events/treasury-secretary-jacob-j-lew-on-the-us-china-economic-relationship/?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=fdpevent).  Government Issued Photo IDs are required.
