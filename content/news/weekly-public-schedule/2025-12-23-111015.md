**** ** WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Secretary Jacob J. Lew will travel to Kyiv, Ukraine and the G-20 Leaders Summit in Antalya, Turkey November 13-16, 2015. In Kyiv on November 13-14, Secretary Lew will meet with the Finance Minister and other senior government officials to discuss Ukraine’s ongoing reforms to strengthen economic governance, repair the financial sector, and tackle corruption.  He will also meet with several members of the Rada. In Antalya on November 15-16, Secretary Lew will join President Barack Obama at the G-20 Leaders Summit.   Additional details and information about possible media opportunities will be announced in the coming days.  Media planning to travel may contact Whitney Smith at whitney.smith@treasury.gov for information regarding logistics. ###
