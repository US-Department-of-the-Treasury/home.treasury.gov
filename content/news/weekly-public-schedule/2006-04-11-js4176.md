U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral will travel to Virginia Beach, Virginia Wednesday to discuss how the President's Medicare prescription drug benefit is helping seniors and Americans with disabilities receive the drugs they need at reduced costs.  While in Virginia Beach, Treasurer Cabral will participate in a Press Conference on Virginia Lewin Savings Study.



The following event is open to credentialed media:
