*(Archived Content)*

 
 
Secretary of the Treasury and Managing Trustee John Snow and Commissioner of the Social Security Administration Jo Anne Barnhart will hold a press briefing on the Social Security aspect of the Social Security and Medicare Trustees Reports at 2:00 p.m. EST on Monday, March 17, 2003 in the Treasury Department's Media Room (Room 4121), 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW. 
 
 Following Secretary Snow and Commissioner Barnhart’s remarks (they will not be taking any questions), the Deputy Commissioner of Social Security James B. Lockhart III and other Social Security Administration executives will hold a presentation and technical panel discussion on the 2003 Social Security Trustees Report.
 
 The Room will be available for pre-set at 1:00 p.m.
 
 Media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-1999.
 
 Copies of the 2003 Social Security and Medicare Trustees Report will be available at 2:00 at the briefing.
