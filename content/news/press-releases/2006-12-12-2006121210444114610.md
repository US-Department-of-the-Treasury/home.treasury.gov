Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson will lead a delegation to Beijing this week for the inaugural meeting of the U.S. – China Strategic Economic Dialogue. Secretary Paulson will be joined by Commerce Secretary Carlos Gutierrez, Labor Secretary Elaine Chao, Health and Human Services Secretary Mike Leavitt, Energy Secretary Sam Bodman, U.S. Trade Representative Susan Schwab, EPA Administrator Stephen Johnson, and other Administration officials.  In addition, Federal Reserve Chairman Ben S. Bernanke will join the Strategic Economic Dialogue discussions.
