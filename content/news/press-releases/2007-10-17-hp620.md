**Washington** - U.S. Treasury Department officials and bankers across the country will team up Thursday to promote wise credit habits for U.S. teens as part of the American Bankers Association Education Foundation's annual Get Smart About Credit Day.  Students will participate in lessons on the responsible use of credit and the importance of a positive credit history, as Treasury officials and staff travel to schools nationwide to teach classes with local bankers.Media interested in attending classes with Treasury officials should contact Jennifer Zuccarelli.  The following events are open to the press:Syracuse, N.Y. 8:21 a.m. EDTAnthony W.
