FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS



js-1732

  

Today, the Air Transportation Stabilization Board met to consider the application of United Airlines for a federal loan guarantee.  Treasury could not support the application as presented.  Should United submit an improved application in the coming days, Treasury is open to reconsidering it.
