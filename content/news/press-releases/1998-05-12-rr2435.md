Treasury Secretary Robert E. Rubin will be joined by ranking House Banking Committee Democrat John J. LaFalce (D-NY), and other House Banking Committee Democrats at a press conference today Tuesday, May 12 at 1:00 p.m., in room 2220 of the Rayburn House Office Building.

Secretary Rubin and Banking Committee members will discuss financial modernization legislation (H.R. 10), that is scheduled for floor consideration on Wednesday, May 13.
