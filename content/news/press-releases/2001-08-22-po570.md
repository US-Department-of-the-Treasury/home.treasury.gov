*Treasury Secretary Paul O'Neill made the following statement on the Mid-Session Budget Review:*

Today's report confirms that this year the federal government will enjoy the second largest surplus in U.S. history. And by cutting taxes, we've taken steps to boost our economy and ensure that large federal surpluses continue for years to come, so long as Congress works with the President to rein in wasteful spending.
