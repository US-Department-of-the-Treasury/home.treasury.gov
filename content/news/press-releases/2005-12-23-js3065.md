JS-3065

I applaud the IMF Board's approval of a stand-by arrangement with Iraq today. The IMF staff has done a remarkable job in working with Iraqi officials to accomplish this. This arrangement will underpin economic stability and help lay the foundation for an open and prosperous economy in Iraq.
