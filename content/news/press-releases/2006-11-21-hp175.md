**Washington, DC--Secretary Henry M. Paulson will travel to London next week to deliver remarks at a Confederation of British Industries conference and meet with his counterpart in the U.K. government, Chancellor of the Exchequer Gordon Brown, as well as Bank of England Governor Mervyn King and Financial Services Authority Chairman Callum McCarthy. His remarks at the CBI conference, which will focus on global capital markets and international trade and investment flows, are open to the media: ** Secretary Henry M.
