****



Treasury Secretary John Snow and Barbados Minister of Industry and International Business Dale Marshall today will hold a signing ceremony for a protocol amending the U.S.-Barbados income tax treaty. The ceremony will take place at 4:45 p.m. EST at the Treasury Department, 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW.
