Treasurer Anna Cabral will visit the City University of New York tomorrow  to deliver remarks on the economy.   Our economy is moving in the right direction and there are lots of opportunities, Cabral said.  The U.S. economy has added jobs for 42 straight months – more than 7.5 million since August of 2003.
