U.S. Treasury Secretary John W. Snow will visitPittsburgh,PA,Thursday, August 11 to discuss economic growth and President Bush's efforts to strengthen and preserve the U.S. Social Security system in conjunction with the commemoration of the 70thanniversary of the creation of Social Security this Sunday, August 14.



Secretary Snow will participate in a tour of the SonyTechnologyCenter and a roundtable lunch in Mt. Pleasant, PA, just outside of Pittsburgh.
