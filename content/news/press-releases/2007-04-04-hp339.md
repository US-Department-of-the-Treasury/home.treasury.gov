**WASHINGTON, D.C.--** Meg Lundsager, appointed by President Bush to serve as U.S. Executive Director at the International Monetary Fund, was sworn in today by Deputy Secretary Robert M. Kimmitt. The Senate confirmed Ms. Lundsager on March 29, 2007. Previously, she served as the Alternate U.S. Executive Director at the IMF since 2000. She has worked for the Treasury Department in numerous capacities, including Deputy Assistant Secretary for International Trade and Investment Policy and Director of the Office of Asian and Near East Nations.
