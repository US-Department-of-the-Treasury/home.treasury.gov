Secretary of the Treasury John Snow will be the sole witness on Thursday, October 30th at a hearing before the US Senate Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs.   The Secretary will deliver testimony on the Treasury Department's Report to Congress on International Economic and Exchange Rate Policy.
