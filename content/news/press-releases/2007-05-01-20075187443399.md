  Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will speak at a conference hosted by the Peterson Institute for International Economics  tomorrow on the importance of the U.S.-China economic relationship and both countries' efforts to strengthen that relationship through the U.S.-China Strategic Economic Dialogue. The second meeting of the SED will occur in Washington in May.
