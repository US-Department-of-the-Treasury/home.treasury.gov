Treasury Secretary John W. Snow appointed Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Randal K. Quarles this week to serve as Treasury's designee on the Air Transportation Stabilization Board. Quarles will replace Assistant Secretary for Financial Markets Timothy Bitsberger who resigned from the Treasury Department last month.
