*(Archived Content)*

Secretary John W. Snow today addresses America’s Community Bankers Association Government Affairs Conference in Washington, DC. The Secretary's remarks focus on the economy, winning the financial war on terror and the regulatory structure for government sponsored entities Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac and the Federal Home Loan Banks (FHLBs).
