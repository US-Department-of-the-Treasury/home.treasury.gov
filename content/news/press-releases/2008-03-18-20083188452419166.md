*(Archived Content)*

Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education Dan Iannicola, Jr. delivered remarks today to Native American financial education practitioners and community leaders from tribal nations. Treasury has been active in numerous endeavors aimed at promoting financial literacy, including the Financial Literacy and Education Commission, which coordinates the federal government's financial education efforts and maintains a website full of free financial literacy resources at[www.mymoney.gov](http://www.mymoney.gov/).
