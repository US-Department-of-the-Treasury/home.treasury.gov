Interim Assistant Secretary for Financial Stability Neel Kashkari delivered remarks Monday on the Treasury Department's progress in implementing the Troubled Asset Relief Program. Treasury is implementing its new authorities with one simple goal - to restore capital flows to the consumers and businesses that form the core of our economy, said Kashkari.
