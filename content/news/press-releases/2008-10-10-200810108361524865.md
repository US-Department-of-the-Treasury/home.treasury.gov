Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. hosted a meeting of the G7 last week at the Treasury Department. The Group of Seven Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors includes Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the United Kingdom and the United States. The meeting precedes the fall gatherings of the IMF and World Bank in Washington.
