The G-7 finance ministers issued a statement today in support of Georgia and the international mediation efforts to end the hostilities there. We, the G-7, stand ready to support Georgia in order to promote the continued health of the Georgian economy, maintain confidence in Georgia's financial system and support economic reconstruction, the statement said.
