WASHINGTON – U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen will host a press conference at 8:30am Central European Summer Time on Sunday, July 11 to discuss the G20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors meeting in Venice, Italy. The press conference will take place at the Venice Arsenal. Only accredited members of the press can attend in person. The press conference will be live-streamed by the G20.Secretary Yellen will also discuss U.S. commitment to multilateralism and advance U.S. policy priorities on global tax policy, climate change, an inclusive economic recovery, and global health.

**WHO:**U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen**WHAT:**Press Conference Following the G20 Finance Ministers Meeting**WHEN:**Sunday, July 11, 2021 at 8:30am Central European Summer Time. Media attending in person should be seated in Tesa 100 no later than 8:15am.**WHERE:**Venice Arsenal, Tesa 100 for members of the press accredited by the G20. Unaccredited members of the press and the public can[view the press conference at the G20 livestream](https://stream.lifesizecloud.com/extension/9926131/f86c5b00-d456-4148-8715-f57dad0003a5)here:[https://stream.lifesizecloud.com/extension/9926131/f86c5b00-d456-4148-8715-f57dad0003a5](https://stream.lifesizecloud.com/extension/9926131/f86c5b00-d456-4148-8715-f57dad0003a5).
