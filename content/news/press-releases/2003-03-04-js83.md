FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS



JS-83



We do not intend to comment on daily fluctuations in currency markets.Secretary Snow made the Administration's position on the dollar very clear at his confirmation hearing.He said that we have a consistent policy of favoring a strong dollar, and sound, pro-growth economic policies and a commitment to free and open markets are the foundation for a strong dollar.The Secretary's position hasn't changed. Tony Fratto, Treasury Department spokesman
