Secretary Paulson, IRS Commissioner Everson, Ways and Means Chairman Rangel, Congressman McCrery, and Congressman John Lewis visited a local tax assistance center this week to discuss the earned income tax credit (EITC), which has brought millions of low-income working Americans out of poverty, and to thank the volunteers for assisting the taxpayers.
