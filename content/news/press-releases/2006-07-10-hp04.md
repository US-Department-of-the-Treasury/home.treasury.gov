WASHINGTON - Henry M. Paulson was sworn into office today as the 74th Secretary of the United States Treasury. President George W. Bush joined Paulson for the ceremony in the Treasury Department's historic Cash Room. Chief Justice John Roberts administered the oath of office.

High resolution photo available at[www.treas.gov](https://home.treasury.gov/). Secretary Paulson's bio available at[http://treas.gov/organization/bios/paulson-e.html](http://treas.gov/organization/bios/paulson-e.html).
