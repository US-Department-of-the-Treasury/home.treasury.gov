Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will hold a one-day conference on Thursday, July 26 in Washington, D.C. to examine ways in which our current business tax system affects economic growth and U.S. competitiveness in the global economy.



**Who** Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Robert Steel Assistant Secretary for Tax Policy Eric Solomon Deputy Assistant Secretary for Tax Analysis Robert Carroll Additional Participants TBD



**What** U.S. Business Tax System Competitiveness Conference



**When** Thursday, July 26, 2007 9 a.m.-12:30 p.m. (EDT)



**Where** Plenary Session: Cash Room, Treasury Department Roundtable Discussions: Media Room (4121), Treasury Department



**Note** Media without Treasury press credentials should contact Frances Anderson at (202) 622-2960, or [frances.anderson@do.treas.gov](mailto:frances.anderson@do.treas.gov) with the following information: full name, Social Security number and date of birth.
