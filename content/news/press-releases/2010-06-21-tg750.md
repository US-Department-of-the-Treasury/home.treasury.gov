**WASHINGTON** –U.S Housing and Urban Development (HUD) Secretary Shaun Donovan will hold a reporter's briefing conference call in conjunction with the U.S. Department of the Treasury on**Monday, June 21, 2010 at 11: 00 a.m.**to introduce a monthly scorecard on the nation's housing market. The scorecard , will incorporate key housing market indicators and highlight the Administration's unprecedented housing recovery efforts, including borrowers assisted through the Federal Housing Administration (FHA) and the Home Affordable Modification ProgramSM(HAMPSM).**WHO**: HUD Secretary Shaun DonovanHUD Assistant Secretary Raphael BosticTreasury Assistant Secretary Herb AllisonBill Apgar, Senior Advisor for Mortgage Finance, HUDPhyllis Caldwell, Chief, Treasury Home Preservation Office**WHAT**: Reporter's briefing conference call**WHEN:**Monday, June 21, 2010 at 11:00 a.m.

To RSVP, please contact Ashley Gammon at (202) 320-3709 or[Ashley.D.Gammon@hud.gov](mailto:Ashley.D.Gammon@hud.gov).
