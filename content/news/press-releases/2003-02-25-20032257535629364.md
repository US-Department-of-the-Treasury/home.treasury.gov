This evening (Tuesday), at 9:00pm eastern, Treasury Secretary John Snow and Commerce Secretary Don Evans will join CNBC regulars Larry Kudlow, Steve Liesman, Alan Murray and an assembled audience of business school students, investment club members, economists and academics - for a one hour town hall meeting to discuss President Bush's jobs and growth plan.
