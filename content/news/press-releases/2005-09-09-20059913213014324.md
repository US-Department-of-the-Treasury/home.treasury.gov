Treasury Secretary John Snow is in theGulfCoastregion today with Commerce Secretary Carlos Gutierrez and Labor Secretary Elaine Chao. The group is visiting with hurricane victims, government relief workers and local and state leaders to discuss economic relief efforts and future plans for rebuilding in the hurricane-ravaged region.
