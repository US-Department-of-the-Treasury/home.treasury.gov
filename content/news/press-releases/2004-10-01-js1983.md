They discussed the global economic outlook, macroeconomic policies in G-7 economies and the Chinese economic situation in a candid way. Among other things, they exchanged views on economic impact of oil prices, fiscal and monetary policies in G-7 economies, Asian economic outlook, and exchange rate flexibility.
