Washington, D.C.--Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson will deliver his first speech as Secretary in New York next week, on Tuesday, August 1, at the Columbia Business School. The speech will focus on the outlook and challenges for the U.S. and global economies.

While in New York, the Secretary will also visit the New York Stock Exchange and NASDAQ's Stock Market. He will meet with business leaders at both stops to discuss current economic conditions.
