WASHINGTON — Earlier today, Secretary of the Treasury Janet L. Yellen spoke with Japanese Finance Minister Shunichi Suzuki.  Secretary Yellen shared her perspectives on developments in the U.S. economy and welcomed ongoing collaboration between the United States and Japan across a range of common priorities in the Indo Pacific and globally.  The Secretary looks forward to continuing to strengthen the bilateral relationship and to working closely with Japan to advance shared interests like supporting a broad-based and durable recovery from the pandemic.
