Treasury Secretary John Snow is inRichmond,Virginiatoday to talk about the importance of energy efficiency in the home. Improving the energy-efficiency of our homes is a highly effective ways of reducing our national energy consumption, which is a priority that the President set forth in his State of the Union Address this year, Snow said while visiting the home of Charles and Martha Donato with a weatherization expert from Dominion.
