Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Robert K. Steel attends the Advisory Committee on Economic Inclusion meeting at the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation today. The Advisory Committee was formed to provide advice and recommendations on initiatives to expand access to banking services by underserved populations. Today's meeting will focus on the subprime mortgage market.
