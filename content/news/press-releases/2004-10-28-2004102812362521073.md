Secretary John W. Snow is in Wilkes-Barre, Pennsylvania today to meet with local business leaders and discuss the President's efforts to strengthen the economy and create jobs. The Pennsylvania economy has added 62,100 jobs to the payrolls so far this year – 4,600 in September. That's good news for a lot of Pennsylvania families, he said.
