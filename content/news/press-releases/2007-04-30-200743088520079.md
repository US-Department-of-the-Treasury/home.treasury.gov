Deputy Assistant Secretary for Middle East and Africa Ahmed Saeed will holda press briefing today at theState Department.Saeedwill discuss the upcoming launch of the International Compact withIraq--an initiative co-chaired by the United Nations andIraqto helpIraqbuild a stable and prosperous nation and a self-sustaining economy--as well as the Expanded Ministerial Conference of the Neighbors of Iraq.Both will take place in Sharm El Sheikh,Egypton May 3 and May 4 respectively.
