* Trip to Highlight Importance of Achieving Sustainable Growth, **Combating Illicit Finance** and Enhanced Partnerships to Tackle Global Challenges*

**WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Deputy Treasury Secretary Neal Wolin will travel to Saudi Arabia,the United Arab Emirates (UAE), and Kuwait from February 13 – February 17, 2010.

The Deputy Secretary will meet with senior government officials, regulators, and members of the private sector to discuss a range of issues includingstabilizing and growing regional economies, the development of clean energy jobs and sustainable energy resources, and improving the security of the financial system.On February 14, Deputy Secretary Wolin will participate in a panel hosted by the Jeddah Economic Forum on global economic governance after the financial crisis.

Additional details regarding the Deputy Secretary's schedule will be made available in the days ahead.
