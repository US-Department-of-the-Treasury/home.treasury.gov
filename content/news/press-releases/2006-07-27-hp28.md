U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral will speak before the Texas Association of Mexican-American Chambers of Commerce on Friday at the organization's 31st annual Convention and Business Expo in El Paso, Texas. The Treasurer will discuss issues facing the Hispanic community including economic growth, financial education and immigration reform.
