On the heels of President Bush's address to Wall Street yesterday about corporate accountability and governance, Treasury Secretary Paul O'Neill addressed a group of business leaders in Washington, DC to highlight the President's plan to restore investor confidence in corporate America. This evening, Secretary O'Neill - accompanied by Commerce Secretary Don Evans - will appear on CNBC for one hour to address the President's proposal. Tune in at 7:00 pm eastern.
