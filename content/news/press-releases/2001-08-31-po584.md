*(Archived Content)*

Deputy U.S. Treasury Secretary Ken Dam will travel to India, Korea and Japan on September 11-23. Deputy Secretary Dam will meet with a wide array of senior government officials and private sector political, financial and economic experts to discuss trade, investment and a range of steps supportive of strong economic growth.
