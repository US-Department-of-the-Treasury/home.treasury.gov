*(Archived Content)*






Secretary John Snow will be in Tampa and Miami, Florida on Friday, June 25 to meet with local business leaders and discuss the President's efforts to strengthen the economy and create jobs. As a result of the President's tax reform policies, more than 6.1 million Florida taxpayers will have lower income tax bills in 2004. May employment reports show that 6,800 new jobs were created in Florida.
