In a statement Tuesday morning, Treasury Secretary John Snow said: Today's report on retail sales is yet another unmistakable sign that theU.S.economy is strong, it is heading in the right direction and Americans are confident. With a jump of 2.3 percent for January, this was one of the biggest month-to-month increases in over a decade and a half.
