JS-2131

    Treasury Secretary John W. Snow appointed Assistant Secretary for Financial Markets Timothy S. Bitsberger this week to serve as Treasury's designee on the Air Transportation Stabilization Board. Bitsberger will replace Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Brian C. Roseboro who is resigning from the Treasury Department at the end of the year. ****
