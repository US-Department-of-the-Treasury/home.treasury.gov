**Ambassador Holmer Holds a Press Briefing in Advance of the Upcoming Strategic Economic Dialogue**The U.S. Special Envoy to China and the Strategic Economic Dialogue Alan Holmer held a press briefing today in advance of the second meeting of the U.S.-China Strategic Economic Dialogue. These meetings will continue discussions in areas including policies to address economic imbalances to ensure continued global growth, China's economic development and further integration into the world trading system, greater openness of markets, cooperation on energy security and the environment, and innovation.
