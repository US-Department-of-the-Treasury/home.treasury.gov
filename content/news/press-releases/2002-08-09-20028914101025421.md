On August 13th, Treasury Secretary Paul O'Neill will chair a panel titled, Economic Recovery & Job Creation at the President's Economic Forum inTexas.  The forum will bring together government policy makers, small investors, small business owners, industry experts, teachers, workers, business ethicists, union members, corporate executives, economists, business students, academics, researchers and others to discuss the fundamentals of the economy and the President's agenda to increase economic growth for the future.  O'Neill's panel will discuss the best ways to sustain economic recovery, promote long-term growth and create new jobs.
