U.S. Treasury Secretary John W. Snow will host a meeting of the G-7 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors on Friday, Feb. 6 and Saturday, Feb. 7 in Boca Raton, Florida at the Boca Raton Resort and Club.



Media planning to cover the meeting will be required to register. Information on registration and accommodations is attached below.



Further details regarding the G-7 meeting will be forthcoming. Treasury Public Affairs contacts are Tony Fratto, 202-622-2910, and Betsy Holahan, 202-622-1997
