The Treasury Department today hosted a live webcast to announce its quarterly financing estimates. The webcast, which can be viewed on the Treasury website, featured Deputy Assistant Secretary for Federal Finance Tim Bitsberger. Quarterly refunding documents can be viewed [here.](http://www.treas.gov/offices/domestic-finance/debt-management/quarterly-refunding/)
