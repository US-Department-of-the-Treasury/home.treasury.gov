*(Archived Content)*

The Treasury Department announced today a change in the time of the public meeting of the Bond Market Association Treasury Borrowing Advisory Committee (TBAC) held as part of its regular quarterly refunding operations. The public meeting of the TBAC originally scheduled for Tuesday, January 30 at 9:00 a.m. has been rescheduled for 10:00 a.m. on January 30 at the Treasury Department, 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, N.W., Washington, DC.
