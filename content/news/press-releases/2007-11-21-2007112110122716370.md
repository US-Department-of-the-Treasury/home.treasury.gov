Secretary Paulson will lead the U.S. delegation to China next month for the next meeting of the U.S-China Strategic Economic Dialogue. Paulson will be joined by Commerce Secretary Carlos Gutierrez, Health and Human Services Mike Leavitt, U.S. Trade Representative Susan Schwab, EPA Administrator Stephen Johnson, Acting Agriculture Secretary Chuck Conner, and other Administration officials. The talks will take place December 12-13 and will focus on integrity of trade, balanced economic development, energy conservation, financial sector reform, environmental sustainability, and advancing bilateral investment.
