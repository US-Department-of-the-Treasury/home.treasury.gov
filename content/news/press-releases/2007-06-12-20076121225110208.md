Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. presented the President's Volunteer Service Award to Charles Bruce, Andrea Eva and Dr. Michael Petelle as part of the USA Freedom Corps Presidential Volunteer Service Recognition Program today in Atlanta, Georgia. Both Bruce and Eva have served countless hours at Volunteer Income Tax Assistance sites while Dr. Petelle has dedicated his time to improving the environment.
