FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

LS-784

Today's survey confirms that the Community Reinvestment Act is profitable for America's communities and financial institutions.

CRA lending has dramatically increased in recent years and has played an important role in our national economic prosperity. CRA is also opening new markets for profitable lending by America's financial institutions.

This report is further evidence that CRA creates and strengthens important relationships between financial institutions and the communities they serve.
