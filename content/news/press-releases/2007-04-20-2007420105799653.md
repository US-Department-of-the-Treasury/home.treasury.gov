Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. delivered remarks at the Committee of 100's 16th Annual Conference in New York City this week.  Paulson's remarks focused on the importance of the U.S.-China economic relationship and both countries' efforts to strengthen that relationship through the U.S.-China Strategic Economic Dialogue (SED). The second meeting of the SED will occur in Washington next month.
