Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson visits the Bureau of Engraving and Printing (BEP) for the unveiling of the first paper currency with his signature.  The Secretary toured the final stages of the printing process with employees of the BEP's printing facilities in Washington, D.C.  Secretary Paulson submitted his signature for use on U.S. currency this summer. This week the BEP will produce the first notes with this signature
