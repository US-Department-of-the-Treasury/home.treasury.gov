*(Archived Content)*

John Taylor, Under Secretary of the Treasury for International Affairs, will travel to Tokyo, Japan, October 20-23 for a series of meetings to discuss economic and financial issues.

In meetings with members of Japan’s economic team and other officials, Under Secretary Taylor will discuss Japanese efforts to return to sustained economic growth.  Taylor will also meet with individuals in the private sector including bankers, financial analysts, and business leaders.

Taylor will arrive in Tokyo on Sunday, October 20 and depart on Wednesday, October 23, 2002.

On Tuesday, October 22, Under Secretary Taylor is scheduled to deliver a lunchtime speech at the Japan National Press Center in Tokyo.
