Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. visits Boise, Idaho Friday to celebrate the successful launch of the Idaho state quarter, which has a design featuring the once endangered peregrine falcon. The Idaho quarter is the 43rdquarter in production for the popular 50 State Quarters Program.
