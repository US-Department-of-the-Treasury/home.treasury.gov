**Treasury Officials Speak Out on the Importance of Financial Education**In separate events today in Rhode Island and Louisiana, Treasurer Anna Cabral and Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education Dan Iannicola, Jr. will speak to community leaders about the importance of financial education. This week 13 Treasury officials joined local bankers and students in schools across the country to talk about the importance of savings.
