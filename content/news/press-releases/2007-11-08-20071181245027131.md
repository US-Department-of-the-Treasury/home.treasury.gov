Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. commended the House of Representatives on today's passage of the Peru Free Trade Agreement. This bipartisan consensus follows the call we heard from business leaders at the White House this week highlighting how important these agreements are for continued job creation and protecting U.S. investments.
