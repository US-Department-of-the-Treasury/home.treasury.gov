​    **WASHINGTON  – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Acting Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence Adam J. Szubin will travel to Israel and Ukraine from July 18 through July 21. In Israel, Acting Under Secretary Szubin will meet with government officials and members of the think tank community to discuss** national security priorities, including efforts to combat Hizballah and the implementation of the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action.    In Ukraine, **Acting Under Secretary Szubin** will meet with his international counterparts to underscore the U.S. commitment to maintain sanctions against Russia until it has fulfilled all of its commitments under the Minsk Agreements. **###**
