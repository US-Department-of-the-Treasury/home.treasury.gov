Washington--U.S. Treasury Deputy Secretary Robert M. Kimmitt will deliver a speech next week on the U.S.-Japan economic relationship. The speech, entitled Japan and the United States: Indispensable Partners, in Asia and Beyond, will focus on issues that are critical to the continued success of the U.S.-Japan economic relationship.
