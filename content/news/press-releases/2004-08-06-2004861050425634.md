Assistant Secretary for Financial Institutions, Wayne Abernathy will lead a financial education roundtable at the InCharge Education Foundation in Orlando, Florida.Abernathy will discuss Treasury's financial education initiatives, the role of the new Financial Literacy and Education Commission and how local organizations can contribute to the overall mission of improving financial education.Orlando business, nonprofit, academic and local government representatives will also participate in the forum.

The InCharge Education Foundation is a national non-profit organization specializing in personal finance education and research.The organization promotes financial education through its various programs.
