I am delighted but not surprised by today's GDP announcement, which showed that the American economy grew at a rate of 3.8 percent in the first quarter of this year. This number illustrates that U.S. workers and businesses are producing more goods and services every day, and that is a terrific sign of economic health. This type of expansion means more opportunity for more American workers, particularly those who need jobs.
