Treasury's EagleCash team recently completed travel to Iraq and Kuwait to install smart card kiosks and terminals at 12 Army bases to make deployment life safer and easier. Pictured here, a U.S. soldier uses an EagleCash kiosk in Al Faw Palace, formerly owned by Sadaam Hussein, near Baghdad, Iraq.
