Secretary John Snow and Secretary of State Colin Powell will lead a delegation from the United States to the Forum on the Future meeting to be held in Rabat, Morocco on Saturday, December 11. The meeting will bring together foreign ministry and finance ministry representatives from the region, the G-8, and other interested nations. The Forum will be co-chaired by the United States and Morocco.
