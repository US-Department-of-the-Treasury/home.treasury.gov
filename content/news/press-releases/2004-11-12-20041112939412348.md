Treasury Secretary John W. Snow will travel to four European countries November 14-22 to discuss the state of the global economy and efforts to continue strong global economic growth. Secretary Snow will visit Ireland, the United Kingdom and Poland, as well as participate in the Group of 20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors' meeting in Germany.
