Treasury Secretary Paul H. O'Neill will hold a discussion at the Cleveland Tomorrow Roundtable Breakfast at 7:30 a.m. on Tuesday, October 16, 2001 at the Union Club, 1211 Euclid Avenue, Cleveland, OH 44115.

This breakfast will only be open to the press for the first five minutes of Secretary O'Neill's remarks. For further information, please contact Sean Miles at (202) 622-2960.
