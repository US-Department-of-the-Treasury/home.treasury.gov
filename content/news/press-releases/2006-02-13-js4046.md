U.S. Treasury Secretary Snow will be joined by Senators Allard (R-CO) and Bennett (R UT) for a press conference to discuss the benefits of Congress passing tax relief legislation. The press conference will take place in the Dirksen Senate Office Building on Capitol Hill prior to the Secretary's testimony before the Senate Budget Committee.
