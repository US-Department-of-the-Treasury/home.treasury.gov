**WASHINGTON** – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin spoke by phone today with Dutch Finance Minister and Eurogroup President Jeroen DijsselbloemSecretary Mnuchin looks forward to working closely with Minister Dijsselbloem and with the Netherlands and the Eurogroup on a range of strategic issues.  He welcomes collaborating to bolster stronger and more inclusive growth and strengthening job creation.
