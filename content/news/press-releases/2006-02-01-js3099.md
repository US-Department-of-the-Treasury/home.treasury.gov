U.S. Treasury Secretary John W. Snow will travel to Charlotte, North Carolina Thursday to discuss American competitiveness and the U.S. economy. While in Charlotte, Secretary Snow will visit the North Carolina Research Campus where he will participate in a discussion on the contributions that innovation and technology make to the economy and to standards of living for the American people.
