2005-8-29-17-17-31-324

              

The IRS has announced that it will extend upcoming deadlines for filing and tax payments to October 31 for those affected by Hurricane Katrina.



The IRS has also announced that it will provide a toll-free number to assist taxpayers affected by Hurricane Katrina.
