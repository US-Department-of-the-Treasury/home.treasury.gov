The Treasury Department hosts a meeting of the Financial Literacy and Education Commission today, one of the Department's numerous endeavors aimed at promoting financial literacy. The Commission coordinates the federal government's financial education efforts and maintains a website full of free financial literacy resources at[www.mymoney.gov](http://www.mymoney.gov/).
