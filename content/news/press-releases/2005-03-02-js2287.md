Treasury Secretary John Snow today launched the 60 Stops in 60 Days tour in which Administration officials from President Bush to Cabinet members and policy experts will crisscross the nation to take the President's message of strengthening Social Security to the American people. The blitz is the next stage in the Administration's long term effort to talk about the need for a permanent fix to save Social Security for future generations.
