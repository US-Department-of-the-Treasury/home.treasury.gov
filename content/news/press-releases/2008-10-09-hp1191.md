Treasury Interim Assistant Secretary for Financial Stability Neel Kashkari will deliver remarks Monday before the Institute of International Bankers in Washington.  He will discuss financial markets and Treasury's progress implementing the Emergency Economic Stability Act.



The following event is open to the media:



**Who  **Interim Assistant Secretary for Financial Stability Neel Kashkari



**What  **Remarks on the Implementation of the Emergency Economic Stability Act



**When  **Monday, October 13, 8:00 a.m. EDT



**Where **The Four Seasons HotelSalon A2800 Pennsylvania Avenue, NWWashington, D.C.
