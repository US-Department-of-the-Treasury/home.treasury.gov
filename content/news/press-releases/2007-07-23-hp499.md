Treasury Deputy Assistant Secretary for Tax Analysis Robert Carroll will hold a pen and pad press briefing today on Treasury's Business Taxation and Global Competitiveness Background Paper, which details a number of issues that will be discussed Thursday, July 26 at the Treasury Conference on Business Taxation and Global Competitiveness.
