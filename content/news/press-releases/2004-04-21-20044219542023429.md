Secretary John Snow will visit New York City on Thursday, April 22 to participate in National Teach Children to Save Day. The Secretary will teach a fifth-grade class in East Harlem about the importance of saving for the future and how budgeting can help people achieve their goals. A number of other Treasury officials will teach similar classes in schools across the country.
