Treasury Secretary John W. Snow is in Minneapolis, Minnesota today to meet with local franchise business leaders to discuss the President's efforts to strengthen the economy and create jobs. The President's tax relief is still working for the people of Minnesota, with more than 1.9 million taxpayers saving money on their income tax bills in 2004 and 480,000 business taxpayers like you able to use your 2004 tax savings to invest in business equipment and employee compensation, he said.
