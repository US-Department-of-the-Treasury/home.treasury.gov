*(Archived Content)*

Treasury Secretary John Snow and Health and Human Services Secretary Tommy Thompson are in Manchester, New Hampshire on Tuesday to discuss the Bush Administration's efforts to make health care more accessible and affordable, as well as the impact of health insurance costs on the economy. They are meeting with members of the business and medical communities in a townhall meeting format. Secretary Snow will visit Pennsylvania on Wednesday and Thursday.
