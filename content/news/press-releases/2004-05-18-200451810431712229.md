*(Archived Content)*

Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Brian C. Roseboro testifies today before the Senate Banking Committee on implementation of the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act (TRIA). While we hope that we will never be called upon to trigger coverage under TRIA, the program stands ready today—as it has from its earliest days—to meet its responsibilities, he said.
