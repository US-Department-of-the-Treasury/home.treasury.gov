U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral spoke this week about public-private partnerships helping homeowners facing foreclosure and helping more Americans access mainstream financial institutions. Her remarks were featured at the Financial Literacy and Education Commission's Regional Conference on Reaching Unbanked People.
