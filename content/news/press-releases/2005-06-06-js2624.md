U.S. Treasury Secretary John W. Snow will be in Calgary, Alberta this week for meetings with Canadian Minister of Finance Ralph Goodale. Snow and Goodale will discuss a broad range of global economic issues on which their two governments work closely together. Snow will discuss the health and strength of the U.S. and Canadian economies, and the Bush Administration's commitment to economic growth and prosperity through sound economic policies. Snow will also address energy and its economic implications in the context of the U.S.-Canada bilateral relationship.
