The U.S. Department of Treasury hosted a symposium today on preserving the lower tax rates on dividends and capital gains. The event, Investing in America's Future -- Preserving the Lower Tax Rates on Dividends and Capital Gains, featured several speakers, including Treasury Secretary John Snow, CEA Chairman Ed Lazear, and former CEA Chairman Martin Feldstein.
