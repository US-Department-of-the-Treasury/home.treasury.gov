HP-307
Washington
, DC
--
Secretary Paulson will welcome German Finance Minister Peer Steinbrück to the U.S. Department of Treasury on Wednesday, March 14, 2007. They will discuss
the German proposal for a New Transatlantic Economic Partnership in advance of the U.S.-EU Summit at the
end of April and macroeconomic conditions in Europe and the United States. Additionally, they will discuss the global economy, financial sector issues, and the G-7/8 agenda.
