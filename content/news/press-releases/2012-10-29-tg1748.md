**WASHINGTON** –The U.S. Department of the Treasury today issued the following statement on its offerings for the week ahead:

Treasury's 13- and 26-week bill auctions will close at the regularly scheduled time of 11:30 am today.

In addition, due to the weather forecast for the coming days, Treasury will move up its previously scheduled 4-week bill auction to October 29 from October 30. The 4-week auction will now close at 10:30 am today.
