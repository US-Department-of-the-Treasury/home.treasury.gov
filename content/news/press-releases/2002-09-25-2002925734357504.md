*(Archived Content)*

Treasury Secretary Paul O'Neill returns today from Tennessee and Kentucky where he was discussing President Bush's efforts to strengthen the economic recovery.   Over the past several months, the Secretary has been traveling across the nation to meet with employees and employers as part of an effort to frequently meet with the people who actually make our economy happen.  Next up for the Secretary, the Annual Meetings of the IMF and World Bank, which begin Thursday and end Sunday.
