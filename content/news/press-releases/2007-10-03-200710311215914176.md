Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. hosted Treasury's Annual Iftaar Dinner, honoring Muslim-Americans and thanking them for their contributions to American society, culture and economy. This year's Iftaar dinner was held Oct. 2, and included a small number of invited guests consisting of prominent businessmen and women, community and charity leaders. This country appreciates and honors Muslims throughoutAmerica– for your friendship and contributions to our society, our economy and the enrichment of our culture, Paulson said.
