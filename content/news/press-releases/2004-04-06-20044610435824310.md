*(Archived Content)*





Deputy Secretary Samuel Bodman will join U.S. Mint Director Henrietta Holsman Fore, Florida Governor Jeb Bush and NASA Director Sean O’Keefe on April 7 at theKennedySpaceCenterto unveil the new Florida State Quarter, the 27th quarter of the U.S. Mint’s State Quarters Program. The quarter design features a space shuttle, a 16th century Spanish galleon, palm trees, the date 1845, and the insignia Gateway to Discovery.
