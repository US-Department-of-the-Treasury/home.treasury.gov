Treasury Acting Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence Adam J. Szubin Op-Ed: A Dangerous Shell Game

WASHINGTON – In an op-ed published in the Tuesday print edition of the Hill, Treasury Acting Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence Adam J. Szubin highlights the importance of financial transparency, underscores that protecting the U.S. financial system should not be a bipartisan issue, and urges Congress to act swiftly by passing beneficial ownership legislation.
