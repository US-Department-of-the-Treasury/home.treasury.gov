Treasury Secretary John Snow and Health and Human Services Secretary Tommy Thompson today held a press conference to discuss the Social Security and Medicare Trustees' Reports. This Administration will continue its open and honest discussion of the issues and remains dedicated to working with Congress to take the steps needed to secure the long-term strength of these programs, said Secretary Snow. The press conference was webcast and can be viewed at [www.treasury.gov](http://www.treasury.gov/).
