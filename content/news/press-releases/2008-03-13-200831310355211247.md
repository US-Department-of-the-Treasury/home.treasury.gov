Secretary Paulson delivered remarks today outlining recommendations from the President's Working Group on Financial Markets. The group - which includes Treasury, the Fed, CFTC and SEC - issued a report today on global financial markets with insights on the causes of the recent market turmoil and next steps for mitigating systemic risk, restoring investor confidence and facilitating stable economic growth.
