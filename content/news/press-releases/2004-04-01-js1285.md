*(Archived Content)*

Secretary of the Treasury John Snow will deliver the commencement address at Kenyon College's one hundred seventy-sixth Commencement ceremony on May 22 in Gambier, Ohio.

Born inToledo,Ohio, Secretary Snow attendedKenyonCollegeas a freshman and a sophomore, before transferring to theUniversityofToledowhere he received his Bachelor's degree in 1962.
