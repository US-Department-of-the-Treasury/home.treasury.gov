Treasury Assistant Secretary Clay Lowery will participate today in the 5th U.S.-sub-Saharan Africa Trade and Economic Cooperation Forum, an opportunity to bring together senior U.S. administration officials, African government ministers and hundreds of U.S. and African business and civil society stakeholders in Washington, D.C. to discuss ongoing efforts to reduce barriers to trade, increase exports and international investment, create jobs and expand opportunity for Africans to build better lives. Please visit[http://www.agoa.gov/](http://www.agoa.gov/)for more information.
