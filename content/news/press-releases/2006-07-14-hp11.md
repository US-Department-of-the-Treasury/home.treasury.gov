MigratedTreasury Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Randal K. Quarles will participate in a panel discussion regarding government sponsored enterprises (GSEs) such as Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac in New York City, NY on Wednesday. Office of Federal Housing Enterprise Oversight (OFHEO) Director James Lockhart will also speak on the panel.

The event is open to the media. Members of the press wishing to attend must RSVP with Jennifer Zuccarelli at the Treasury Department.
