U.S. Treasury Assistant Secretary for Economic Policy Phillip Swagel will hold a media briefing to review economic indicators from the last month as well as discuss the state of the U.S. Economy.  The event is open to the media:

 

**Who** U. S. Treasury Assistant Secretary Phillip Swagel **What** Economic Media Briefing **When** Thursday, July 3, 2008, 10:00 a.m. EDT **Where** Treasury DepartmentMedia Room (Room 4121)1500 Pennsylvania Ave, NWWashington, D.C. **Note            Media without Treasury press credentials should contact Frances Anderson at (202) 622-2960, or frances.anderson@treasury.gov, with the following information: full name, social security number, and date of birth.                        -30-**
