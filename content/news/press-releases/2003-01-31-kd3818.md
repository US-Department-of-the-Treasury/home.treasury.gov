*(Archived Content)*

Treasury Assistant Secretary for Tax Policy Pam Olson will hold the “Blue Book” technical background briefing on the President’s tax proposals on Monday, February 3, 2003 at 2:00 p.m. in room 4121 (the new media room). This session will provide a synopsis of the tax proposals and will also allow for a Question and Answer session with Tax Policy staff. No cameras will be admitted-- this is a “pen and pad” only briefing.



The “Blue Book” will be posted on the Internet Monday, February 3rd at 8:00 a.m. at[http://www.treas.gov/offices/tax-policy/library/bluebk03.pdf](http://www.treas.gov/offices/tax-policy/library/bluebk03.pdf).



Media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-1999.
