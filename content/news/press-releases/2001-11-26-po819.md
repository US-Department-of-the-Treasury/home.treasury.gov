Treasury Secretary Paul H. O'Neill will hold the United States-Cayman Islands tax information exchange agreement signing ceremony at 10:00 a.m. EST on Tuesday, November 27, 2001 in the Treasury Department's Diplomatic Reception Room (Room 3311), 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW. Treasury Secretary O'Neill, United Kingdom Ambassador Sir Christopher Meyer and Cayman Island Governor Peter J. Smith CBE will be signing the tax information exchange agreement.

The Room will be available for pre-set at 9:00 a.m.

Media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202-622-2960) with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxes to (202) 622-1999.
