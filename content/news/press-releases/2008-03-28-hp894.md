U.S. Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will deliver remarks at the Treasury Department Monday, March 31. The Secretary will discuss issues relating to financial institutions and financial markets. The event will be available for viewing via webcast at[www.treasury.gov](http://www.treasury.gov/).  The following event is open to press:

Paulson, Jr.**What**Remarks on Financial Market Issues**When**Monday, March 31, 10 a.m. (EDT)**Where**U.S. Treasury DepartmentCash Room1500 Pennsylvania Ave.Washington, D.C.**Note**Media without Treasury press credentials should contact Frances Anderson at (202) 622-2960, or[Frances.Anderson@do.treas.gov](mailto:Frances.Anderson@do.treas.gov)with the following information: full name, Social Security Number and date of birth.
