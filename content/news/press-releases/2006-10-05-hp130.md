U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral will give remarks at the Hispanic Leaders Conference at the Inter-American Development Bank in Washington, D.C. tomorrow.  The Treasurer will discuss the U.S. economy, financial education and Hispanic Heritage Month.

The U.S. Treasurer serves as an adviser to the Secretary of the Treasury on matters relating to currency production and financial literacy. Before taking her office at the Treasury, she served as director of the Smithsonian Institution's Center for Latino Initiatives and as president and CEO of the Hispanic Association on Corporate Responsibility.
