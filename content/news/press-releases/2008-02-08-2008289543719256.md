Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. attends the meeting of the G7 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors in Tokyo this weekend. Their discussions will include current conditions and financial market developments, the international financial institutions, development issues and climate change, said Under Secretary for International Affairs David H. McCormick at a press conference earlier this week.
