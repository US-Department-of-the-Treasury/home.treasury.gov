**Treasury to Host G7 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors April 13**



Secretary Paulson will host a meeting of the G7 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors at the Treasury Department on Friday, April 13. The G7 gathering takes place in advance of the spring meetings of the World Bank and IMF which are scheduled April 14-15 to discuss progress on the work of the Bank and the Fund. Additional details on the meeting of the G7 will be announced at a later date.
