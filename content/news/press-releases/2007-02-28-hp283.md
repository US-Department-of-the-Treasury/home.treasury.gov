U.S. Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr., IRS Commissioner Mark W. Everson, Ways and Means Chairman Charles B. Rangel, Congressman Jim McCrery, and Congressman John Lewis will visit a local tax assistance center tomorrow to discuss the earned income tax credit (EITC), which has brought millions of low-income working Americans out of poverty, and to thank the volunteers for assisting the taxpayers.
