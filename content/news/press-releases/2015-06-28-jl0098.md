WASHINGTON - Secretary Lew spoke by telephone today with Greek Prime Minister Alexis Tsipras on the evolving situation in Greece.  He underscored that it is in the best interests of Greece, Europe, and the global economy to find a sustainable solution that puts Greece on a path toward reform and recovery within the Eurozone.  He noted the importance of  all parties continuing to work to reach a solution, including both a commitment to reforms and a discussion of potential debt relief for Greece, in the run up to the July 5th referendum.  Secretary Lew also stressed the need for Greece to take necessary steps to maintain financial stability in the run up to the referendum, including by working closely with its international partners.  He noted that the United States continues to  monitor the situation closely and offered to stay in close touch in the following days.   # # # * *
