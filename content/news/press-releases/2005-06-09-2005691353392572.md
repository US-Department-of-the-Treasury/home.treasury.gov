FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS



2005-6-9-13-53-39-2572

    

The Treasury Department today announced the designation of Damascus-based SES International Corp., General Zuhayr Shalish and Asif Shalish for their support of Saddam Hussein's fallen regime.Zuhayr and Asif**used SES as a vehicle to put military goods into the hands of Saddam Hussein and his regime, allwhileevadingUN sanctions, said Stuart Levey, the Treasury's Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence.
