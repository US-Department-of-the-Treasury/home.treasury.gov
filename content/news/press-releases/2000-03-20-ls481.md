FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

LS-481

The U.S. Treasury Department anticipates conducting the next stage of its debt buyback program during the second half of April, in accordance with Treasury Secretary Lawrence H. Summers announcement in January.

Treasury will provide additional information concerning the debt buyback program at the Quarterly Refunding announcement in May.
