HP-959

 

**Washington  , DC**--The United States and New Zealand announced today that they plan to begin negotiation of a protocol to amend the bilateral income tax treaty, which entered into force in 1983.  The first round of negotiations is expected to take place beginning June 16 in Washington, D.C.

The Treasury Department welcomes written comments from the public regarding the upcoming negotiations.  Comments should be sent to John Harrington, International Tax Counsel, Room 3054 MainTreasuryBuilding, 1500 Pennsylvania Ave., NW, Washington, DC20220.

Comments also may be sent by fax to (202) 622-0646, or by e-mail to[John.Harrington@treasury.gov](mailto:John.Harrington@treasury.gov).
