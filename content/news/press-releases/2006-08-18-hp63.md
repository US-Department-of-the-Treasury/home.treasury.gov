U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral will visit West Des Moines, Iowa on Monday to participate in Senator Chuck Grassley's 2006 Women's Conference.  The Treasurer will discuss current economic and financial education initiatives at the U.S. Treasury Department.
