Secretary John Snow on Thursday and Friday will meet with local business leaders in Phoenix, Arizona and Albuquerque, New Mexico to discuss the state of the economy and the Administration's policies on growth. He will highlight the importance of tax cut permanency and free trade policies, and how both are critical elements for economic and jobs growth.[Click here for Snow's release](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/js1289)
