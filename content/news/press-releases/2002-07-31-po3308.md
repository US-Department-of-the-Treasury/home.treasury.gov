*(Archived Content)*

On Thursday, August 1, Treasury Secretary Paul O'Neill will host a press conference in advance of his visits to Brazil, Uruguay, and Argentina next week. The press conference will be held at the Department of the Treasury's Diplomatic Reception Room at 2:30 PM.



Members of the media not holding Treasury or White House press credentials should contact the Office of Public Affairs at 202-622-2960 and provide full name, date of birth, and social security number for security clearance to the Treasury Building.
