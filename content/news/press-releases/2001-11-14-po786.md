The United States has worked closely with our allies around the world to ensure coordinated action against terrorist financing. Today the Treasury Department released an update of the global campaign:



Countries with blocking orders in force - 120



Accounts under review in the U.S. - 1086



Al Qaida and Taliban dollars blocked worldwide - over $56 million
