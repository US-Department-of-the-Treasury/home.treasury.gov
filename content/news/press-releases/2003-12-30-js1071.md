*(Archived Content)*

 

The Air Transportation Stabilization Board today announced that World Airways, Inc. has closed on a $30 million loan.  The loan is backed by a $27 million Federal guarantee issued under the Air Transportation Safety and System Stabilization Act and implementing regulations promulgated by the Office of Management and Budget.
