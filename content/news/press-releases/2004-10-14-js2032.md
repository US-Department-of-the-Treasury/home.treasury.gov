JS-2032



The Administration today is releasing the September 2004 Monthly Treasury Statement of Receipts and Outlays of the United States Government[[1]](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/js2032#_ftn1). The statement shows the actual budget totals for the fiscal year that ended September 30, 2004, as follows:


- A deficit of $413 billion;
- total receipts of $1,880 billion; and
- total outlays of $2,292 billion.


[[1]](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/js2032#_ftnref1)The September 2004 Monthly Treasury Statement of Receipts and Outlays of the United States Government containing these results can be found on the Financial Management Service website at: www.fms.treas.gov.
