**Media Contact**Since the introduction of the smaller-size notes in 1929, the signatures of 24 Treasury Secretaries and 15 Treasurers - including Secretary Snow and Treasurer Marin – have appeared on U.S. paper currency.

All media queries should be directed to the Press Office at (202) 622-2960. Only call this number if you are a member of the media.
