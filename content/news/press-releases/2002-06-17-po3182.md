---
title: Treasury Postpones Announcement of June 2-Year Note Auction
date: 2002-06-17
draft: false
url: /news/press-releases/po3182
---
*(Archived Content)*

The Treasury Department is postponing announcement of the June 2-year note, originally scheduled to be announced on June 19, auctioned on June 26, and settled on July 1. Treasury will announce plans for this auction once Treasury is assured that it has sufficient borrowing authority.
