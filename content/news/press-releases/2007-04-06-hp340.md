HP-340

**Job Creation Continues:    Job Growth:**180,000 new jobs created in March. In addition, employment estimates for January and February were revised up, adding 32,000 jobs.Nearly 2 million new jobs have been created over the past 12 months. Since August 2003, 7.8 million jobs have been created – more jobs than all the other major industrialized countries combined. Our economy has added jobs for 43 straight months. Employment has increased in 47 states within the past year.*(Last updated: **April 6, 2007**)*

**Low Unemployment:**4.4% unemployment rate – among lowest readings in 6 years.Unemployment rates have decreased or held steady in 40 states over the past year.*(Last updated: **April 6, 2007**)*
