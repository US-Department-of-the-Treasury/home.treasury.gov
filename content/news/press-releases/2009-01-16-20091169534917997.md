The mission of The Department of the Treasury is to serve the American people and strengthen national security by managing the U.S. Government's finances effectively, promoting economic growth and stability, and ensuring the safety, soundness and security of the U.S. and international financial systems. The Department advises the President on economic and financial issues, encourages sustainable economic growth, and fosters improved governance in financial institutions.
