Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral and Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education Dan Iannicola, Jr. delivered remarks Wednesday at the Financial Literacy and Education Commission National Financial Education Network of State and Local Governments Meeting. The Commission coordinates the federal government's financial education efforts and maintains a website full of free financial literacy resources at[http://www.mymoney.gov](http://www.mymoney.gov/).
