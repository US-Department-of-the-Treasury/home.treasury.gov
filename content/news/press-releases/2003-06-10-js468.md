FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

JS-468


I am saddened to learn of the death of Don Regan.  Don will be remembered as a great Treasury Secretary and an innovative leader of the American business community.  Many at Treasury will miss him, and our thoughts today are with his wife and family.
