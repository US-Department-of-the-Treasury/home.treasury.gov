Deputy Assistant Secretary for Terrorist Financing and Financial Crimes Daniel Glaser testified at a hearing today on U.S. policy options to prevent a nuclear Iran. The international financial system is becoming an increasingly challenging and unfriendly environment for Iran's illicit conduct. But it is important that, along with our international partners, we keep up the pressure, said Glaser.
