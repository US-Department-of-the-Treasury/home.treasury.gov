Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will deliver remarks at the Heritage Foundation in Washington, DC next week. Secretary Paulson's remarks will focus on the U.S.-China long term economic relationship and both countries' efforts to strengthen that relationship through the U.S.-China Strategic Economic Dialogue.

The event is open to credentialed media.
