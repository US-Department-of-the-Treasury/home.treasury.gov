Secretary John W. Snow is in Orlando, Florida today to participate in a financial forum with local business owners and representatives of local universities and financial institutions. Entrepreneurs and small-business owners are at the heart of President Bush's economic policies, he said. The President understands that creating an environment in which they can flourish is the essential ingredient in any recipe for economic growth.
