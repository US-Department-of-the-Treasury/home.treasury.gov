*(Archived Content)*

Treasury Deputy Secretary Samuel Bodman and Jean-David Levitte, French Ambassador to the United States, will hold a ceremony to sign protocols amending the U.S.-France Income Tax and Estate Tax Treaties at3:00 p.m. ESTonWednesday, December 8, 2004in the Treasury Department's Media Room (Room 4121),1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW.

Media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office ofPublic Affairsat (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-1999.
