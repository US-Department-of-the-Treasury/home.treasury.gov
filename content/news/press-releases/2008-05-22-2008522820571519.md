Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. moderated a panel on successful economic transformations in an era of global competitiveness today at the Commerce Department's 2008 National Summit on American Competitiveness in Chicago. The summit brings together business, government and academic leaders to ensure America's economy remains the most competitive in the world.
