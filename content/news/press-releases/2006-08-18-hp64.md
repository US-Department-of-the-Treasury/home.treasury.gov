Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson was joined today by OMB Director Rob Portman and CEA Chairman Edward Lazear for a conference call with reporters following their meeting with the President and his economic team at Camp David this morning. The economic team met Friday to discuss the Administration's agenda to maintain continued and strong economic growth.
