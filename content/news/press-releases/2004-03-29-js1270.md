*(Archived Content)*

Treasury Secretary John Snow will host an event to discuss Treasury's efforts to implement Health Savings Accounts (HSAs) at 10:00 a.m. EST on Tuesday, March 30, 2004 in the Treasury Department's Media Room (room 4121), 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW.

Participants will include:Roy Ramthun, Treasury Senior Advisor on Health InitiativesWilliam F. Sweetnam, Jr., Treasury Benefits Tax CounselMichael D. Parkinson, MD, MPH, Lumenos, Chief Health and Medical Officer

The Room will be available for pre-set at 9:00 a.m.

Media without Treasury press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-1999.
