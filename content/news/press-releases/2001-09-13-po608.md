Treasury Secretary Paul O'Neill, will make a statement to the media at 4:30 p.m. this afternoon to update media on the status of Treasury Operations.



Treasury Secretary Paul O'Neill Thursday, September 13, 2001 4:30 p.m. Treasury Department, Diplomatic Reception Room 1500 Pennsylvania Ave. NW Washington, D. C.

**Note:



Only members of the media holding Treasury or White House security passes will be admitted.**
