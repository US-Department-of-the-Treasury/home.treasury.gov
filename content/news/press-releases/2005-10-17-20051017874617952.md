*(Archived Content)*

Treasury Secretary John Snow visited a local outdoor market in the Chinese village of Mulan, located in the western province of Sichuan.  The visit was part of a week-long visit to China that included visits to Shanghai and Chengdu. In Beijing Secretary Snow participated in meetings of the Group of 20 finance ministers and central bank governors, and the US-China Joint Economic Committee.  The trip concludes on Tuesday. (CREDIT: Tony Fratto, US Treasury)
