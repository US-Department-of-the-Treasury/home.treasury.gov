U.S. Treasury Assistant Secretary for Economic Policy Mark Warshawsky will visit Philadelphia, Pennsylvania to discuss the U.S. economy and President Bush's agenda for continued strong growth and job creation.  While in Philadelphia, Assistant Secretary Warshawsky will make remarks at the University of Pennsylvania Wharton Business School.
