Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. traveled to Russia, Germany and the UK this week to meet with senior government officials and market participants. They discussed the global economy, building partnerships to address environment and energy issues, the need to keep economies around the world open to investment and efforts to protect the global financial system from abuse by Iran.
