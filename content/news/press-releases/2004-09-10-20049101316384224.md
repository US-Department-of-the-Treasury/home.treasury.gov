Treasury Secretary John Snow is in Lima, Ohio today to speak to the National Credit Union Administration meeting of the Ohio credit union community. He will address initiatives that impact Ohio credit unions, the President's economic agenda, and the Administration's efforts to create jobs, curb health insurance costs, reduce frivolous lawsuits, and adopt a national energy policy. The Secretary is joined by House Financial Services Committee Chairman Michael Oxley of Ohio.
