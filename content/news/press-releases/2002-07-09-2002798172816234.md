Treasury Secretary Paul O'Neill appeared on two network morning shows today to highlight remarks President Bush will make in New York this afternoon to restore investor confidence in corporate America. President Bush has been calling for corporate responsibility since January of this year and unveiled a ten point plan in March designed to hold corporate officers accountable, ensure better information for investors, and bolster our auditing and accounting system.
