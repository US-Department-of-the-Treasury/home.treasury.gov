*(Archived Content)*

Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. hosted a meeting of the G-7 Friday at the Treasury Department. The Group of Seven Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors includes Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the United Kingdom and the United States. This meeting precedes the spring gatherings of the IMF and World Bank this weekend in Washington.
