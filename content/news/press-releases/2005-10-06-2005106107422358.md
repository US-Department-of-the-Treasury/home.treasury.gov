In testimony on Capitol Hill this morning, Treasury Secretary John W. Snow emphasized the importance of helping to rebuild the hurricane-ravaged Gulf Coast higher and better while pointing out that It is critically important – and the President has made this clear – that as we help the region, we do so in a fiscally responsible way.
