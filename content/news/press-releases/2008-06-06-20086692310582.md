Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral is leading a U.S. delegation to the 2008 Global Summit of Women in Hanoi, Vietnam. The summit brings together female business, professional, and governmental leaders to promote economic opportunity for women around the world. The 2008 summit, taking place June 5-7, emphasizes the importance or public-private partnerships in advancing the economic status of women. The Treasurer spoke Friday on megatrends affecting the world's economies.
