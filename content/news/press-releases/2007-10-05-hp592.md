Assistant Secretary Phillip Swagel, October 5, 2007

**Job Creation Continues: Job Growth: 110,000 new jobs were created in September, the 49th straight month of job gains. The United States has added 1.6 million jobs in the past 12 months and over 8.4 million jobs since August 2003.
