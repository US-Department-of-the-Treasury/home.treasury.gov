WASHINGTON — U.S. Treasury Deputy Secretary Justin Muzinich has concluded a five-day trip to the Middle East, which included leading the US delegation to the November 22-23 Manama Dialogue in Bahrain, as well as bilateral meetings with counterparts from Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, the UAE, Oman and Qatar.

The meetings focused on continuing to strengthen cooperation in counter terrorism financing, as well as other Treasury priorities.
