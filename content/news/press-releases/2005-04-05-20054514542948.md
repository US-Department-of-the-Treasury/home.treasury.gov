FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS



Secretary John W. Snow participates in Social Security Radio Day today, which is being held in the Treasury Department's historic Cash Room. The Department is hosting 30 radio hosts from across the country as they broadcast live and interview senior Administration officials on the President's efforts to strengthen and preserve Social Security.
