FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

2005-5-23-14-14-47-18651

     

Secretary Snow today spoke to the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants about the importance of strengthening and preserving Social Security. The President's leadership on Social Security is providing our country with a tremendous opportunity to save the program for current and near retirees and improve it for younger generations, he said.
