*(Archived Content)*

Secretary John W. Snow is in the Columbus, OH area today to conduct a roundtable discussion on workforce development and retraining at Hondros College. He also will talk about the President's efforts to strengthen the economy and create jobs. The American workforce must be more flexible than ever before in order to remain competitive, he said. Nothing will have a bigger, more lasting impact on the American economy than educating and preparing America's workforce for the jobs of today and tomorrow.
