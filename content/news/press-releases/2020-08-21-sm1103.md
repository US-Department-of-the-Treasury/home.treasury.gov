**WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today published a fact sheet on Treasury’s role as lender to the United States Postal Service (USPS).Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin serves as the Chairman of the Federal Financing Bank, the sole lender to USPS, and as Chair of the Task Force on the United States Postal System (Task Force).  This fact sheet provides clarifying information on Treasury’s role relative to the USPS.

[View the Fact Sheet on the United States Postal Service](https://home.treasury.gov/system/files/136/US-Treasury-Role-as-Lender-to-US-Postal-Service.pdf)

For more information on the USPS, its history, its role in the U.S. economy, and the Task Force’s full analysis and recommendations for establishing a sustainable business model under which the USPS can continue to provide necessary mail service for all Americans,[view the Task Force report on the USPS](https://home.treasury.gov/system/files/136/USPS_A_Sustainable_Path_Forward_report_12-04-2018.pdf).
