Secretary Snow, along with the rest of the President's economic team, met with President Bush at the President's Crawford, TX, ranch Tuesday to discuss the growing economy and positive new economic indicatorsincluding last Friday's jobs report.With nearly 4 million jobs created since May 2003, solid GDP growth, increased productivity, and low inflation,America's economy today is strong.
