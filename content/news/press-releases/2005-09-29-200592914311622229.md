Secretary John W. Snow met with the President's Working Group to discuss the financial markets, including the effects of Hurricane Katrina yesterday. The President's Working Group is chaired by Treasury Secretary Snow and includes Federal Reserve Chairman Alan Greenspan, Securities and Exchange Commission Chairman Chris Cox, and Commodity Futures Trading Commission Chairman Reuben Jeffery. Pictured are Secretary Snow and SEC Chairman Cox.
