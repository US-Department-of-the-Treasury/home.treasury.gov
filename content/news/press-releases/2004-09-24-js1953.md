*(Archived Content)*

Treasury Deputy Secretary Samuel W. Bodman will be in Wheeling, West Virginia next week to meet with local business leaders and discuss the President's efforts to strengthen the economy and create jobs. Also, as a part of the Department's launch of the newly redesigned $50 bill, the Deputy Secretary will be making one of the first transactions in the U.S. with the new bill in Wheeling on Tuesday. (Additional background on the release of the new $50 can be found athttp://www.moneyfactory.com/newmoney/.)
