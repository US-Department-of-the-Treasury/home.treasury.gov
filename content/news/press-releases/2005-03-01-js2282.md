Good afternoon Chairman Grassley, Ranking Member Baucus, and members of the Committee.  I appreciate the opportunity to discuss the Administration's proposal to reform and strengthen the single employer defined benefit pension system.  In my testimony, I will focus on the proposal's funding rules, in particular, the calculation of the funding targets.
