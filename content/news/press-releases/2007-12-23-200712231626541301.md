U.S. Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. wrapped up the year with a cross-country tour to hear from U.S. homeowners about their mortgage concerns. Secretary Paulson offered advice to struggling homeowners and discussed President Bush's initiative to help more families stay in their homes, including a market-based plan to reach more borrowers through streamlined refinancing and modifications.
