**WASHINGTON** – On Saturday, April 22, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Finance Minister Sri Mulyani of Indonesia.  Secretary Mnuchin looks forward to increasing the bilateral engagement between the two nations.  The Secretary highlighted collaboration on a lasting economic relationship between the U.S. and Indonesia.  The Secretary and Finance Minister also resolved to continue to work together to counter terrorist financing and enforce United Nations Security Council resolutions against North Korea.
