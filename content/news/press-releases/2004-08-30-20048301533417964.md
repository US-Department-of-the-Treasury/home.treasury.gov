Treasury Secretary John Snow heads to Philadelphia, Pennsylvania on Friday, September 3rd for meetings with local business leaders. During his visit, he will discuss the President's economic agenda and specifically address the Bush Administration's efforts to create jobs, curb health insurance costs, reduce frivolous lawsuits, and adopt a national energy policy.
