*(Archived Content)*

Treasury Secretary Paul H. O'Neill will hold a news conference in advance of a trip that will take him first to Romania and then on to Africa with visits to Ghana, South Africa, Uganda and Ethiopia. The news conference will be held in the Treasury Department's Diplomatic Reception Room at 10:30 a.m. on Thursday, May 16, 2002.



Departing Friday, May 17, O'Neill will attend the annual meeting of the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development in Bucharest, Romania. On Monday, May, 20 O'Neill will depart Romania for Africa, returning to Washington on Friday, May 31.
