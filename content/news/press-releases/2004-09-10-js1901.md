Sandra Pedroarias, Director of Outreach for Treasury's Office of Financial Education will participate as a panelist in the National Credit Union Administration's*Access Across America Empowerment Summit*in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Pedroarias will deliver remarks about Treasury's efforts to improve financial literacy across the country and discuss federal financial education resources available to credit unions.
