**Secretary Snow Emphasizes Global Growth in Remarks to Enterprise Conference**Secretary Snow highlighted the importance of promoting global growth and stability, which will be at the top of the G-8 leaders' summit meeting agenda this year, in remarks delivered Monday to the Enterprise Conference in London. The need for increased global economic growth also will be the top agenda item at the upcoming G-7 finance ministers' meeting in Florida on February 6-7.[See the full text of his remarks here.](file:///C:/press/releases/js1115.htm)
