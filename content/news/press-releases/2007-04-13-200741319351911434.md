Secretary Paulson hosted a meeting of the G-7 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors at the Treasury Department  Friday. The G-7 gathering took place in advance of the spring meetings of the World Bank and IMF which are this weekend to discuss progress on the work of the Bank and the Fund.
