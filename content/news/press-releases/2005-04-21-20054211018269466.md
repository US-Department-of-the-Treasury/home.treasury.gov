Secretary John Snow today testified before a House Appropriations Subcommittee on the President's fiscal year 2006 budget for the Treasury Department. The Department's budget reflects the President's top priorities for fiscal year 2006: fighting the financial war on terror while ensuring America's economic strength, and demonstrating the fiscal responsibility necessary to reduce the deficit, he said.
