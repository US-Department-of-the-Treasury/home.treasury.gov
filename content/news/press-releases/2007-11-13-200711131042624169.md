Secretary Paulson is in Cape Town, South Africa where he participated in a signing ceremony for USAID financial support for South African small businesses and delivered remarks at the[U.S.-Africa Business Summit](http://www.africacncl.org/%28lcd4zx5552515uau2qxf1q45%29/CCA_Summits/2007_Summit.asp)organized by the[Corporate Council on Africa](http://www.africacncl.org/About_CCA/index.asp). He will attend the[meeting of G-20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors in Kleinmond, South Africa](http://www.g20.org/)on Saturday. Secretary Paulson met Thursday with students at the Lake Manyara Primary School near Arusha, Tanzania.
