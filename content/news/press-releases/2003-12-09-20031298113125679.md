The Department of the Treasury on Nov. 5 will host a live webcast to announce the quarterly financing statement. The webcast will feature Acting Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Brian Roseboro, Deputy Assistant Secretary for Federal Finance Tim Bitsberger and Jeff Huther, Director of Market Finance. The event will take place in the Treasury Media Room at 10:00 am EST.
