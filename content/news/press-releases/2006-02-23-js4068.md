*(Archived Content)*

U.S. Treasury Secretary John W. Snow will visit Richmond, Virginia Friday, February 24, 2006 to promote new tax credits for home energy efficiency.  While in Richmond, Secretary Snow will visit the home of the Charles Donato family to discuss the availability of new tax credits for homeowners who improve the energy efficiency of their existing homes. The new tax credits are part of President Bush's Energy Policy Act.  The following event is open to credentialed media:
