JS-2983

  U.S. Treasury Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education Dan Iannicola participated in the JumpStart Coalition for Personal Financial Literacy event today in Washington, DC.   The following is an excerpt of Iannicola's remarks:   

For years the JumpStart Coalition has been a pioneer in helpingAmerica's young people improve their financial literacy.  Through state coalitions across the country, JumpStart levers the power of partnerships to do collectively what no one organization could do alone.  On behalf of the Department of Treasury I commend you for your efforts and encourage you to continue your important work.
