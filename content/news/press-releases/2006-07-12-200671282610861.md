Newly sworn-in Treasury Secretary Paulson hit the ground running in his first week in office. In addition to holding in-depth policy briefings with Treasury staff, he conducted extensive outreach to his global  counterparts, Members of Congress on both sides of the aisle, colleagues in the Administration and others.
