Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. hosted the meeting of the G7 at Treasury on Friday. The Group of Seven Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors includes Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the United Kingdom and the United States. This meeting precedes the fall gatherings of the IMF and World Bank this weekend in Washington, DC.
