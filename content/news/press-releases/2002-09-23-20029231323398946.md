Treasury Secretary Paul O'Neill is in Tennessee and Kentucky this week to discuss President Bush's efforts to strengthen the economic recovery. This fall, the Secretary is traveling throughout the nation to meet with employees and employers as part of a broader effort to meet with the people who actually make our economy happen, and to talk with students who are eager to make their contribution as well.
