**WASHINGTON**– The United States will host the upcoming G7 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors meeting by videoconference.  Secretary Mnuchin and Chair Powell are grateful to the city of Philadelphia for its willingness to host the event, assistance in planning, and support in this decision.
