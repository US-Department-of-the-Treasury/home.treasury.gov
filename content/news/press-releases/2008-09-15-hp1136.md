The Treasury Department, in partnership with the Ad Council, will release a new multi-media campaign Tuesday aimed at combating the issue of financial illiteracy among young adults. The campaign includes television, radio and web banners, including a radio spot in Spanish. Department officials will preview the television and radio spots, as well as a new web site providing free financial information and tools.
