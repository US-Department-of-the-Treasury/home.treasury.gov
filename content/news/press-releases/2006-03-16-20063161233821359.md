In a weekly press briefing held by Treasury Assistant Secretary Tony Fratto, Treasury's top terrorism finance official briefed reporters on current and ongoing efforts of the Office of Terrorism and Financial Intelligence (TFI). TFI Under Secretary Stuart Levey cited international cooperation among finance ministries as being both critical and effective since financial institutions are on the front lines of both the war on terror and international efforts to stop nuclear weapons proliferation
