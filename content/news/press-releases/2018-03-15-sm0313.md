**Washington**– *U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Steven T. Mnuchin today issued the following statement on Larry Kudlow’s appointment as Director of the National Economic Council.*



“Larry Kudlow and I worked very closely together during the campaign in developing the President's economic plan. I now look forward to working with him to continue to implement it. He has been a thought leader advancing the pro-growth principles that President Trump has made central to the economic policies of his administration. This has led to a strong economy with renewed optimism among the American people. I am delighted to have Larry join President Trump's economic team and am committed to working together to ensure the continued success of our economy.”
