Fourteen Treasury department officials and staff members volunteered their time Tuesday to teach America's elementary and high school students the importance of saving. This was Treasury's fifth year participating in Teach Children to Save Day, when bankers and Department officials connect with students in classrooms and after-school programs to share real life lessons about money.
