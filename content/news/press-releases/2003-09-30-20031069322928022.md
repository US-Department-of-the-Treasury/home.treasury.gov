Treasury Secretary John Snow will travel to Boston this week for meetings with local business leaders and officials from the mutual fund industry. During his meetings, Secretary Snow will discuss the state of the U.S. economy, global growth prospects, and President Bush's efforts to strengthen the economic recovery and create jobs.
