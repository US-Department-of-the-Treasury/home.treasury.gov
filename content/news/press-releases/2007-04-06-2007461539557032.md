E. Whitney Debevoise, appointed by President Bush to serve as U.S. Executive Director at the World Bank, was sworn in Friday by Dpty Sec Kimmitt. The Senate confirmed Debevoise on March 29. Previously, he served as senior partner at Arnold & Porter where he was the lead partner of the international practice for more than 10 years. In this role, he was heavily involved in foreign investment and sovereign lending, international trade, arbitration, and international law enforcement. He received his BA from Yale University and his JD from Harvard Law School.
