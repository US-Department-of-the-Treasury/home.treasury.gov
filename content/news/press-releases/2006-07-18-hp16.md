HP-16



Treasury International Capital (TIC) data for May are released today and posted on the U.S. Treasury web site ([www.treas.gov/tic](https://www.treasury.gov/resource-center/data-chart-center/tic)).  The next release date, which will report on data for June, is scheduled for August 15, 2006.Net foreign purchases of long-term securities were $69.6 billion.


- Net foreign purchases of long-term domestic securities were $88.8 billion.  Of this, net purchases by foreign official institutions were minus $1.4 billion and net purchases by private foreign investors were 90.2 billion.
- U.S. residents purchased a net $19.2 billion in foreign issued securities.
