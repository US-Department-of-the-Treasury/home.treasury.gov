*(Archived Content)*

Treasury Secretary John Snow and Barbados Minister of Industry and International Business Dale Marshall will hold a signing ceremony for a protocol amending the U.S.-Barbados income tax treaty at 4:45 p.m. EST on Wednesday, July 14, 2004 in the Treasury Department's Media Room (room 4121), 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW.



The Room will be available for pre-set at 4:00 p.m.



Media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-1999.
