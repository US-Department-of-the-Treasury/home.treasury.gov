​**WASHINGTON **– U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Pham Binh Minh of Vietnam. Secretary Mnuchin and Deputy Prime Minister Minh welcomed the opportunity to engage on issues of mutual importance between the U.S. and Vietnam. Secretary Mnuchin indicated support and collaboration for a successful APEC host year in Vietnam in 2017. Secretary asked for the Deputy Prime Minister for Vietnam’s support and cooperation on combating illicit finance.
