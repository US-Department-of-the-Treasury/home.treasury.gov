Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. visits China this week to meet with government officials and discuss the U.S.-China Strategic Economic Dialogue (SED) launched by Presidents Bush and Hu last year. He met with government officials in Beijing today to discuss a broad range of issues that are critical to the U.S.-China economic relationship. Paulson will continue the meetings on Wednesday before returning to the U.S.
