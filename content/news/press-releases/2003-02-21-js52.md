FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

JS-52



We welcome the positive outcome of discussions between Uruguay's authorities and IMF staff and management. We look forward to timely review by the IMF Board of Uruguay's economic program and welcome this important step to restored economic growth.
