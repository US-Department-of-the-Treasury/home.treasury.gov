Treasury Secretary John Snow will visitLima,Ohioon Friday, September 10 to speak to the National Credit Union Administration meeting of theOhiocredit union community.  He will address initiatives that impactOhiocredit unions, the President's economic agenda, and the Administration's efforts to create jobs, curb health insurance costs, reduce frivolous lawsuits, and adopt a national energy policy. The Secretary will be joined by House Financial Services Committee Chairman Michael Oxley ofOhio.
