​

**WASHINGTON** –Treasury today announced that it will delay publication of the semi-annual Report to Congress on International Economic and Exchange Rate Policies of our major trading partners so that progress may be assessed following several upcoming international meetings: the G-20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors Meeting April 19-20; the spring meetings of the International Monetary Fund (IMF) and World Bank April 20-21; and the upcoming U.S.-China Strategic and Economic Dialogue.

 

Treasury last published the semi-annual Report December 27, 2011.
