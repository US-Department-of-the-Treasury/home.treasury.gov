*(Archived Content)*

U.S. Treasury Secretary John Snow departs Tuesday for a seven day trip to Chicago, Illinois, Madrid, Spain and Morelia, Mexico.   In Chicago, the Secretary will tour the  MERC, the CBOT and the CBOE, meet with local CEOs and deliver remarks about the economy.  In Madrid, the Secretary will co-chair with Secretary of State Colin Powell the U.S. delegation attending the Iraq Donors Conference.   In Morelia, the Secretary will attend the G20 meeting.
