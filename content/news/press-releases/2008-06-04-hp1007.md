U.S. Treasury Assistant Secretary for Economic Policy Phillip Swagel will hold a media briefing to review economic indicators from the last month as well as discuss the state of the U.S. Economy.  The event is open to the media:

**Who** U. S. Treasury Assistant Secretary Phillip Swagel **What** Economic Media Briefing **When** Friday, June 6, 2008, 11:00 a.m. EDT **Where** Treasury DepartmentMedia Room (Room 4121)1500 Pennsylvania Ave, NWWashington, D.C. **Note** Media without Treasury press credentials should contact Courtney Forsell  at (202) 622-2960, or[Courtney.Forsell@treasury.gov](mailto:Courtney.Forsell@treasury.gov)with the following information: full name, Social Security number, and date of birth.
