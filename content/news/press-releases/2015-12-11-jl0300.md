​ **WASHINGTON** – The U.S. Department of the Treasury today announced that Under Secretary for International Affairs Nathan Sheets is traveling to Sanya, China and Jakarta, Indonesia on December 13-16, 2015.   On December 13-15 in Sanya, Under Secretary Sheets will attend the G-20 Finance and Central Bank Deputies Meeting, the first G-20 Meeting since China assumed its role as the 2016 G-20 host.   On December 16 in Jakarta, Under Secretary Sheets will discuss the state of the global economy and developments in the United States, Indonesia, and worldwide with government and finance officials at the Coordinating Ministry for Economic Affairs, Financial Services Authority, Bank Indonesia, and the Ministry of Finance.  Under Secretary Sheets will also meet with members of the private sector.   # # #
