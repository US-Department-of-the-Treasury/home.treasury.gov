Under Secretary of the Treasury Jimmy Gurulé will have as press briefing today at4:30 p.m. in the Treasury Department's Diplomatic Reception (Room 3311),1500 Pennsylvania Ave. NW

Media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at 202-622-2960, by 2:00 p.m. today Tuesday, September 18, 2001, with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to 202-622-1999.
