*(Archived Content)*

Secretary Snow Nov. 11--12 will travel to Arizona and New Mexico to meet with local business leaders and discuss the President's efforts to strengthen the economy. He will tour an Intel Corporation facility in Albuquerque and speak to the Chambers of Commerce in both Phoenix and Albuquerque.
