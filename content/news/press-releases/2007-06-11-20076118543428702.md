Treasury Assistant Secretary Anthony W. Ryan will deliver remarks in Chicago, Ill. today before the Managed Funds Association. The Assistant Secretary will discuss hedge funds, systemic risk and how the President's Working Group on Financial Markets Principals and Guidelines for Private Pools of Capital seek to mitigate this risk.
