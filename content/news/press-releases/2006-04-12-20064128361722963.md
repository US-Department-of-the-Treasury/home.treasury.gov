U.S. Treasury Secretary John W. Snow will be inOxford,Mississippitoday to discuss how the President's Medicare prescription drug benefit is helping seniors and Americans with disabilities receive the drugs they need at reduced costs. He will also give a speech on the President's agenda for continued strong growth in the economy and job creation.  While inOxford, Secretary Snow will give remarks to Senior Citizens on Medicare, tour the Lott Leadership Institute with U.S. Senator Trent Lott and teach an Econ – 203 class at theUniversityofMississippi.
