Assistant Secretary for Financial Markets Anthony W. Ryan traveled to Boston Tuesday to give remarks before the Asset Managers Group at the SIFMA Management Conference. Ryan discussed risk management, fiduciary responsibility, and the[Principles and Guidelines for Private Pools of Capital](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/hp272)that the President's Working Group on Financial Markets released in February.
