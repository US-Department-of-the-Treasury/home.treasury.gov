The Treasury Department is continuing comprehensive actions to strengthen public confidence in our financial institutions and restore functioning of our credit markets. Acting Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Anthony Ryan recently delivered remarks on financial markets and the troubled asset relief program and Interim Assistant Secretary for Financial Stability Neel Kashkari testified before the Senate Banking Committee at a hearing on U.S. financial markets.
