**Secretary Paulson Visits Mexico City**



Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will meet with President Calderon, Finance Minister Carstens, and Foreign Affairs Secretary Espinosa today in Mexico City to underscore his support for their strong economic policy agenda. He will also host a conversation with small business owners and finance providers to discuss how to expand opportunity.
