Assistant Secretary for Financial Institutions David Nason and Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education Dan Iannicola, Jr. hosted the 13th meeting of the Financial Literacy and Education Commission Tuesday. The Commission coordinates financial education efforts through the federal government and supports the promotion of financial literacy.
