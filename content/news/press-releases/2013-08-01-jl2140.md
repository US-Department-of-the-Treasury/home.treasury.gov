“Karen Dynan is a world-class economist whose skills and background will be of tremendous benefit to the President and me. Karen has a great wealth of experience—including a long tenure at the Federal Reserve—and shares our commitment to strengthening the middle class. Karen’s ability to sort through complex challenges and develop corresponding practical solutions will be incredibly important to the work we are doing to help accelerate economic growth.”
