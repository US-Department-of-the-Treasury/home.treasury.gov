Deputy Secretary Robert M. Kimmitt delivered remarks today at the opening plenary session of the Palestine Investment Conference. The Deputy Secretary is leading a Presidential Delegation to the conference in Bethlehem. The conference aims to integrate Palestine into the global economy.
