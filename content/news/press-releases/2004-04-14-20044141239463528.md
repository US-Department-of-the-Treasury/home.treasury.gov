Secretary Snow today announced that Ambassador Paul Speltz, currently the U.S. Executive Director to the Asian Development Bank, will also serve as his economic and financial emissary to China. Ambassador Speltz will lead Treasury's ongoing program to strengthen its economic and financial engagement with China.[Click here for Snow's release](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/js1325)
