The fourth Cabinet level meeting of the SED took place this week at the U.S. Naval Academy in Annapolis, Md. The meeting wraps up today with closing statements and a U.S. delegation press conference at the Treasury Department. Today's events will be[webcast](http://www.yorkmedia.com/treasury/webcasts2008.html)here starting at 4:00 p.m. Presidents Bush and Hu established the SED in 2006 to provide a focused and effective framework for addressing economic issues of mutual concern. The SED focuses on a range of issues including financial sector reform and increased currency flexibility, the integrity of trade and product safety, and investment.
