U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral visits Atlanta, Ga. Friday to discuss ways that homeowners can find help if they are struggling to make their mortgage payments. The Treasurer's efforts are part of Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson's initiative to help subprime homeowners stay in their homes.
