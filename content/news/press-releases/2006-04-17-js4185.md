U.S. Treasury Secretary John W. Snow will be in Manchester , NH tomorrow – Tax Day for the New England region – to discuss taxes and the American economy. Secretary Snow will speak to local business owners to talk about the benefits of lower tax rates for all Americans, especially small businesses, and illustrate how President Bush's tax relief has helped the American economy grow and create jobs.

The following event is open to credentialed media:

Who

U.S. Treasury Secretary John W. Snow

What

Remarks to Greater Manchester Chamber of Commerce

When

Tuesday, April 18, 12:00 p.m. (EDT)

Where

The Derryfield Restaurant

625 Mammoth Road

Manchester, NH
