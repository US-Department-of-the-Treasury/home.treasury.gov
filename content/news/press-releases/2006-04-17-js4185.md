U.S. Treasury Secretary John W. Snow will be inManchester,NHtomorrow – Tax Day for theNew Englandregion – to discuss taxes and the American economy. Secretary Snow will speak to local business owners to talk about the benefits of lower tax rates for all Americans, especially small businesses, and illustrate how President Bush's tax relief has helped the American economy grow and create jobs.

The following event is open to credentialed media:

**Who**U.S. Treasury Secretary John W. Snow**What**Remarks to Greater Manchester Chamber of Commerce**When**Tuesday, April 18, 12:00 p.m. (EDT)**Where**The Derryfield Restaurant625 Mammoth RoadManchester, NH
