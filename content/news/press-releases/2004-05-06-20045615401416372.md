*(Archived Content)*

Secretary John Snow on Friday will address the Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago during its annual conference on bank structure and competition. In his remarks, the Secretary will highlight the President's efforts to strengthen the economy. In Illinois, 4.6 million taxpayers will have lower income tax bills in 2004 as a result of the President's tax reform measures.
