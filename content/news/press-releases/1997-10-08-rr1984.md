We welcome the announcement by the government of Indonesia that it is working with theIMF, the World Bank and the Asian Development Bank to develop a program to strengthen thefinancial system and improve the prospects for future growth. We fully suppport theseefforts, and we look forward to a strong response from the International FinancialInstitutions.
