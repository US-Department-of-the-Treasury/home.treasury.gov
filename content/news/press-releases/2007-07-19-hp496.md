Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will host a one-day conference on Thursday, July 26, 2007 in Washington, D.C. to examine ways our current business tax system affects economic growth, job creation and competitiveness.

All conference events will be web cast for public viewing, visit[www.treasury.gov](https://www.treasury.gov/)on the morning of the conference to find the appropriate link.  Following is a schedule of events:
