The Treasury Department is continuing comprehensive actions to strengthen public confidence in our financial institutions and restore functioning of our credit markets. Acting Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Anthony Ryan delivered remarks on financial markets and the troubled asset relief program this week and Interim Assistant Secretary for Financial Stability Neel Kashkari testified last week before the Senate Banking Committee at a hearing on U.S. financial markets.
