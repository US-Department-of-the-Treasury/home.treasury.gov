The Air Transportation Stabilization Board announced that yesterday, Frontier Airlines, Inc. fully repaid the remaining balance of a $70.0 million loan issued on February 14, 2003. The final payment had been due in 2007. The loan was backed by $63.0 million federal guarantee issued under the Air Transportation Safety and System Stabilization Act.
