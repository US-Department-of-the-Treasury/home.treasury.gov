In a speech to PricewaterhouseCoopers' Global Tax Symposium last week, Treasury Secretary John Snow noted that a steady stream of positive indicators has given us increased confidence that the underlying fundamentals of our economy are solid, and that our path of growth is steady. As the President has said, our best economic days are ahead of us.
