U.S. Treasury Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Randal K. Quarles will give remarks at a breakfast with Women in Housing and Finance on Tuesday, June 13 at the Hotel Washington. The Under Secretary will discuss government sponsored enterprises (GSEs) and current policy initiatives at the Treasury.
