Treasury Secretary John W. Snow is in Berlin, Germany this weekend for the Group of 20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors' meeting. The Secretary this week traveled to Dublin, Ireland; London, England; and Warsaw, Poland to discuss the state of the global economy and efforts to continue strong global economic growth.
