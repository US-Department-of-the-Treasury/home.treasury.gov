**WASHINGTON** – U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin spoke by phone today with Mexican Secretary of Finance and Public Credit José Antonio Meade.

Secretary Mnuchin underscored the strategic and economic importance of the U.S.-Mexico bilateral relationship, and that he looked forward to working closely with Secretary Meade as part of the comprehensive dialogue that Presidents Trump and Peña Nieto announced to discuss all aspects of that relationship.
