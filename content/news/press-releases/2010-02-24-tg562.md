We saw the Senate act in a bipartisan manner to pass several of the President's top priorities, among them: tax incentives for businesses to hire new workers, a tax cut to support investments by small businesses, and additional funds to keep people at work making much-needed repairs to our roads and bridges. We hope this important step will allow the President to sign a jobs bill into law quickly.
