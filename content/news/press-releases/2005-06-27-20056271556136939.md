Tonight, Treasury Secretary John Snow will speak to the Council on Foreign Relations in New York, N.Y. His remarks will focus on the financial agenda of the upcoming G8 meetings in Gleneagles, Scotland including the need for global growth, debt cancellation for the world's poorest countries and economic development assistance, particularly in Africa
