FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

LS-967

Treasury Secretary Lawrence H. Summers will hold a pre G-20 press conference at 9:30 a.m. EDT on Tuesday, October 24, 2000 in the Treasury Department's Diplomatic Reception Room (Room 3311), 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW.The room will be available for pre-set at 8:30 a.m. Media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-2999.
