​**Washington**– Yesterday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Prime Minister Youssef Chahed of Tunisia.  The Secretary congratulated the Prime Minister on Tunisia’s political transition. The Secretary and Prime Minister discussed Tunisia’s economic reform initiatives and commitments under its IMF program. The Secretary underscored his support for Tunisia’s efforts to counter terrorism, including terrorist financing particularly with respect to ISIS.
