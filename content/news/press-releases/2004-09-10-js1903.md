*(Archived Content)*

Sandra Pedroarias, Director of Outreach for Treasury's Office of Financial Education will lead a financial education roundtable in Phoenix, Arizona Tuesday. Pedroarias will deliver remarks about Treasury's efforts to improve financial literacy across the country and discuss federal financial education resources available to the Hispanic community.

The roundtable, titled*Financial Education: Strengthening Hispanic Families,*will be hosted by Friendly House, a community-based organization providing comprehensive literacy services to adults and focusing on improving their standards of living.
