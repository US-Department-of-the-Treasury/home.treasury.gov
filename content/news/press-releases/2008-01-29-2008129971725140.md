*(Archived Content)*

Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral and Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education Dan Iannicola, Jr. will announce a new partnership today between the Treasury Department Federal Credit Union and Operation Hope. Through public/private partnerships, Operation Hope develops and implements programs focused on providing economic tools and services to minority communities. This new partnership will provide free counseling and educational services to DC residents.
