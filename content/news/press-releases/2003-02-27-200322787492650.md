Today, Thursday, February 27th, Treasury Secretary John Snow will join House and Senate leaders for two press conferences to introduce President Bush's jobs and growth plan.  The House press conference takes place at 10:15am in room H-129.  The Senate press conference takes place at 11:30am in room 608 in the Dirksen Senate Office Building.  While he has only been on the job for three weeks, Snow has been working vigorously to promote the President's plan.
