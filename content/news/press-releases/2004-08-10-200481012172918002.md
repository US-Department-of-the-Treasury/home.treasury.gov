Assistant Secretary Abernathy in Florida to Discuss Financial Issues

Assistant Secretary for Financial Institutions Wayne Abernathy is in Orlando, FL today to meet with government, law enforcement and private sector leaders on how banks can best prepare for a potential emergency, and discuss efforts to protect the financial sector from terrorist attacks. While in Orlando, Abernathy also will lead a roundtable discussion on financial education with local business, nonprofit, academic and government representatives.
