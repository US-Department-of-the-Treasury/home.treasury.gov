Secretary Snow and U.K. Chancellor Gordon Brown today hosted an Entrepreneurship Summit in New York City to focus on the key role that enterprise and innovation plays in economic progress. During the roundtable with 17 U.S. and U.K. entrepreneurs, moderated by journalist Charlie Rose, the Secretary highlighted the Administration's efforts to foster an environment that allows entrepreneurship to flourish.
