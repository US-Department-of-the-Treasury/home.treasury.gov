Secretary John Snow will visit Portland, Maine and Manchester, New Hampshire on July 8-9 to meet with local business leaders and discuss the President's efforts to strengthen the economy and create jobs. Last month, Maine gained 2,100 new jobs and New Hampshire gained 4,600 new jobs.
