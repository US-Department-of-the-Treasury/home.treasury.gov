FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

JS-742

Despite the closing of federal agencies today in preparation for Hurricane Isabel, the following events will proceed as scheduled:

The 13 week/26 week bill announcement will occur at 11:00 am EDT. ([www.publicdebt.treas.gov](http://www.publicdebt.treas.gov/))

The results of the TIO auction will be posted to the Financial Management Services website at 12:00 pm EDT ([www.fms.treas.gov](http://www.fms.treas.gov/)).

The Daily Treasury Statement will be released at 4:00 pm EDT ([www.fms.treas.gov](http://www.fms.treas.gov/)).
