Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will deliver remarks tomorrow at the China Institute Executive Summit in New York. His remarks will focus on the progress of the Strategic Economic Dialogue with China. The dialogue, established by Presidents Bush and Hu, is a framework for addressing issues of mutual concern to the U.S. and China.
