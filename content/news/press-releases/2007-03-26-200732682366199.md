draft: false
url: /news/press-releases/200732682366199
---

The U.S. Treasury Department will lead a Financial Literacy and Education Commission regional conference in Seattle, Wash. tomorrow to discuss ways to bring the millions of unbanked Americans into the financial mainstream. Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education Dan Iannicola will participate in the day-long discussion to help the unbanked, who do not participate in traditional accounts.
