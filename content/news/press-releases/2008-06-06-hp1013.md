Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will deliver remarks next week to the Carnegie Endowment for International Peace on the U.S. – China economic relationship and the upcoming meeting of the Strategic Economic Dialogue (SED). The dialogue, established by Presidents Bush and Hu, is a framework for managing our bilateral economic relationship on a long-term strategic basis. The fourth Cabinet-level meeting will take place June 17-18, 2008 at the U.S. Naval Academy.
