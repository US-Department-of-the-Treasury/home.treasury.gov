U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral will visit the Kansas City Hispanic Chamber of Commerce on Friday, November 3 to discuss the economy and the national jobs report to be announced that day. The U.S. Treasurer, whose name appears on U.S. currency, serves as an adviser to the Secretary on matters of currency design and production.
