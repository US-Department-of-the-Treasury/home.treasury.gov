Treasury Secretary John Snow visited the hurricane-ravaged Gulf Coast region on Friday with Commerce Secretary Carlos Gutierrez and Labor Secretary Elaine Chao. The group visited with hurricane victims, government relief workers and local and state leaders to discuss economic relief efforts and future plans for rebuilding and economic redevelopment in the region.
