U.S. Treasury Assistant Secretary for Financial Institutions Emil W. Henry, Jr. will give remarks before the Housing Policy Council of the Financial Services Roundtable on Monday, June 26 at the Financial Services Roundtable. The Assistant Secretary will discuss government sponsored enterprises (GSEs) and systemic risk.
