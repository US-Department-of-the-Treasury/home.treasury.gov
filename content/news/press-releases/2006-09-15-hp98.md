U.S. Treasury Assistant Secretary for Financial Institutions Emil W. Henry, Jr. will speak at the National Association of Federal Credit Unions' 2006 Congressional Caucus on Tues., Sept. 19 in Washington, D.C.  He will discuss the role credit unions play in the financial marketplace.
