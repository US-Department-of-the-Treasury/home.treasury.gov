FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

LS-176

The Treasury Department today released U.S. reserve assets data for the week ending October 22, 1999.

As this table indicates, U.S. reserve assets totaled $73,930 million as of October 22, 1999, up from $73,795 million as of October 15, 1999.
