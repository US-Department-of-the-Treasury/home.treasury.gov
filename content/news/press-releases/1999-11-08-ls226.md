FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

LS-226

The Treasury Department today released U.S. reserve assets data for the week ending November 5, 1999.

As this table indicates, U.S. reserve assets totaled $72,644 million as of November 5, 1999, as compared with $73,228 million as of October 29, 1999.
