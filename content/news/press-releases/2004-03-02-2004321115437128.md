The Treasury Department today announced the designation of seven Zimbabwean entities as Specially Designated Nationals (SDNs) of Zimbabwe. The action illustrates the Bush Administration’s commitment to condemning those who threaten the democratic process and institutions in Zimbabwe by isolating and exposing them and the entities they own and control.
