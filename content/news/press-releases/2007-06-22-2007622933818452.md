Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. visited the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN) last week to discuss FinCEN's continued efforts to protect the financial system from abuse by money launderers, terrorists, and other criminals.  The Secretary also discussed how FinCEN is working to administer the Bank Secrecy Act  in a more efficient and effective manner.
