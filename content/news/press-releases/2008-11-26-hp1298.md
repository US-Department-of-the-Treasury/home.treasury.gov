HP-1298

Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will deliver remarks on the U.S.-China Strategic Economic Dialogue to the World Affairs Council of Washington, D.C. in advance of the upcoming SED meeting in China. The SED, established by Presidents Bush and Hu, is a framework for managing our bilateral economic relationship on a long-term strategic basis.
