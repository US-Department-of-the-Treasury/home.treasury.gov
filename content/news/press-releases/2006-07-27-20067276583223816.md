Treasury Secretary Paulson will deliver his first speech on Tuesday, Aug. 1 in New York at the Columbia Business School. The speech will focus on the outlook and challenges for the U.S. and global economies. While in New York, the Secretary will also visit the New York Stock Exchange and NASDAQ's Stock Market. He'll meet with business leaders at both stops to discuss current economic conditions.
