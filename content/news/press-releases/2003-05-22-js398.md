FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS



JS-398



The Treasury Department is postponing announcement of its weekly 13-week and 26-week bill auctions and its monthly 2-year note auction, scheduled to be announced May 22, 2003, until further notice. This postponement is due to the statutory debt limit.
