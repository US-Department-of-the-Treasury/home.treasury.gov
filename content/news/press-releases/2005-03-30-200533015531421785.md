Secretary Snow is inBozeman, MT today as part of his trip through Oregon, Montana and North Dakota this week to meet with local business leaders, students and community leaders about the President's efforts to strengthen and preserve Social Security. In remarks to Bozeman community leaders he said, With voluntary personal accounts, younger workers would have the chance to learn about their financial choices, build a nest egg and benefit from sound long-term investment in the free market system without disrupting the system of benefits for today's retired beneficiaries. He will be Bismarck, ND on Thursday.
