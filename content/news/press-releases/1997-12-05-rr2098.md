date: 1997-12-05

---
*(Archived Content)*

The Treasury Department today opened its new Briefing Room on the World Wide Web (*www.treas.gov/press/*). The Treasury Briefing Room is a public resource for the latest press releases, speeches, and other public documents from the Department, in addition to the weekly public schedule of events.



The web page will also feature publication-quality photographs of daily Treasury events and an archive of photographs of Department officials.
