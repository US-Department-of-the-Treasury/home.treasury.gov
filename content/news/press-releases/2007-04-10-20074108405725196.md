Secretary Paulson will host a meeting of the G7 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors at the Treasury Department this Friday. The G7 gathering takes place in advance of the spring meetings of the World Bank and IMF which are scheduled this weekend to discuss progress on the work of the Bank and the Fund. Under Sec Tim Adams will hold a press conference Thursday to discuss the meetings.
