Stuart Levey, Treasury Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence, will testify tomorrow before the Senate Committee on Banking, Housing and Urban Affairs on the topic of money laundering and terror financing issues in the Middle East. The testimony will be in room 538 of the DirksenSenateOfficeBuilding at 10:00 am.
