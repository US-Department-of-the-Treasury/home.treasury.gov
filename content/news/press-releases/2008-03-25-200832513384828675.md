Treasury Secretary and Managing Trustee Henry M. Paulson, Jr. and members of the Social Security and Medicare Boards of Trustees Secretary of Labor Elaine L. Chao, Secretary of Health and Human Services Michael Leavitt and Commissioner of Social Security Michael J. Astrue met today to discuss the release of the annual Trustees Reports. The facts are clear: the sooner Social Security and Medicare are reformed, the fairer reforms will be to future generations, said Paulson
