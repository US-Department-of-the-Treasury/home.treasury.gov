Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. travels to China this week for the third Cabinet-level meeting of the U.S.-China Strategic Economic Dialogue. The SED will focus on integrity of trade, balanced economic development, energy conservation, financial sector reform, environmental sustainability, and advancing bilateral investment.
