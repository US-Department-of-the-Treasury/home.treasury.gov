This week, Treasury Secretary John Snow proposed a package of changes to the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) that balances two critical consumer interests: 1) the need for security and accuracy of their personal financial information, and 2) the need for access to credit and other financial services.[Click here](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/js515)for Snow's remarks and a fact sheet on the Bush Administration's proposal.
