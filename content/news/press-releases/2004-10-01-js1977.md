FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS



JS-1977

****

Treasury Deputy Secretary Samuel W. Bodman will be in Charleston and Parkersburg, West Virginia next week to meet with local business leaders and discuss the President's efforts to strengthen the economy and create jobs. Also, while in Parkersburg, the Deputy Secretary will tour Treasury's Bureau of Public Debt (BPD) facilities there and meet with employees. As the agency that borrows the money needed to operate the federal government, BPD issues and services U.S. Treasury marketable, savings and special securities.
