U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Tim Geithner and Finance Minister Pranab Mukherjee announced the establishment of a new U.S.-India Economic and Financial Partnership to strengthen bilateral engagement and understanding on macroeconomic, financial sector, and infrastructure-related issues. Secretary Geithner will visit India in early 2010 for the Partnership's launch with Finance Minister Mukherjee.
