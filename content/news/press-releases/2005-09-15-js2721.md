*(Archived Content)*

U.S. Treasury Secretary John W. Snow will travel to Atlanta, Georgia, tomorrow to discuss relief efforts following Hurricane Katrina.  In Atlanta, Secretary Snow will visit the Home Depot emergency headquarters to see their emergency command center and review how the company assists victims of the hurricane.  Following his Home Depot visit, the Secretary and IRS Commissioner Everson will tour an IRS call center where several thousand IRS employees are helping the Federal Emergency Management Agency answer telephone calls from Hurricane Katrina victims seeking to register for federal relief benefits.
