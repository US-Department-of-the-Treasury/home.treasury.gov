The White House Task Force on Middle Class Families, chaired by Vice President Joe Biden, held a meeting at Syracuse University in New York to discuss ways to help families save and pay for college. The meeting, Making College More Accessible and Affordable for Middle Class Families, also highlighted specific improvements the Administration is making to the overall process of paying for college.
