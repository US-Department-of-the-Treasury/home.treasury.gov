Treasury Secretary John Snow will hold a press conference today in advance of his trip to Israel, Saudi Arabia, Afghanistan, Pakistan and UAE.  The press conference will be held at 2:30 PM at the Treasury Building.  Secretary Snow will make an opening statement and then be available for questions.
