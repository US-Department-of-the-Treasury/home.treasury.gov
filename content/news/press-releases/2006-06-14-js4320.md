U.S. Treasury Assistant Secretary for Financial Institutions Emil W. Henry, Jr. will give remarks before the Real Estate Roundtable on Thursday, June 15 at the Mandarin Oriental. The Assistant Secretary will discuss government sponsored enterprises (GSEs) and Treasury's process for approving GSE debt issuance.
