*(Archived Content)*

Secretary John Snow is in New York City on Saturday and Sunday to participate in the Finance Ministers' meetings ahead of the G-8 Leaders Summit in June. He will hold a press conference at the conclusion of the meetings, at approximately 2:30 pm on Sunday at the Waldorf-Astoria Hotel, and will release a written statement at that time.
