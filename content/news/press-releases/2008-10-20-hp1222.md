**Washington, DC--** Treasury, the Federal Reserve, the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency, the Office of Thrift Supervision, and the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation today issued application guidelines and other documents for the[Capital Purchase Program](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/hp1207)announced last week. Attached are application guidelines, an application form and a Frequently Asked Questions document to provide additional details.** -30- REPORTS**
