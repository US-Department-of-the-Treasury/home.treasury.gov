*(Archived Content)*

2007-9-12-14-2-9-7436


Secretary Paulson and HUD Secretary Alphonso Jackson met with mortgage servicers at the Treasury Department to discuss ways to help identify homeowners before they face hardships, help them understand their financing options, and assist them in finding a mortgage product that works for them. President Bush recently announced a series of steps to help homeowners facing mortgage troubles keep their homes.
