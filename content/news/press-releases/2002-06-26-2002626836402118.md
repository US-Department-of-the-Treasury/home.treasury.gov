Citing the critical priority of maintaining the full faith and credit of the United States Government, President Bush - in a letter to House Speaker J. Dennis Hastert (R-Ill.) and Minority Leader Richard A. Gephardt - appealed to House leaders yesterday to raise the ceiling on the national debt.
