**WASHINGTON –** The fifth round of the U.S.-China Strategic and Economic Dialogue will be held in Washington, D.C., July 10-11, 2013.  Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew and Secretary of State John F. Kerry will be joined for the Dialogue by Chinese co-chairs, Vice Premier Wang Yang and State Councilor Yang Jiechi, along with members of the Chinese delegation and their U.S. colleagues.    The schedule of press events will include but is not limited to an opening session on Wednesday, July 10, and a closing session on Thursday, July 11.   Further details regarding the agenda and additional press opportunities will be made available shortly.   Applications for media credentials were due June 30, 2013.  Members of the media who were granted credentials will receive a confirmation email with pick up information.  For more details, please contact [megan.jordan@treasury.gov](mailto:megan.jordan@treasury.gov).   ###​
