​

**Washington**– Yesterday, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met today with Finance Minister Moshe Kahlon of the State of Israel.  The Secretary highlighted the close ties between the two nations and underscored the United States’ commitment to the security and safety of Israel.  The Secretary and Finance Minister discussed the importance of the thriving bilateral economic relationship.
