**Washington** – Advisor to the President Ivanka Trump will join U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin in California to discuss the importance of tax reform with local businesses and hardworking Americans.

"I'm looking forward to Ivanka joining me in California to discuss directly with the American people how our tax reform package will empower them to keep more of their hard-earned money. Ivanka has been tireless in her efforts to deliver tax relief to middle income families," said Secretary Mnuchin.

Ms. Trump will give remarks and introduce Secretary Mnuchin for his remarks at the Ronald Reagan Presidential Library on Sunday, November 5. The Secretary and Ms. Trump will then participate in a fireside chat with John Heubusch, the Reagan Foundation's Executive Director. Additionally, Ms. Trump will join Secretary Mnuchin at a business roundtable in Newport Beach on Monday, November 6.
