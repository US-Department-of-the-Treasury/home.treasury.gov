FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

LS-1044



We welcome El Salvador's proposal to use the U.S. dollar as its currency. Combined with a strong economic policy framework, this step should help contribute to financial stability and economic growth in El Salvador and its further integration into the global economy.
