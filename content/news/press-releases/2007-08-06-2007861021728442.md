Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. traveled to Montana last week to discuss the importance of trade and the economy in rural America. While in Billings, Paulson participated in a public forum on keeping rural areas competitive in the global economy. He also toured MRL equipment and gave remarks to the employees.
