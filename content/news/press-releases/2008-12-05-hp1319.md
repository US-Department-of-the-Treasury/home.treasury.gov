HP-1319

**Washington-** The Treasury Department announced today that it distributed 1.546 million additional stimulus payments in November, totaling $1.092 billion. As of the end of November, a total of 118.919 million payments have been distributed totaling $96.130 billion since disbursements started April 28.

While mass disbursement of stimulus checks ended July 11, small batches of payments continue to be sent out to American households. The Treasury Department will announce updates monthly until the end of the year.
