Deputy Secretary Robert M. Kimmitt will hold an on-camera briefing Tuesday on the Palestine Investment Conference.  Deputy Secretary Kimmitt is leading a Presidential Delegation to the conference, which will take place in Bethlehem May 21-23, 2008.  More information on the Palestine Investment Conference is available at:[http://www.pic-palestine.ps/](http://www.pic-palestine.ps/).
