Deputy Secretary Robert M. Kimmitt will participate in a discussion tonight hosted by the Council of Foreign Relations on international investment in the United States. The forum will begin at 6 p.m. at 58 East 68th Street in New York City. Media wishing to attend should R.S.V.P. to: [nypressrsvp@cfr.org](mailto:nypressrsvp@cfr.org).
