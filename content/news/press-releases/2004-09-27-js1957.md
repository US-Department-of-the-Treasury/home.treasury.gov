Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education Dan Iannicola, Jr. will launch Treasury'sFinancialEducationTechnicalAssistanceCenter, a new service provided by Treasury's Office of Financial Education, tomorrow at the Credit Industry Research Council Fall 2004 Conference inLas Vegas,Nevada.Iannicola will explain how those with questions about financial education programs can access the service and will provide information about Treasury's financial education initiatives.

**WHO**: Deputy Assistant Secretary for Financial Education, Dan Iannicola, Jr.** WHAT:** Launch of Financial Education Technical Assistance Center and remarks on Treasury's financial education initiatives** WHEN**: Tuesday, September 28, 200410:30 a.m. PDT** WHERE:** The Ritz-Carlton Hotel and Resort1610 Lake Las Vegas ParkwayHenderson, NV
