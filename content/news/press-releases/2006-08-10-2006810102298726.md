Treasury Secretary Paulson will be in Chicago today to discuss the U.S. economy with market participants and business owners. He will visit the Chicago Board Options Exchange, Chicago Board of Trade, and the Chicago Mercantile Exchange.  Secretary Paulson will also meet with small business owners to discuss current economic conditions.
