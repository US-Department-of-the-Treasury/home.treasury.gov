Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Randal K. Quarles will participate in a roundtable discussion with Members of Congress at the Financial Services Forum on Monday.  The panel, which will be hosted by former Secretary of Commerce Donald L. Evans, will discuss the effects ofU.S.corporate governance rules on the competitiveness of American businesses and financial markets.
