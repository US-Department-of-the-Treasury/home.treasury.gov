Treasury Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Brian Roseboro will open the third meeting of the Financial Literacy and Education Commission in the Department of the Treasury's Cash Room. Under Secretary Roseboro will deliver remarks and welcome the 20 representatives of the Financial Literacy and Education Commission, as well as best practices presenters representing the government, nonprofit, and private sectors.

Treasury's Office of Financial Education coordinates the efforts of the Financial Literacy and Education Commission, which is chaired by the Secretary of Treasury and composed of representatives from 20 federal departments, agencies and commissions, which works to improve financial literacy and education for people throughout the United States.
