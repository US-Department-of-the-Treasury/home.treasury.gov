Secretary Snow has a bad chest cold and will not be traveling to the meeting of G7 finance ministers and central bank governors inLondon,UK, on February 4-5.  John B. Taylor, Under Secretary for International Affairs, will represent the U.S. Treasury at the meetings.  Secretary Snow will spend the rest of the week at his home inRichmond,VA, and return toWashington,DC, on Monday.
