Treasury Secretary John Snow will keynote Money Magazine's Money Summit on Tuesday, June 17 at noon at the St. Regis Hotel.  Secretary Snow will discuss the current state of the U.S. economy and how key provisions of the President Bush’s Jobs and Growth plan will impact growth prospects.
