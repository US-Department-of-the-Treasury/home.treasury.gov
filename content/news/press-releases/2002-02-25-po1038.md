MEDIA ADVISORY
WHAT: Treasury Secretary Paul O'Neill will provide an update on the war against terrorist financing to Customs Service employees at the Naval Air Station in Jacksonville, Florida.

WHERE: Naval Air Station Jacksonville
Highway 17, Jacksonville, FL
To access this press event please meet at the Main Gate of the Naval Air Station at 1:30 p.m. Base personnel will escort members of the media onto the base at this time. For questions regarding access to this event please contact Rick Crews, Public Affairs NAS Jacksonville at (904) 542-4032.
