*(Archived Content)*

Secretary John W. Snow will visit San Francisco and San Jose, California and Las Vegas, Nevada on Thursday and Friday to meet with local business leaders and discuss the President's efforts to strengthen the economy and create jobs. As a result of the Administration's tax policy reforms, 12.4 million California taxpayers and 845,000 Nevada taxpayers will have lower income tax bills in 2004.
