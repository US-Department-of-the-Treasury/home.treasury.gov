*(Archived Content)*

Deputy U.S. Treasury Secretary Ken Dam will travel to Japan December 4-8. Dam will meet with a wide array of senior government officials and private sector political, financial and economic experts to discuss terrorist financing, investment and a range of steps supportive of strong economic growth.
