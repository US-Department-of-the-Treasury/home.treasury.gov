WASHINGTON
–
U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin issued the following statement today following the passing of the former Federal Reserve Chairman and U.S. Treasury official Paul Volcker:
“My condolences to the family of the former Federal Reserve Chairman and U.S. Treasury official Paul Volcker. He served our country and the Department proudly.”
