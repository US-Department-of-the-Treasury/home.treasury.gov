Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. met with President Calderon, Finance Minister Carstens, and Foreign Affairs Secretary Espinosa Tuesday in Mexico City to underscore his support for their strong economic policy agenda. He also hosted a conversation with small business owners and finance providerswhere they discussed expanding the benefits of economic growth.
