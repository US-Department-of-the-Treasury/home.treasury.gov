U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral spoke to the annual Summit of Executive Women in Government Wednesday morning. The conference celebrated Women's History Month and Cabral shared the story of her own personal and professional life before giving an overview of the strength of the American economy and the steps that government must take to stay on a path of economic growth and job creation.
