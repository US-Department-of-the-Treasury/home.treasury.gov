U.S. Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will deliver remarks at the Financial Literacy and Education Commission's public meeting at the Treasury Department tomorrow.  Secretary Paulson will discuss his recent meeting with President Bush and the importance of financial education for future homeowners considering a mortgage.
