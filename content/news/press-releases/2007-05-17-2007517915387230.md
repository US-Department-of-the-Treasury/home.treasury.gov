U.S. Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. announced initiatives today to enhance U.S. capital markets competitiveness, focused on strengthened financial reporting and a more sustainable and transparent auditing profession.  Under Secretary for Domestic Finance Robert K. Steel will deliver a speech discussing the plan today.
