U.S. Treasury Under Secretary for International Affairs Tim Adams will visit Houston, Texas to discuss the U.S. and international economy and President Bush's agenda for continued strong growth and job creation. While in Houston, Under Secretary Adams will make remarks to the Asia Society.



The following event is open to credentialed media. **WHO** U.S. Treasury Under Secretary for International Affairs Tim Adams



**WHAT** Remarks to Asia Society



**WHEN** Friday, April 7, 12:30 p.m. (CDT)



**WHERE** Houston Society of Financial Analysts 4605 Post Oak Place Suite 205 Houston, TX
