​**WASHINGTON **– On Saturday, April 22, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin met with Qatar’s Minister of Finance Ali Shareef al-Emadi  The Secretary and Minister discussed the growing bilateral economic relationship, and Secretary Mnuchin underscored the U.S. commitment to the Gulf Banking Dialogue as a venue to discuss banking and financial services issues between the U.S. and Qatar.  Secretary Mnuchin discussed deepening cooperation regarding countering the financing of terrorism.
