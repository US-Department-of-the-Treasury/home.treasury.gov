HP-576

**Washington, DC –**Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will welcome Mexican Finance Minister Agustin Carstens to the U.S. Department of the Treasury today, September 25, 2007. This meeting is a continuation of their regular dialogue and close cooperation on a range of issues, including global and regional economic and financial developments, the effectiveness of international financial institutions, efforts to combat money laundering and terrorist finance, and tax issues.
