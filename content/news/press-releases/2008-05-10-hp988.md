**Washington - Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will welcome Greek Minister of Economy and Finance George Alogoskoufis to the U.S. Department of the Treasury on Wednesday, May 21. They will discuss global economic and financial developments, open investment, the effort to relieve Iraq debt and safeguarding the global financial system from abuse.** - Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will welcome Greek Minister of Economy and Finance George Alogoskoufis to the U.S. Department of the Treasury on Wednesday, May 21.
