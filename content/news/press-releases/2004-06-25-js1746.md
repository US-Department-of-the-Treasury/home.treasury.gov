U.S. Treasury Secretary John W. Snow will visit Anchorage, Alaska on Monday, June 28 to meet with local business leaders and discuss the President's efforts to strengthen the economy and create jobs.



The President's tax reform policies have ensured that nearly 265,000 Alaska taxpayerswill have lower income tax bills in 2004, said Secretary Snow.
