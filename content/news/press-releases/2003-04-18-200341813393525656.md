Over the 2 week Congressional recess, 14 treasury officials will fan out over 17 states to participate in 39 events in 26 cities to promote the President's jobs and growth plan.      During Secretary Snow's 10 weeks on the job, he has traveled to New York, Ohio, Michigan, Pennsylvania, Florida, Indiana, and Louisiana to advance the President's economic agenda
