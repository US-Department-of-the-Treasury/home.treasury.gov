WASHINGTON, DC – The Treasury Department today announced that the United States and the Federal Republic of Germany exchanged diplomatic notes correcting typographical errors in the recently signed Protocol to the U.S.-German income tax treaty.   The corrected text replaces the original text from the date on which the Protocol was signed and will be incorporated into the original text when the Protocol is printed in the Treaties and Other International Acts Series (TIAS).
