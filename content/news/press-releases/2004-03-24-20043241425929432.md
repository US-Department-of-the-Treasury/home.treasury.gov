Secretary John W. Snow today testifies on the Social Security and Medicare Trustees' Reports before the House Ways and Means Committee. At the rollout of the reports yesterday, Secretary Snow said, This Administration will continue its open and honest discussion of the issues and remains dedicated to working with Congress to take the steps needed to secure the long-term strength of these programs.
