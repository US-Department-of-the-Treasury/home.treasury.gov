*(Archived Content)*

Secretary John Snow on Monday, April 12 will join U.S. Small Business Administrator Hector Barreto in Kansas City to meet with small business owners as part of SBA's Economic Growth Tour. He will talk about the President's efforts to help small businesses grow and create jobs, further strengthening the economy.
