*(Archived Content)*

Treasury Secretary John Snow and Ambassador Ryozo Kato, the Japanese Ambassador to the United States, will hold a signing ceremony for the new US-Japan income tax treaty at 4:30 p.m. EST on Thursday, November 6, 2003 in the Treasury Department's Cash Room, 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW.

The Room will be available for pre-set at 3:30 p.m.

Media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-1999.
