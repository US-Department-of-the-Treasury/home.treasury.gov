Treasury Secretary John Snow will travel toPhiladelphiatoday to discuss the state of theU.S.economy.  While inPhiladelphia, Secretary Snow will visit Centocor, a biotechnology company located inRadnor,Pennsylvania, where he will make note of the contributions that innovation and technology make to the economy and to standards of living for the American people.  Secretary Snow will also deliver remarks to the 2006 Greater Philadelphia Investment Conference.
