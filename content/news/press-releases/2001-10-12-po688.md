Treasury Secretary Paul H. O'Neill will hold a Southeast Business Roundtable discussion at 8:00 a.m. on Monday, October 15, 2001 in the Boardroom of the Peabody Hotel, 149 Union Avenue, Memphis, TN 38103.



The meeting will only be open to the press for the first five minutes of the Secretary's remarks. Press members should assemble outside of the Boardroom by 8:00 a.m. to be escorted in. For further information, please contact Sean Miles at (202) 622-2015.
