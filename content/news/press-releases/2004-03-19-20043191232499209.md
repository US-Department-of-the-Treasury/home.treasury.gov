*(Archived Content)*

In his first major address, Deputy Secretary Samuel Bodman will highlight the need for tax cut permanency in remarks before the Tax Executive Institute on March 23. He will outline the positive impact of the President's tax policies on the economy, and discuss what more needs to be done to strengthen the economy and create jobs.
