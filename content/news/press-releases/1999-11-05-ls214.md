FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS

LS-214

Treasury Deputy Secretary Stuart E. Eizenstat and Count Otto Graf Lambsdorff, Special Representative for the German Foundation Initiative, will hold a joint press conference on the Holocaust forced and slave labor negotiation meetings **today** at**3:30 p.m.** in the** Cash Room** of the Main Treasury Building, 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, N.W.

Media without Treasury, White House, State Department, Defense Department or Congressional press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasurys Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960, with the following information: name, social security number or date of birth. Foreign press should include foreign passport numbers. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-1999. The Cash Room will be available for pre-set at 2:30 p.m.
