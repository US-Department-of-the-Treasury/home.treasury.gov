Deputy Treasury Secretary Robert Kimmitt will participate in a *Foreign Affairs* magazine media conference call on sovereign wealth funds and the world economy. He will discuss his article in the January/February 2008 issue of *Foreign Affairs*, Public Footprints in Private Markets: Sovereign Wealth Funds and the World Economy. Deputy Secretary Kimmitt's article is available at: [http://www.foreignaffairs.org/20080101faessay87109/robert-m-kimmitt/public-footprints-in-private-markets.html](http://www.foreignaffairs.org/20080101faessay87109/robert-m-kimmitt/public-footprints-in-private-markets.html)

- **When:**2:00 p.m. (EST) Monday, January 28
