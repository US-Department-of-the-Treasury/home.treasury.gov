*(Archived Content)*

Treasury Secretary John W. Snow and U.S. Treasurer Rosario Marin on Wednesday, March 5, 2003, will provide their signatures to the Bureau of Engraving and Printing for use on Series 2003 U.S. currency.



Once provided to the Bureau of Engraving and Printing, the signatures of Secretary Snow and Treasurer Marin are transferred by engravers to steel plates, which are used to print all new U.S. currency.



The ceremony will be held at 11:00 a.m. EST at the Treasury Department (Room 4121), 1500 Pennsylvania Ave., NW, Washington, DC. It will be web cast live on the Treasury web site,[www.treasury.gov](http://www.treasury.gov/).



All media attending the event must have a Treasury or White House press pass to enter the building. Those without a Treasury or White House press pass must be cleared in by the Secret Service IN ADVANCE.
