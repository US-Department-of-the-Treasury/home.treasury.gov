**FROM THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC AFFAIRS** *We recommend printing this release using the PDF file below. To view or print the PDF content on this page, download the free Adobe Acrobat Reader. * JS-4259 Treasury International Capital (TIC) data for March are released today and posted on the U.S. Treasury web site ( [www.treas.gov/tic](https://www.treasury.gov/resource-center/data-chart-center/tic)). The next release date, which will report on data for April, is scheduled for June 15, 2006. Net foreign purchases of long-term securities were $69.8 billion. 
- Net foreign purchases of long-term domestic securities were $89.2 billion, $1.6 billion of which were net purchases by foreign official institutions and $87.6 billion of which were net purchases by private foreign investors.
- U.S. residents purchased a net $19.4 billion in foreign issued securities
