*(Archived Content)*

Deputy Secretary Sam Bodman congratulated Donald F. Donahue for being designated by the Treasury Department as the new Private Sector Coordinator in the national effort to protect the U.S.’s critical financial infrastructure.  As Sector Coordinator, Donahue will work with Treasury to respond to President Bush’s call to develop strategies to strengthen and protect our critical financial infrastructure and ensure that the private sector and the government work cooperatively to enhance the resilience of our financial infrastructure.
