*(Archived Content)*

Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. is in California today and tomorrow to promote financial literacy and discuss a range of issues critical to the global and U.S. economies and financial markets. Paulson will be joined today by U.S. Treasurer Anna Escobedo Cabral, as well as members of the President's Financial Literacy Council, for a discussion on financial education with community leaders in Oakland. On Friday Paulson will discuss economic issues with local representatives of companies listed on the NASDAQ, tour the headquarters of an energy company and deliver remarks to the 2008 Stanford Institute of Economic Policy Research Economic Summit.
