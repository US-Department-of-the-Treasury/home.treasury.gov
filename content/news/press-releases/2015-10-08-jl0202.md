​​  **LIMA** – U.S. Treasury Secretary Jacob J. Lew met today with Brazil Finance Minister Joaquim Levy on the sidelines of the Annual Meetings of the International Monetary Fund (IMF) and the World Bank Group.  Secretary Lew and Minister Levy exchanged views on the global economic outlook and recent developments in emerging markets.  They also discussed Brazil’s efforts to establish a foundation for renewed economic growth.  
Secretary Lew encouraged Minister Levy to continue his efforts to move forward forcefully with efforts to stabilize the economy, and lay the groundwork for the Brazilian middle class to realize its aspirations.
