Treasury Secretary John Snow will speak to the 2006 Governmental Affairs Conference of the Credit Union National Association (CUNA) this morning inWashington,DC. The Secretary will be discussing the strong state of theU.S.economy and credit unions' role in economic growth, the financial war on terror and financial literacy.
