Assistant Secretary for Financial Institutions Wayne Abernathy on Thursday this week will speak about the government's efforts to protect the financial sector in the event of an emergency or future terrorist attack. He will make his remarks at the FDIC/FBIIC/FSSCC Outreach Conference being held at George Washington University.
