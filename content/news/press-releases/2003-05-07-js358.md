U.S. Treasury Secretary John Snow will hold a press conference this afternoon at 3:30 PM in the Treasury Department Media Room immediately followed by a background briefing with Senior Treasury Officials.

**WHAT**: Press Conference by Secretary John Snow ** WHEN**:  3:30 PM ** WHERE**:  Treasury Department Media Room ** PARTICIPANTS**: Secretary SnowSenior Treasury Officials ** COVERAGE**: On-camera, on-the-record

Briefing will be open to Treasury Department and White House press pass holders.
