*(Archived Content)*

Secretary of the Treasury Paul H. O'Neill and U.S. Treasurer Rosario Marin will unveil



Series EE savings bonds designated as Patriot Bonds on Tuesday, December 11, 2001. They will be joined by Bureau of Public Debt Commissioner Van Zeck, Sens. Mitch McConnell (R-KY), Tim Johnson (D-SD), and Conrad Burns (R-MT).



The event will take place at 8:46 a.m. EST in the Diplomatic Room (Rm. 3311) at the Department of the Treasury, 1500 Pennsylvania Ave., NW, Washington, DC.



The room will be available for pre-set at 8:00 a.m. on Tuesday. News media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Frances Anderson at Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 by 8:00 a.m. on Dec. 11 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-1999.
