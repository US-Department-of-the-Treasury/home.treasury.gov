Assistant Secretary for International Affairs Clay Lowery will travel to Brazil this weekend to lead the Treasury delegation at the Annual Meeting of the Inter-American Development Bank in Belo Horizonte.

Following the IDB meetings, Lowery will travel to Sao Paulo to deliver a speech on reducing poverty in Latin America.  In Sao Paulo, Lowery will also meet with business leaders, infrastructure investors, bankers and micro-entrepreneurs to discuss how to accelerate economic growth and poverty reduction.  He will tour a Sao Paulo neighborhood to see how innovative anti-poverty programs are improving the lives of Brazil's poor and expanding opportunity for the next generation.
