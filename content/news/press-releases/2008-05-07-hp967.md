**Washington, DC**--Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson, Jr. will tour a Treasury Department printing facility in Kansas City Thursday to observe the first mass production printing and packaging of the 2008 stimulus checks.  He will also deliver remarks on the economic stimulus payments at the Kansas City Central Library.

By the end of June nearly 130 million stimulus payments should be in the hands of Americans, providing an immediate boost to the economy and helping to create more than 500,000 new jobs by the end of the year, said Paulson.
