Secretary John W. Snow will visit Independence, Missouri on Thursday, October 7 and Toledo, Ohio on Friday, October 8 to meet with local business leaders on the economy. He will discuss the President's efforts to strengthen the economy and create jobs, noting that 1.7 million new jobs have been created since August 2003. The President's tax reform policies have ensured that more than 2 million Missouri taxpayers and 4.4 million Ohio taxpayers will have lower income tax bills in 2004.
