*(Archived Content)*

Iannicola joined with the St. Louis Mayor, Francis G. Slay and to present a $52 million New Market Tax Credit to the St. Louis Development Corporation, one of 62 organizations selected by Treasury to receive a total of $3.5 billion in New Market Tax Credit awards this year. While in St. Louis, Iannicola is also participating in several events with community leaders and in local schools to promote financial education.
