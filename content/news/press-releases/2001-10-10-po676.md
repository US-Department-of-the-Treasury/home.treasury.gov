*(Archived Content)*

Deputy U.S. Treasury Secretary Ken Dam will join House Republican Policy Committee Chairman Christopher Cox, and other Members of House Leadership for a press availability at 10:00 am on Thursday, October 11th in the Hall of Columns stakeout area in the House side of the U.S. Capitol.



This press availability will follow Dam's meeting with the House Republican Policy Committee,a 46 member organization consisting of the Elected Leadership of the House, key committee Chairmen, and other Members elected by their colleagues from geographical regions and Congressional classes.
