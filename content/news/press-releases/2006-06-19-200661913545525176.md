The President's pro-growth policies are working.To date tax revenues are up 12.9% on top of last year's 14.6% total increase.Meanwhile, the deficit is decreasing and on the right path to meet the President's goal early.

Additional U.S. Economic Strength Indicators:

- More than 5.3 million new jobs created since the President's tax relief took effect.
- 5.3% economic (real GDP) growth for first quarter 2006 – fastest rate since 2003 – fastest of any major industrialized nation.
- 4% average real GDP growth since mid-2003.
- 12 straight quarters of business investment growth occurring immediately after May 2003, averaging 9%.
- 4.6% unemployment rate – lower than the average of the 60's, 70's, 80's or 90's.
