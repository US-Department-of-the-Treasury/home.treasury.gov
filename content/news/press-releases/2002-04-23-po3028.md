**Tony Fratto, Treasury Director of Public Affairs, will hold a press briefing at Noon on Tuesday, April 23 to announce Secretary Paul O'Neill's upcoming trip to Africa.**

Tony Fratto, Director of Public Affairs Africa Trip Announcement Tuesday, April 23, 2002 Noon Diplomatic Reception Room 3311 The Treasury Building Washington, DC



Media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202) 622-2960 with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxed to (202) 622-2999.
