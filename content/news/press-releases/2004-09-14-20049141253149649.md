Secretary John W. Snow tonight addresses the Economic Club of New York at the club's dinner in New York City. During his remarks, he will highlight the President's efforts to strengthen the economy and create jobs, increase the accessibility and affordability of health care, and reduce the economic headwind caused by frivolous lawsuits. He also will touch on the global agenda for growth and the importance of education in building a strong economic future for America.
