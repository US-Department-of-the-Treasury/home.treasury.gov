*(Archived Content)*

Treasury Secretary Paul H. O'Neill will hold the United States-Bahamas tax information exchange agreement signing ceremony at 11:00 a.m. EST on Friday, January 25, 2002 in the Treasury Department's Diplomatic Reception Room (Room 3311), 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW. Treasury Secretary O'Neill and Bahamian Finance Minister William Allen will be signing the tax information exchange agreement.



The Room will be available for pre-set at 10:00 a.m.



Media without Treasury or White House press credentials planning to attend should contact Treasury's Office of Public Affairs at (202-622-2960) with the following information: name, social security number and date of birth. This information may also be faxes to (202) 622-1999.
