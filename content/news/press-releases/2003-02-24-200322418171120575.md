*(Archived Content)*

Mexico announced today its intention to issue external bonds with collective action clauses. 

TheUnited States strongly supports and welcomes Mexico’s decision.  Mexico has been one of the top performing emerging markets in recent years, reflecting the country’s sound policies and economic fundamentals.  As a solid investment grade borrower, Mexico is making an important contribution to strengthening the international financial system.
