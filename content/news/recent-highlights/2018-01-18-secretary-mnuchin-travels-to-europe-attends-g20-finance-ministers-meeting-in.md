On March 15-18, Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin traveled to Europe for bilateral meetings with counterparts and to attend the G20 Finance Ministers Meeting.  He discussed ways to maintain robust economic relationships with our trading partners and to combat threats posed by North Korea and Iran.  Readouts from some of the Secretary’s key meetings are included below:

- [Readout from a Treasury Spokesperson on Secretary Mnuchin’s Meetings with Xiao Jie, Finance Minister of China, and Zhou Xiaochuan, Governor of the People’s Bank of China](https://www.treasury.gov/press-center/press-releases/Pages/sm0036.aspx)
- [Readout from a Treasury Spokesperson on Secretary Mnuchin’s Meeting with Michel Sapin, Finance Minister of France​​](https://www.treasury.gov/press-center/press-releases/Pages/sm0035.aspx)
- [Readout from a Treasury Spokesperson on Secretary Mnuchin’s Meeting with Taro Aso, Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister of Japan](https://www.treasury.gov/press-center/press-releases/Pages/sm0033.aspx)
- [Readout from a Treasury Spokesperson on Secretary Mnuchin’s Meeting with Mohammed al-Jadaan, Finance Minister of Saudi Arabia](https://www.treasury.gov/press-center/press-releases/Pages/sm0034.aspx)
- [Joint Readout on Secretary Mnuchin’s Meeting with Philip A. Hammond, Chancellor of the Exchequer, United Kingdom](https://www.treasury.gov/press-center/press-releases/Pages/sm0031.aspx)
