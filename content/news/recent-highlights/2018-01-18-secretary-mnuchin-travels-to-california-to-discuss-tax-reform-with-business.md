**Washington**– On November 1-6, U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin traveled to California to meet with local business leaders and hardworking Americans on the importance of tax reform.  He delivered remarks and engaged in a moderated discussion at the Ronald Reagan Presidential Library in recognition of President Reagan's historic tax cuts 31 years ago. Secretary Mnuchin also delivered remarks at the Los Angeles Convention Center before the International Franchise Association's Franchise Expo West, which brought together thousands of entrepreneurs and future business owners. He also met with local business leaders in Corona and Newport Beach to discuss how the tax relief plan will help their companies.  His prepared remarks are available below:
