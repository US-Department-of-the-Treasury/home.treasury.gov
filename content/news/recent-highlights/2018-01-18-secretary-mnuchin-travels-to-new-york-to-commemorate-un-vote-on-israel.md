**New York**—On November 28, 2017, Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin traveled to New York City with Vice President Mike Pence.  There he visited the Queens Museum and attended the Vice President's speech commemorating the 70th anniversary of the United Nations' historic 1947 vote establishing the state of Israel.The Secretary also visited the Federal Reserve Bank of New York and delivered remarks at The Evolving Structure of the U.S. Treasury Market:  Third Annual Conference.  The Secretary's speech focused on the United States' long-term financial prospects and the positive impacts of the Administration's tax and regulatory reform initiatives.  His remarks are available below:[Remarks by U.S. Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin before the Third Annual Conference on the Evolving Structure of the U.S. Treasury Market](https://www.treasury.gov/press-center/press-releases/Pages/sm0223.aspx)
