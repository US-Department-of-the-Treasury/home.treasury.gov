**Parkersburg, WV**– On August 28, Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin traveled to Parkersburg, West Virginia where he met with Senator Shelley Moore Capito and Congressman David McKinley.  They toured the Bureau of the Fiscal Service facility and held a roundtable discussion with local business leaders.  The focus of the discussions were the Administration and Congress’ tax reform efforts and ways to spur economic growth, higher wages, and create jobs.
