On October 24-30, Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin led a delegation including senior Treasury officials and members of the media to Saudi Arabia, Israel, UAE, and Qatar.  The Secretary commemorated the opening of the Terrorist Financing Targeting Center in Saudi Arabia, delivered the keynote address to the Future Investment Initiative summit, and met with government officials across the region to discuss terrorist financing and other national security issues.  The Secretary’s remarks, readouts of key meetings, and other announcements are included below:
