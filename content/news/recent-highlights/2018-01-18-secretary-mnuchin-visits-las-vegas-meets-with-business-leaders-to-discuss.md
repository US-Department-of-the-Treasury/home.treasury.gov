​**Las Vegas, NV **– On August 28, Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin traveled to Las Vegas, Nevada where he met with Senator Dean Heller and held a roundtable discussion with local business leaders.  The discussion focused on tax reform and ways to simplify the tax code, provide middle-income families and workers with tax relief, and spur economic growth.
