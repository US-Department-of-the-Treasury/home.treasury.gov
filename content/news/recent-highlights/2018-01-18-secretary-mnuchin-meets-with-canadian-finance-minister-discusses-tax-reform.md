**Ottawa**– On June 9, 2017, Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin traveled to Ottawa to meet with Canadian Finance Minister Bill Morneau as well as other senior Canadian administration officials and business leaders.  The discussions focused on the Trump Administration’s tax reform efforts as well as trade issues and middle-class job creation.
