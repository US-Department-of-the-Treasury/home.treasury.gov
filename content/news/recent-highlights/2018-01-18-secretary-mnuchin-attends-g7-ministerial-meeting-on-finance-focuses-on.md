On May 11-14, Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin traveled to Bari, Italy to attend the G7 Ministerial Meeting on Finance.  There he met with his counterparts from the UK, Germany, Italy, and Japan, among other officials, where he advocated for policies that will lead to greater economic growth for the United States and our allies, and stressed the importance of working together to address threats posed by rogue actors such as North Korea and Iran.  Readouts from some of the Secretary’s key meetings are included below:

- [Readout from a Treasury Spokesperson on Secretary Mnuchin’s Meeting with Italian Minister of Economy and Finance Pier Carlo Padoan](https://www.treasury.gov/press-center/press-releases/Pages/sm0081.aspx)
- [Readout from a Treasury Spokesperson on Secretary Mnuchin’s Meeting with Japanese Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister Taro Aso](https://www.treasury.gov/press-center/press-releases/Pages/sm0085.aspx)
- [Readout from a Treasury Spokesperson on Secretary Mnuchin’s Meeting with UK Chancellor of the Exchequer Philip Hammond](https://www.treasury.gov/press-center/press-releases/Pages/sm0084.aspx)
