New York—On November 9, 2017, Treasury Secretary Steven T. Mnuchin traveled to Manhattan for a moderated discussion on tax reform at The Economic Club of New York.  The Secretary discussed tax cuts for middle-income families, simplifying the tax code to make it fairer, and reducing business rates to make American companies more competitive.  The event was moderated by the Fox Business Networks' Maria Bartiromo.
