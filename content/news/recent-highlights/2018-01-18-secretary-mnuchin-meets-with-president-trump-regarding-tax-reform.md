**New York **– On August 15, 2017, Treasury Secretary Steven Mnuchin traveled to New York City to meet with the President at Trump Tower to discuss tax reform and tariffs.  He was joined on the trip by various Administration officials, including Director of the Office of Management and Budget Mick Mulvaney, Director of the National Economic Council Gary Cohn, and Transportation Secretary Elaine Chao.
