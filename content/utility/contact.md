### Lost or Stolen Checks

Contact the [paying agency](https://fiscal.treasury.gov/checkclaims/i-want-to.html#paying-agency) and report the loss or theft. You will be sent information on the check claims process, including forms you must return for processing.

After you have completed all the required steps, you can call and request an update of the status of your claim: 1-855-868-0151, option 1

**United States Mint Customer Service**  
To place an order, check the status of an existing order or inquire about a shipment or lost order, please call:
- Within the 50 United States: 1-800-USA-MINT (1-800-872-6468); TTY 1-888-321-MINT (6468)
- From Outside the 50 United States: 001-202-898-MINT (6468)

**United States Mint General Information**
