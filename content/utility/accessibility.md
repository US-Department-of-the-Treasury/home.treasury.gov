The U.S. Department of the Treasury is committed to making our website accessible to all users, including people with disabilities.

### Section 508 Compliance

We strive to meet the requirements of Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act, which requires federal agencies to make electronic and information technology accessible to people with disabilities.

### Accessibility Features

Our website includes:
- **Skip Navigation** - Skip to main content link for keyboard users
- **Alt Text** - Descriptive text for images
- **Keyboard Navigation** - Full keyboard accessibility
- **Screen Reader Compatibility** - ARIA labels and landmarks
- **Color Contrast** - WCAG 2.2 AA compliant contrast ratios
- **Resizable Text** - Support for browser text sizing
- **Focus Indicators** - Visible focus for keyboard navigation

### Assistive Technology

This website is designed to work with:
- Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
- Screen magnification software
- Voice recognition software
- Alternative input devices

### Feedback

If you encounter accessibility barriers on our website, please contact us:

**Email**: accessibility@treasury.gov

Please include:
- URL of the page with the issue
- Description of the accessibility barrier
- Assistive technology you are using

We will work to resolve accessibility issues promptly.
