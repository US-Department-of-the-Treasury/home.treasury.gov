The U.S. Department of the Treasury plays a central role in promoting economic growth and stability, managing the government's finances, and protecting the integrity of the financial system.

### Key Accomplishments

#### Economic Policy
- Supported economic growth through sound fiscal policy
- Advanced international economic cooperation through G-7 and G-20 engagement
- Strengthened the U.S. position in the global economy

#### Financial Stability
- Worked with the Financial Stability Oversight Council to monitor systemic risks
- Strengthened oversight of financial institutions
- Enhanced financial market resilience

#### Tax Policy
- Administered tax policy to support American families and businesses
- Continued implementation of tax reform initiatives
- Improved tax administration efficiency

#### Combating Illicit Finance
- Implemented targeted sanctions to protect national security
- Strengthened anti-money laundering frameworks
- Disrupted terrorist financing networks

#### Debt Management
- Maintained the lowest cost of borrowing consistent with prudent risk management
- Successfully conducted Treasury auctions
- Managed the federal government's debt portfolio

### Looking Forward

Treasury remains committed to:
- Promoting inclusive economic growth
- Strengthening the financial system
- Protecting taxpayers and consumers
- Advancing U.S. interests internationally
