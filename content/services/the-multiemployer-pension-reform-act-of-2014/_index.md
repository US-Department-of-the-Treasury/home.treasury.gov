The Multiemployer Pension Reform Act of 2014 (MPRA) amended the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA) and the Internal Revenue Code to address the funding challenges facing multiemployer defined benefit pension plans.

## Applications for Benefit Suspension

Under MPRA, certain multiemployer pension plans in critical and declining status may apply to Treasury to suspend benefits.

- [Applications for Benefit Suspension](/services/the-multiemployer-pension-reform-act-of-2014/applications-for-benefit-suspension)

## Special Financial Assistance Program

The American Rescue Plan Act of 2021 established a Special Financial Assistance Program for eligible multiemployer pension plans.

- [Special Financial Assistance Program](https://www.pbgc.gov/sfa)
