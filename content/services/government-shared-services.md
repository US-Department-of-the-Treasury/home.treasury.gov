Treasury provides shared services to federal agencies, helping to improve efficiency and reduce costs across government.

## Administrative Resource Center (ARC)

The Bureau of the Fiscal Service's Administrative Resource Center provides:

- Financial management services
- Human resources services
- Travel services
- Acquisition services

## Financial Management Services

The Bureau of the Fiscal Service offers:

- Payment services
- Collections services
- Debt management
- Accounting services

## Benefits to Agencies

- **Cost Savings** - Economies of scale reduce per-transaction costs
- **Expertise** - Access to specialized staff and systems
- **Compliance** - Services designed to meet federal requirements
- **Innovation** - Continuous improvement and modernization
