Contact TIC & Using TIC

- To view or print the ".zip" content on this page, download one of the many available[data compression utilities](http://directory.google.com/Top/Computers/Software/Shareware/Windows/Utilities/Data_Compression/).
- To view or print the PDF content on this page or contained in the ".zip" files, download the free[Adobe Acrobat Reader](http://www.adobe.com/products/acrobat/readstep.html).

Below are archives of Treasury International Capital (TIC) data that were released monthly on Treasury's TIC website. The archived data cover: long-term securities reported monthly by U.S. custodians and brokers/dealers of securities (aggregate holdings by U.S. and foreign residents, sales and purchases by U.S. residents to residents of foreign countries, and fair value changes); claims and liabilities to foreign-residents reported monthly/quarterly by U.S. banks and brokers/dealers of securities; claims and liabilities to foreign-residents reported quarterly by U.S. nonbanks; and holdings and settlements of derivatives contracts with foreign-residents reported quarterly by U.S. businesses with large holdings of derivatives contracts.

[Descriptions of the archived data files](https://treasury.gov/resource-center/data-chart-center/tic/Documents/arcdatadesc.txt), also in[PDF](https://treasury.gov/resource-center/data-chart-center/tic/Documents/arcdatadesc.pdf). Each archive is a ".zip" file containing the TIC data released on the date shown in the filename. In each zip file since the TIC data release on 4/17/2023, the data files include five-to-seven ".html" files (one was a ".csv" file until the release on 09/18/2023), and numerous space-delimited or tab-delimited ".txt" files that you can view in word processors and spreadsheets. In each zip file before the release on 4/17/2023, all data files except two were usually space-delimited or tab-delimited ".txt" files; one exception was a ".csv" file, and the other was a ".pdf" file. The descriptions of the data files will help you more easily find and extract (from the .zip file) the data file you want to view. The most recent[list of country codes-country names](https://home.treasury.gov/data/treasury-international-capital-tic-system-home-page/help-files/country-names-and-country-codes-reference-lists)covers data released on 04/15/2015 and later.More details about the archived data can be found below.
