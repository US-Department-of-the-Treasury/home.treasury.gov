## Secretaries of the Treasury

Since its establishment in 1789, the Department of the Treasury has had over 75 Secretaries.

### Notable Secretaries

- **Alexander Hamilton (1789-1795)** - First Secretary, established the nation's financial system
- **Albert Gallatin (1801-1814)** - Longest-serving Secretary, reduced national debt
- **Salmon P. Chase (1861-1864)** - Financed the Civil War, created national currency
- **Andrew Mellon (1921-1932)** - Served under three presidents
- **Henry Morgenthau Jr. (1934-1945)** - Longest-serving since Gallatin, WWII financing

## Recent Secretaries

The Department continues to be led by distinguished public servants committed to economic prosperity and financial security.
