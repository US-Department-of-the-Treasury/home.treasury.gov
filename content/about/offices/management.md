## Office of Management

The Office of Management provides administrative leadership and support services for the U.S. Department of the Treasury, ensuring efficient and effective operations across the department.

### Mission

To provide exceptional management support that enables Treasury to accomplish its mission while maintaining the highest standards of stewardship.

### Key Functions

#### Human Capital
- Workforce planning and recruitment
- Employee development and training
- Performance management
- Labor relations

#### Information Technology
- IT infrastructure and systems
- Cybersecurity
- Digital services
- Technology modernization

#### Facilities and Operations
- Building management and security
- Administrative services
- Environmental programs
- Emergency preparedness

#### Budget and Finance
- Budget formulation and execution
- Financial management
- Grants management
- Internal controls

#### Procurement
- Acquisition services
- Contract management
- Vendor relations
- Small business programs

### Strategic Initiatives

The Office of Management leads several key initiatives:

- **Digital Transformation** - Modernizing IT systems and services
- **Workforce Development** - Building a skilled and diverse workforce
- **Operational Excellence** - Improving efficiency and effectiveness
- **Sustainability** - Reducing environmental impact

### Leadership

The Assistant Secretary for Management leads the Office of Management and reports directly to the Deputy Secretary of the Treasury.
