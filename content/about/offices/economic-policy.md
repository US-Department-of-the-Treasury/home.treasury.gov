The Office of Economic Policy assists policymakers by analyzing and reporting on current and prospective economic developments in the domestic and world economies.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Economic Analysis** - Analyzing domestic and international economic conditions
- **Policy Development** - Developing and evaluating economic policy recommendations
- **Forecasting** - Preparing economic forecasts and assessments
- **Research** - Conducting economic research on policy-relevant topics

## Publications

The Office produces various economic reports and analyses that inform Treasury policy decisions.
