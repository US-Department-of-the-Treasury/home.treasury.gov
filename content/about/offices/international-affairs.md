The Office of International Affairs advises and assists the Secretary and other senior officials on international monetary, financial, commercial, energy, and trade policies and programs.

## Key Responsibilities

- **International Financial Policy** - Developing policy on international financial institutions
- **Trade Policy** - Supporting trade policy development and enforcement
- **Technical Assistance** - Providing financial sector technical assistance to developing nations
- **Sanctions Policy** - Coordinating international sanctions implementation

## International Engagement

The Office represents Treasury at international forums including:

- G-7 and G-20
- International Monetary Fund (IMF)
- World Bank
- Financial Stability Board
