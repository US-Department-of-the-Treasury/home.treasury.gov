The Departmental Offices are responsible for policy formulation, overall management, and the general direction of the Department. These offices work directly under the Secretary and Deputy Secretary to develop and coordinate Treasury policy.

## Policy Offices

### [Domestic Finance](/about/offices/domestic-finance)
Advises on financial services policy, federal debt management, fiscal policy, and financial regulation. Oversees relationships with financial regulators and the Bureau of the Fiscal Service.

### [Economic Policy](/about/offices/economic-policy)
Provides economic analysis and policy advice on domestic and international economic issues, including forecasting and research to support Treasury decision-making.

### [International Affairs](/about/offices/international-affairs)
Leads Treasury's engagement with international financial institutions, foreign governments, and multilateral forums on international economic and financial issues.

### [Tax Policy](/policy-issues/tax-policy)
Develops tax policy recommendations, drafts tax legislation, and provides guidance on tax regulations in coordination with the IRS.

### [Terrorism and Financial Intelligence](/about/offices/terrorism-and-financial-intelligence)
Combats illicit finance through economic sanctions, anti-money laundering efforts, and intelligence activities to protect national security.

## Management and Support Offices

### Office of Management
Provides administrative and management services including human resources, procurement, facilities, and budget.

### Office of the General Counsel
Provides legal advice and representation to Treasury leadership and coordinates legal policy across the Department.

### Office of Legislative Affairs
Manages Treasury's relationships with Congress and coordinates legislative strategy.

### Office of Public Affairs
Handles media relations, public communications, and speechwriting for Treasury officials.

## Operating Bureaus

Treasury's operating bureaus carry out specific government operations. See the [Organizational Chart](/about/general-information/organizational-chart) for a complete list of Treasury bureaus.
