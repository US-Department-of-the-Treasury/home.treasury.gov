All positions at the U.S. Department of the Treasury are posted on [USAJOBS](https://www.usajobs.gov/), the federal government's official employment website.

### Step-by-Step Application Process

#### 1. Create a USAJOBS Account
- Visit [USAJOBS.gov](https://www.usajobs.gov/)
- Create a login.gov account
- Complete your USAJOBS profile
- Build your resume

#### 2. Search for Treasury Positions
- Use keywords like "Treasury" or specific job titles
- Filter by location, salary, and job series
- Set up job alerts for Treasury positions
- Save interesting job announcements

#### 3. Review Job Announcements Carefully
- Read the entire announcement
- Note the closing date
- Review qualification requirements
- Understand the application process

#### 4. Prepare Required Documents
- Federal resume (more detailed than private sector)
- Transcripts (if education is required)
- Veterans' preference documentation
- Performance evaluations (if available)

#### 5. Answer Assessment Questions
- Complete all questionnaires honestly
- Provide specific examples of experience
- Relate responses to job requirements

#### 6. Submit Your Application
- Apply before the closing date
- Confirm your application was received
- Save a copy of your application

### Tips for Success

**Federal Resume Tips**
- Include specific dates of employment (month/year)
- List hours worked per week
- Describe duties and accomplishments in detail
- Include relevant keywords from the job announcement
- Keep resume length appropriate for experience level

**Understanding Status**
- **Referred** - Your application was sent to the hiring manager
- **Not Referred** - You did not meet minimum qualifications
- **Selected** - You were chosen for the position
- **Not Selected** - Another candidate was chosen

### Special Hiring Authorities

- **Veterans' Preference** - Veterans receive additional consideration
- **Schedule A** - Hiring authority for people with disabilities
- **Pathways** - Programs for students and recent graduates
- **Reinstatement** - Former federal employees

### Contact Information

For questions about specific job announcements, contact the Human Resources office listed in the job posting.
