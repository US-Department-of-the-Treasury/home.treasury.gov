## Benefits and Career Growth at Treasury

The U.S. Department of the Treasury offers a comprehensive benefits package and numerous opportunities for professional development and career advancement.

### Federal Employee Benefits

#### Health Insurance
- Federal Employees Health Benefits (FEHB) Program
- Multiple plan options from various carriers
- Coverage for employees and eligible family members
- Dental and vision insurance options

#### Retirement
- Federal Employees Retirement System (FERS)
- Thrift Savings Plan (TSP) with matching contributions
- Social Security benefits
- FERS annuity

#### Leave
- Annual leave (vacation) - 13-26 days based on service
- Sick leave - 13 days per year
- Paid federal holidays - 11 days per year
- Family and medical leave

#### Life Insurance
- Federal Employees' Group Life Insurance (FEGLI)
- Basic coverage at no cost
- Optional additional coverage available

### Work-Life Balance

- **Flexible Work Schedules** - Alternate work schedules and compressed work weeks
- **Telework** - Remote work opportunities where applicable
- **Transit Benefits** - Subsidized public transportation
- **Fitness Programs** - On-site fitness facilities at many locations
- **Employee Assistance Program (EAP)** - Counseling and support services

### Career Development

#### Training and Education
- Professional development courses
- Leadership development programs
- Tuition assistance for continuing education
- Certifications and specialized training

#### Career Advancement
- Merit-based promotions
- Career ladder positions
- Rotational assignments
- Special project opportunities

#### Mentoring
- Formal mentoring programs
- Coaching and development support
- Networking opportunities
- Knowledge sharing initiatives

### Competitive Salaries

Treasury offers competitive federal salaries based on:
- General Schedule (GS) pay system
- Locality pay adjustments
- Performance-based awards
- Special salary rates for certain positions
