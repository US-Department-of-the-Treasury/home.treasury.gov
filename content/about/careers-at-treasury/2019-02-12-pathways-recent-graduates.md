### Pathways Recent Graduates Program

The Recent Graduates Program was developed for candidates who have completed a degree in the past two years. Recent Graduates may have a two-year/vocational degree up through Masters/Ph.D. Treasury recognizes the importance of continued development for those with new careers in the government. Because of this there are strict guidelines for successfully completing the program.

### Development

All Recent Graduates participants must complete the following requirements in order to be considered for conversion to a permanent position:

- Maintain a mentor,
- Complete 40 hours of training,
- Develop a Individual Development Plan (IDP).
