Treasury has operating bureaus that carry out specific operations. These bureaus make up 98% of the Treasury workforce.

## Bureaus

- [Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB)](https://www.ttb.gov/) - Collects federal excise taxes on alcohol, tobacco, firearms, and ammunition.

- [Bureau of Engraving and Printing (BEP)](https://www.bep.gov/) - Designs, produces, and supplies Federal Reserve notes and other government securities.

- [Bureau of the Fiscal Service](https://www.fiscal.treasury.gov/) - Promotes the financial integrity and operational efficiency of the federal government.

- [Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN)](https://www.fincen.gov/) - Safeguards the financial system from illicit use.

- [Internal Revenue Service (IRS)](https://www.irs.gov/) - Collects taxes and enforces tax laws.

- [Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC)](https://www.occ.gov/) - Charters, regulates, and supervises national banks.

- [U.S. Mint](https://www.usmint.gov/) - Produces circulating coins and commemorative coins.

## Inspectors General

- [Office of Inspector General (OIG)](https://oig.treasury.gov/) - Conducts independent audits, investigations, and reviews.

- [Treasury Inspector General for Tax Administration (TIGTA)](https://www.tigta.gov/) - Provides oversight of IRS activities.

- [Special Inspector General for Pandemic Recovery (SIGPR)](https://www.sigpr.gov/) - Conducts audits and investigations of pandemic relief programs.
