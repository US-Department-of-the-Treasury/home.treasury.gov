## Department Structure

The U.S. Department of the Treasury is organized into departmental offices and operating bureaus.

### Departmental Offices

The Departmental Offices are primarily responsible for the formulation of policy and management of the Department as a whole.

### Operating Bureaus

The operating bureaus carry out the specific operations assigned to the Department:

- **Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB)**
- **Bureau of Engraving and Printing (BEP)**
- **Bureau of the Fiscal Service**
- **Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN)**
- **Internal Revenue Service (IRS)**
- **Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC)**
- **U.S. Mint**

### Inspectors General

- Office of Inspector General (OIG)
- Treasury Inspector General for Tax Administration (TIGTA)
- Special Inspector General for Pandemic Recovery (SIGPR)
