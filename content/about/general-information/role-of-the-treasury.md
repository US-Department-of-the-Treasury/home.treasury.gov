## Mission

The U.S. Department of the Treasury's mission is to:

- Maintain a strong economy and create economic and job opportunities by promoting the conditions that enable economic growth and stability at home and abroad
- Strengthen national security by combating threats and protecting the integrity of the financial system
- Manage the U.S. Government's finances and resources effectively

## Core Functions

Treasury's core functions include:

1. **Managing Federal Finances** - Collecting revenue and making payments, managing federal finances, and operating the government's accounts
2. **Financial Infrastructure** - Producing currency and coinage, managing U.S. accounts, and acting as the government's financial agent
3. **Economic Policy** - Advising the President on domestic and international economic policy
4. **Tax Policy** - Enforcing federal finance and tax laws
5. **National Security** - Protecting the nation's financial systems and combating financial crimes

## Leadership

The Department is administered by the Secretary of the Treasury, who is a member of the President's Cabinet.
