## Secretary of the Treasury

The Secretary of the Treasury is the head of the U.S. Department of the Treasury which is concerned with financial and monetary matters, and, until 2003, also with some issues of national security and defense.

## Deputy Secretary

The Deputy Secretary of the Treasury is the second-ranking official at the Department of the Treasury.

## Under Secretaries

The Department has four Under Secretaries:

- **Under Secretary for Domestic Finance** - Oversees activities related to domestic finance, financial institutions, and fiscal affairs
- **Under Secretary for International Affairs** - Advises the Secretary on international economic and financial issues
- **Under Secretary for Terrorism and Financial Intelligence** - Leads the Department's efforts to combat financial crime
- **Under Secretary for Management** - Responsible for managing the Department's internal operations

## Assistant Secretaries

Assistant Secretaries lead specific policy and management areas within the Department.
