## Inspector General Audits and Investigative Reports

The Treasury Department is overseen by multiple Inspectors General who conduct independent audits, evaluations, and investigations to promote efficiency and prevent fraud, waste, and abuse.

### Treasury Inspectors General

#### Office of Inspector General (OIG)
The Treasury OIG provides oversight of:
- Departmental Offices operations
- Bureau programs and activities (except IRS)
- Government-wide programs administered by Treasury

**[Visit Treasury OIG](https://oig.treasury.gov/)**

#### Treasury Inspector General for Tax Administration (TIGTA)
TIGTA provides oversight of:
- IRS programs and operations
- Tax administration activities
- IRS employee conduct

**[Visit TIGTA](https://www.tigta.gov/)**

#### Special Inspector General for Pandemic Recovery (SIGPR)
SIGPR provides oversight of:
- Treasury pandemic relief programs
- CARES Act implementation
- Economic stabilization programs

**[Visit SIGPR](https://www.sigpr.gov/)**

### Types of Reports

#### Audit Reports
- Financial statement audits
- Performance audits
- Compliance audits
- Information technology audits

#### Evaluation Reports
- Program evaluations
- Efficiency reviews
- Management advisories

#### Investigative Reports
- Criminal investigations
- Civil investigations
- Administrative misconduct

#### Semiannual Reports to Congress
Each IG provides semiannual reports summarizing:
- Audit and investigation activities
- Significant findings
- Recommendations
- Recoveries and savings

### How to Access Reports

Reports are available on each Inspector General's website:
- [OIG Reports](https://oig.treasury.gov/)
- [TIGTA Reports](https://www.tigta.gov/)
- [SIGPR Reports](https://www.sigpr.gov/)

### Reporting Fraud, Waste, and Abuse

If you suspect fraud, waste, or abuse in Treasury programs:
- [Report to Treasury OIG](https://oig.treasury.gov/report-fraud-waste-and-abuse)
- [Report to TIGTA](https://www.tigta.gov/report-fraud-waste-abuse)
